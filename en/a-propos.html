Our story

FROM FRUSTRATION TO REVOLUTION

Pirabel Labs was founded by entrepreneurs who experienced firsthand the frustration of working with traditional digital marketing agencies. After years of watching businesses pay premium prices for mediocre results, fragmented services and opaque reporting, we decided to build the agency we wished had existed when we were on the client side.

Our founding principle is radical: every euro a client invests in digital marketing must generate measurable, attributable revenue growth. Not vanity metrics. Not airy strategy decks. Real numbers tied to real business outcomes — qualified leads booked, deals closed, recurring revenue compounded, customer acquisition costs reduced and lifetime value maximized through compounding organic growth.

From day one we have served founders, marketing directors and CEOs who refuse to accept marketing investment as a black box. Our clients demand transparency, accountability and craftsmanship — the same standards we hold ourselves to in every audit, every campaign, every line of code, every keyword strategy and every conversion optimization sprint we deliver.

"We don't just want to be your digital agency. We want to be the partner who transforms your relationship with growth — making it predictable, scalable and built on data instead of luck."

Pirabel Labs · Founding Manifesto

Where we operate

A GLOBAL FOOTPRINT WITH LOCAL ROOTS

Pirabel Labs serves ambitious brands across three continents from strategic operational hubs. Each market we cover combines deep local expertise with global best practices in digital marketing, search engine optimization, conversion-focused web design and intelligent automation.

Europe

Paris remains our European strategic hub serving French and pan-European brands. We extend operational coverage to Marseille for southern France market intelligence, Lyon for Auvergne-Rhone-Alpes region coverage and Brussels for Belgian and EU institutional clients. Our European operations master GDPR-compliant tracking, multilingual SEO across French, English and Dutch, regional consumer behavior, French and Belgian search engine result page dynamics and EU advertising regulations including digital services act compliance and consumer protection requirements that affect ad creative, claims substantiation and audience targeting strategy.

North & West Africa

Casablanca anchors our North African presence with deep Maghreb market expertise extending into Tunisia. Our West African footprint covers Senegal through Dakar, Ivory Coast through Abidjan, Benin through Cotonou, Cameroon through Douala and Yaounde, Democratic Republic of Congo through Kinshasa, Gabon through Libreville and Togo through Lome. We pioneer mobile-first digital strategies, master local payment integration challenges, navigate emerging digital advertising platforms and respect cultural authenticity across diverse French-speaking African markets where digital adoption accelerates rapidly across every consumer category and B2B vertical.

North America

Montreal serves as our North American operational base, supporting Canadian businesses and international brands targeting the lucrative North American consumer and B2B markets. Quebec presence brings genuine bilingual French-English digital marketing capability. Our team understands North American search behavior nuances, advertising platform peculiarities, content cultural calibration between Canadian and US audiences and the regulatory landscape including CASL email compliance, accessibility standards, sales tax implications and cross-border ecommerce considerations that affect digital strategies for brands operating in or targeting North American markets.
