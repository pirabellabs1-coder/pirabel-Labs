Frequently Asked Questions

TOUT SAVOIR SUR LE DEVELOPPEMENT SUR MESURE

When faut-il opter for a développement custom? expand_more
Le développement custom is la bonne solution when the CMS classiques (WordPress, Webflow, Shopify) ne suffisent more a couvrir your needs. The signaux the most courants : you have need features metier spécifiques qu'no plugin ne couvre, you devez connecter several systèmes between eux via API, you gérez volumes de données importants, or you construisez un produit SaaS. Platforms de reservation, portails clients, tableaux de bord analytiques, marketplaces, applications metier — si your project ne rentre not in the cases of a CMS, le custom is la response.
Quelles technologies usesz-you? expand_more
We use un stack technologique moderne and éprouvé. For le front-end : React and Next.js with TypeScript for interfaces rapides, reactives and maintenables. For le back-end : Node.js (Express or NestJS) or Python (FastAPI, Django) according to the needs. For the bases de données : PostgreSQL for the données relationnelles, MongoDB for the données non-structureds, Redis for the cache. Deploiement on Vercel, AWS or serveurs dedicateds according to the exigences de performance and souverainete données. Le choix technologique is always justifie par the needs concrets de your project.
How much does it cost un projand développement custom? expand_more
The projects custom start a 8 000 euros for a application web simple (portail, tableau de bord). The platforms de complexity moyenne (SaaS basique, client portal avance) isnt between 15 000 and 30 000 euros. The projects ambitieux (marketplace, SaaS multi-tenant, platform metier complexe) will de 30 000 a 50 000 euros and plus. Every project is unique and le budgandpend features, integrations and the complexity technique. We chiffrons after un brief détaillé and you receive un quote transparent with a planning precise.
How long does it take un développement custom? expand_more
Un projet custom prend généralement between 8 and 16 weeks de la specification a la mise in production. The projects more simples (portail client, tableau de bord) peuvent be livres en 6 a 8 weeks. The platforms complexes (SaaS, marketplace) nécessitent 12 a 20 weeks. We work en sprints agiles de 2 weeks with livraisons incrementales : you testez the features au fur and a mesure and pouvez ajuster the priorites to each sprint.
Does je suis proprietaire du code? expand_more
Oui, a 100% and without restriction. A la livraison du projet, you receive l'integralite du code source heberge on your propre repository Git (GitHub or GitLab). You receive also la documentation technique complete, la documentation API, the acces a all the services (hosting, base de donnees, monitoring). Le code you appartient fully. You pouvez le faire evoluer with we, with a équipe interne or with a autre provider. Aucun lock-in, no dependance.
Do you offer la maintenance and l'evolution du projet? expand_more
Oui. We offer forfaits de maintenance and d'evolution mensuels adaptés a every project. Le forfait maintenance inclut the corrections de bugs, the mises up to date de sécurité dependances, le monitoring de performance and disponibilite, and the backups. Le volet evolution inclut un volume d'hours mensuel dedicated au développement de nouvelles features. Your application grandit with your business and s'adapte à your nouveaux needs. The forfaits start a 500 euros per month according to the complexity du projet.
