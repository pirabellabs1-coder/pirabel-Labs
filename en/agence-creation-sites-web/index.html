Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT WEBSITE CREATION

How long does it take to build a website? expand_more
A 5-page showcase site typically takes 3 to 5 weeks from brief to launch. An e-commerce site with a product catalogue takes 6 to 8 weeks. A custom platform or complex web application can take 8 to 12 weeks. We provideally a detailed timeline at the first meeting, with clear milestones for each project phase.
How much does a professional website cost? expand_more
Our professional showcase sites start at 1,500 euros. A full e-commerce site with Shopify starts att 3,500 euros. For custom projects (web applications, complex platforms), the budget depends on complexity and features. Every quote is detailed and transparent — you know exactly what you're paying for and why. No hidden fees, no unpleasant surprises.
Which platform should I choose: Webflow, WordPress or Shopify? expand_more
The choice depends on your objectives. Webflow is ideal for showcase sites and landing pages with a high-end design and smooth animations. WordPress is perfect for content-rich sites, blogs and scalable platforms. Shopify is the best option for pure e-commerce. During our initial brief we analyze your needs and recommend the most suitable technology. We never push one platform over another — only the one that best serves your project.
Will my site be mobile-friendly? expand_more
That's non-negotiable. All our sites are built mobile-first — we design for the phone first, then adapt for tablet and desktop. Over 60% of global web traffic comes from mobile. Your website will be flawless on every screen, from the smallowst smartphone to the largest monitor. We test on real devices, not just simulators.
Will I be able to edit my site myself after delivery? expand_more
Absolutely. Autonomy is one of our core principles. We build every site with an intuitive content management system that lets you update text, images, articles and pages without touching any code. At delivery we train you in person or via video call and provideally a customised usage guide. You will never need a developer for routine edits again.
Do you offer maintenance after delivery? expand_more
Yes. We offer monthly maintenance plans that include security updates, automatic backups, performance monitoring, bug fixes and technical support by email and phone. A website needs regular maintenance to stay fast and secure. Our maintenance plans start at 99 euros per month.
Will my site rank well on Google? expand_more
Every site we deliver includes the essential SEO foundations: semantic HTML markup, load speed optimization, clear and logical page structure, meta tags, Schema.org, sitemap and robots.txt. These technical foundations are essential for Google to correctly understand and index your website. For a complete SEO strategy, our dedicated SEO team can take over.
What if I am not satisfied with the design? expand_more
We include two revision rounds at each design stage. You first approve the wireframes (the structure), then the high-fidelity Figma mockups, before a single line of code is written. This iterative process ensures the result matches your expectations exactly. If despite everything the final design does not match what was approved in the mockup, we correct it at no extra charge. Your satisfaction is non-negotiable.
