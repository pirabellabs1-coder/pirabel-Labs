The problem

WHY YOUR ONLINE STORE IS NOT SELLING

You have visitors, but sales don't follow. Here are the most common reasons.

remove_shopping_cart

Your cart abandonment rate is massive

On average, 70% of carts are abandoned. If your checkout funnel is complicated, slow or doesn't inspire trust, that number explodes. Every extra step in checkout is an opportunity for the customer to leave. We design minimalist, fluid sales funnels that drastically reduce abandonment and maximize conversions.

image_not_supported

Your fiches produits ne do not make them want

Mediocre photos, short descriptions, no customer reviews, no product staging. Your visitors can't find the information they need to make a purchase decision. We create structured product pages that answer every objection and trigger the desire to buy.

phone_android

The mobile experience is disastrous

More than 70% of e-commerce traffic comes from mobile. If your store is hard to navigate on a phone — buttons too small, images overflowing, forms impossible to fill — you lose the majority of your potential sales. We design every page mobile-first for a smooth shopping experience on all screens.

money_off

You are not capitalizing on your existing customers

Acquiring a new customer costs 5 to 7 times more than retaining an existing one. If you have no upsell, cross-sell, post-purchase email or loyalty program strategy, you're leaving money on the table. We integrate these mechanisms directly into your Shopify store to increase your average order value and customer lifetime value.

Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT OUR SHOPIFY SERVICES

Why choose Shopify for my e-commerce store? expand_more
Shopify is the world's number-one e-commerce platform, used by millions of merchants in more than 175 countries. Its main advantage is that it handles all the technical side for you: hosting, SSL security, updates, PCI compliance for payments. You focus on selling and your business, not on technical maintenance. Its ecosystem of more than 8,000 apps lets you add features without complex development. And unlike WooCommerce, you never have to manage updates that break your store.
How much does a Shopify store cost with Pirabel Labs? expand_more
A custom Shopify store starts at 3,500 euros. This price includes the Figma design, custom Liquid theme development, product catalogue configuration, optimized sales funnel, payment and logistics integrations, e-commerce SEO and training on managing your store. For more complex projects with advanced integrations (ERP, marketplace, multi-currency), the budget is quoted on request. Add to this the monthly Shopify subscription (starting from 36 euros per month).
How long does it take to create a Shopify store? expand_more
A standard Shopify store with a product catalogue is delivered in 4 to 6 weeks. This timeline includes e-commerce strategy, UX design, custom theme development, catalogue configuration, integrations, full purchase funnel testing and training. Complex projects with ERP integrations, multi-currency or specific features can take 6 to 10 weeks.
Can you migrate my store to Shopify? expand_more
Yes, we regularly carry out migrations from WooCommerce, PrestaShop, Magento and other platforms. We transfer your products (with images, descriptions, variants), your customers and order history. We take care to preserve your existing SEO by setting up the necessary 301 redirects. The migration is planned to minimize disruption to your sales. You lose neither data nor SEO.
Do you use a pre-made or custom Shopify theme? expand_more
We develop a custom Shopify theme for every client, coded in Liquid (Shopify's native templating language). A custom theme guarantees a unique design that reflects your brand, optimal performance without unnecessary code, and a shopping experience perfectly adapted to your products. Themes bought on the Shopify Theme Store are a good starting point for small budgets, but they impose compromises on design and conversion.
Does Shopify handle payments and logistics? expand_more
Yes, Shopify natively integrates the main payment gateways: Shopify Payments (powered by Stripe), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and many more. PCI DSS compliance is managed automatically by Shopify — your customers pay with complete security. For logistics, we configure shipping zones, rates by weight or price, parcel tracking and integrations with your logistics providers. Confirmation and tracking emails are sent automatically.
