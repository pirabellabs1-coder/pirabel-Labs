The problem

WHY YOUR CURRENT SITE IS HOLDING YOU BACK

Most websites suffer from the same problems. Webflow solves each of them.

lock

You are hostage to your developer

Changing a text, adding a page, updating an image — every time you have to email your developer, wait for their availability and pay the bill. With a classically hand-coded site you have no autonomy. Webflow radically changes this dynamic: its visual CMS lets you change everything yourself, without touching code, in a few clicks.

timer_off

Your website takes months to be delivered

Traditional development is slow. Between design, integration, back-and-forth and bugs, a simple showcase site can take 3 to 6 months. With Webflow, we design and build in the same tool. The result: a site delivered in 2 to 4 weeks, without sacrificing quality. Every day saved is revenue you don't lose.

brush

Templates are killing your brand image

You used a WordPress or Wix template and your website looks like thousands of others. Your visitors can feel it — the lack of originality undermines your credibility. With Webflow, we create every element from scratch. Your website is unique, a true reflection of your brand, with a custom design that genuinely sets you apart from competitors.

animation

Your animations are non-existent or tacky

A static site with no interactions feels like a dead page. Conversely, poorly executed animations slow the site and annoy visitors. Webflow offers a native animation engine that enables smooth transitions, elegant micro-interactions and cinematic scroll effects — without impacting performance.

Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT OUR WEBFLOW SERVICES

What is Webflow and why choose it? expand_more
Webflow is a no-code website creation platform that combines a visual design tool with a powerful CMS and integrated hosting. Unlike classic page builders, Webflow generates clean, high-performance HTML, CSS and JavaScript code. It gives you total control over every design element without writing a single line of code. We recommend it for showcase sites, landing pages, business websites and small e-commerce stores that need a high-end design and smooth animations.
How much does a Webflow site cost with Pirabel Labs? expand_more
A 5-page Webflow showcase site starts att 1,500 euros. A multi-page site with CMS, blog and advanced animations ranges between 3,000 and 6,000 euros depending on complexity. This price includes Figma design, Webflow development, CMS configuration, SEO optimization, testing and training. No hidden fees. You receive a detailed quote before committing.
How long does it take to create a Webflow site? expand_more
A standard showcase site is delivered in 2 to 4 weeks from brief to launch. That is significantly faster than traditional development thanks to no-code. A more complex site with many pages and advanced interactions can take 4 to 6 weeks. We provide a precise timeline at the initial brief with clear milestones for every step.
Can I edit my Webflow site myself after delivery? expand_more
Yes, that is one of Webflow's main advantages. The visual CMS lets you edit text, images and content directly from an intuitive interface — no technical skills required. We configure the CMS with clear editable fields and train you on how to use it. Within one hour of training you are autonomous for all routine changes.
Is Webflow good for SEO? expand_more
Webflow generates clean, semantic HTML code, which is an excellent starting point for SEO. The platform offers total control over title tags, meta descriptions, URLs, image alt attributes and heading structure. The integrated CDN hosting guarantees very fast load times. Combined with our SEO expertise, your Webflow website will be perfectly optimised for search engines.
Can Webflow handle an e-commerce store? expand_more
Webflow offers a native e-commerce module suited for stores with a limited catalogue — generally fewer than 50 products. It handles payments via Stripe, product pages and the purchase funnel. For an e-commerce store with a large catalogue, advanced inventory management or complex logistics needs, we recommend Shopify instead. We analyze your needs and guide you towards the best solution.
