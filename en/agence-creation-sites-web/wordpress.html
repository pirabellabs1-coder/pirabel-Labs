THE WORDPRESS TRAPS YOU KNOW ALL TOO WELL

Most WordPress sites are poorly built. If you recognize yourself here, it is time to change your approach.

speed

Your website is un empilage de plugins

Vingt, trente, sometimes cinquante plugins empiles the uns on the autres. Chacun ajoute du poids, conflits potentiels and flaws de security. Your website rame, the mises up to date cassent features, and you don't have more quel plugin fait quoi. We build with a minimum de plugins soigneusement sélectionnes and du code custom for the rest.

security

La sécurité is a passoire

WordPress is la cible numéro un pirates justement because it is le CMS the most uses. Un theme achete on un marketplace, plugins obsoletes, no pare-feu — your website is a invitation aux hackers. We secure every layer : core, theme, plugins, serveur, backups. Your tranquillite d'esprit is non-negotiable.

Your theme is un template achete online

The themes ThemeForest are séduisants en demo but catastrophiques in production. Code surchargé, options inutiles par thousands, impossible a personnaliser proprement. Your website ressemble a hundreds d'autres and it is lent. Un theme custom is more leger, more rapideally, and 100% suited to your needs. It's a investment, not a dépense.

A each mise up to date de WordPress, de your theme or of a plugin, you retenez your breath. Does that will casser something? When un site is construit with du code propre and plugins maintenus, the mises up to date se passent without issue. Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING ABOUT OUR WORDPRESS SERVICES

Why choose WordPress over another platform? expand_more
WordPress alimente more than 40% sites web in the world. Il offre a flexibilite inegalee thanks to son ecosysteme de plugins and themes. Son architecture is designed for the SEO, with URLs propres, a structure sémantique and a excellente management du contenu. Il is particulierement adapté aux sites riches en contenu (blogs, magazines, sites institutionnels), aux projects multilingues and aux businesss that have need faire evoluer their site over time. Combine with WooCommerce, il devient also a solution e-commerce powerfule.
How much does a professional WordPress site cost? expand_more
Un showcase site WordPress custom with theme custom starts at 2 000 euros for 5 a 7 pages. Un site with blog, features avancées and integrations is between 3 500 and 6 000 euros. Un site e-commerce WooCommerce complandmarre a 4 000 euros. The projects more ambitieux (portail, member area, multilingue) are chiffres on quote. Each euro is justifie and le quote is transparent — no frais caches.
Do you use pre-made themes or custom code? expand_more
We develop exclusivement themes WordPress custom. Pas de themes ThemeForest, not d'Elementor, no Divi, no constructeurs de pages lourds. Why? Un theme achete contient thousands de lignes de code inutiles that ralentissent your website and creent flaws de security. Our theme custom contient only le code nécessaire à your website. Le result : un site more rapideally, more sécurisé, more facile a maintenir and 100% suited to your needs.
Will my WordPress site be well secured? expand_more
La sécurité is at the heart of every project WordPress that we deliver. We apply un ensemble de mesures rigoureuses : mise up to date réguliere du core WordPress and plugins, pare-feu applicatif (WAF), protection contre the attaques brute force and the injections SQL, desactivation de l'editeur de code in production, backups automatiques quotidiennes on serveur externe, and certificat SSL. With our packages maintenance, your website is surveille continuously.
Can you create a WooCommerce store? expand_more
Oui, WooCommerce is le plugin e-commerce the most uses au monde and we master every aspect. We configurons the fiches produits optimised for the conversion, the passerelles de paiement (Stripe, PayPal, etc.), la management stocks, the frais de livraison, the codes promo, the upsells and cross-sells. Le theme is développé custom for mettre en valeur your products and guideallyr l'acheteur towards la finalisation de la commande.
Do you offer WordPress maintenance? expand_more
Oui, and we recommend it vivement. Un site WordPress non maintenu is a faille de sécurité that attend d'be exploitee. Our forfaits maintenance mensuels incluent the mises up to date testees du core, du theme and plugins, the backups quotidiennes automatiques, le monitoring de sécurité 24/7, le monitoring de performance and le support technique par email and telephone. The forfaits start a 99 euros per month. It's a investment that protege your website and your tranquillite d'esprit.
