Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING ABOUT OUR WORDPRESS SERVICES

Why choose WordPress over another platform? expand_more
WordPress powers more than 40% of websites worldwide. It offers unmatched flexibility thanks to its ecosystem of plugins and themes. Its architecture is designed for SEO, with clean URLs, semantic structure and excellent content management. It is particularly suited to content-rich sites (blogs, magazines, institutional sites), multilingual projects and businesses that need to evolve their site over time. Combined with WooCommerce, it also becomes a powerful e-commerce solution.
How much does a professional WordPress site cost? expand_more
A custom WordPress showcase site with a custom theme starts at 2,000 euros for 5 to 7 pages. A site with a blog, advanced features and integrations ranges between 3,500 and 6,000 euros. A WooCommerce e-commerce site starts at 4,000 euros. More ambitious projects (portal, member area, multilingual) are quoted on request. Every euro is justified and the quote is transparent — no hidden fees.
Do you use pre-made themes or custom code? expand_more
We exclusively develop custom WordPress themes. No ThemeForest themes, no Elementor, no Divi, no heavy page builders. Why? A purchased theme contains thousands of unnecessary lines of code that slow down your website and create security vulnerabilities. Our custom theme contains only the code necessary for your website. The result: a faster, more secure, easier-to-maintain site that is 100% suited to your needs.
Will my WordPress site be well secured? expand_more
Security is at the heart of every WordPress project we deliver. We apply a rigorous set of measures: regular updates of the WordPress core and plugins, web application firewall (WAF), protection against brute force attacks and SQL injections, disabling the code editor in production, automatic daily backups on an external server, and SSL certificate. With our maintenance packages, your website is monitored continuously.
Can you create a WooCommerce store? expand_more
Yes, WooCommerce is the most widely used e-commerce plugin in the world and we master every aspect. We configure product pages optimized for conversion, payment gateways (Stripe, PayPal, etc.), inventory management, shipping costs, promo codes, upsells and cross-sells. The theme is custom-developed to showcase your products and guide the buyer toward completing their order.
Do you offer WordPress maintenance? expand_more
Yes, and we strongly recommend it. An unmaintained WordPress site is a security vulnerability waiting to be exploited. Our monthly maintenance plans include tested updates of the core, theme and plugins, automatic daily backups, 24/7 security monitoring, performance monitoring and technical support by email and phone. Plans start at 99 euros per month. It is an investment that protects your website and your peace of mind.
