The problem

IS YOUR LOGO WORKING AGAINST YOU?

A bad logo does more damage than you think. Here are the signs that it's time to act.

thumb_down

Your logo looks amateurish

You had it made by a friend, on Canva or through a 50-euro website. The result doesn't reflect the quality of what you sell. Your competitors have polished logos and you're missing out on contracts because your image doesn't inspire the trust needed to trigger a purchase.

history

Your logo is dated

It was created ten years ago, with the graphic trends of the time. The 3D effects, excessive gradients and overloaded fonts send a clear message to your prospects: you're behind the times. Your logo ages your business instead of making it shine.

broken_image

Your logo doesn't work everywhere

It's illegible at small sizes, pixelated on mobile, invisible on dark backgrounds. You have no monochrome version for engraving or simplified version for favicons. With every new medium, you improvise and the result is always a disappointing compromise.

groups

Your logo looks like everyone else's

Online generators and template banks produce generic logos used by hundreds of businesses. If your logo doesn't distinguish you at a glance, you're interchangeable. Your prospects don't remember you because nothing makes you memorable.

Frequently Asked Questions

YOUR QUESTIONS ABOUT LOGO DESIGN

How much does professional logo design cost? expand_more
Professional logo design starts att 800 euros at Pirabel Labs. This price includes an in-depth creative brief, the research phase, 3 distinct creative concepts presented with their rationales, 2 revision rounds and delivery of all vector and export files. The final price depends on project complexity and requested variants. Each project receives a customised quote.
How many logo proposals will I receive? expand_more
You systematically receive 3 completeely distinct logo concepts. These are not variants of the same logo, but 3 different creative directions, each exploring a unique approach. Each concept is presented with a detailed creative rationale explaining the choices of form, color and typography. After your selection, we proceed with 2 revision rounds to refine the chosen concept.
How long does it take to create a logo? expand_more
Allow 2 to 4 weeks for a quality professional logo. The first week is dedicated to the brief and research on your sector. The second week to creating 3 concepts. Weeks 3 and 4 are for revisions and finalization. We never rush the creative process — a good logo requires time, research and strategic thinking.
What files will I receive for my logo? expand_more
You receive a completee and organized kit: editable vector files (Adobe Illustrator AI, SVG, EPS), high-resolution exports (PDF, transparent PNG in 5 sizes), and all variants (color, monochrome, negative, light background, dark background). Everything is structured in a clearly named folder so you can find the right file instantly, whatever the usage.
Why not use an online logo generator? expand_more
Online generators use generic templates shared by thousands of businesses. Your logo will be neither unique, nor suited to your positioning, nor optimised for all media. A professional logo is custom-designed after a thorough strategic reflection on your market, your values and your target audience. That reflection is what makes the difference between a throwaway logo and one that builds your brand for years.
Do I own the logo created? expand_more
Yes, you hold 100% of the rights to your logo once the project is finalized and payment made. No hidden license, no usage restrictions, no royalties. You can use your logo freely across all your media — web, print, merchandising, vehicles, signage — forever. We retain no rights to the delivered creations.
