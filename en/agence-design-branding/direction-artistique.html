Frequently Asked Questions

YOUR QUESTIONS ABOUT ART DIRECTION

What is art direction exactly? expand_more
Art direction is the overarching creative vision that guides all of your visual communications. It defines the photographic style, color palettes, typography in context, layouts, visual tone and the aesthetic universe of your brand. It is the creative thread that makes your brand instantly recognizable across all touchpoints and channels.
What is the difference between art direction and visual identity? expand_more
A visual identity is a set of fixed elements: logo, colors, typography. Art direction goes further: it defines how those elements are used creatively in your communications. It guides the photo style, campaign mood, product staging and overall visual storytelling. Visual identity is the vocabulary; art direction is how you tell the story.
What types of projects do you handle? expand_more
We work on all projects that require a cohesive creative vision: advertising campaigns, photoshoots, content creation for social media, websites, packaging, catalogs, events and product launches. We can also define the overall art direction of your brand to guide all your future creations with coherence.
How does a photoshoot directed by your team work? expand_more
We manage the photoshoot from A to Z: creative brief, reference moodboard, model casting if needed, location scouting, coordination of the photographer and stylist, real-time art direction during the shoot and final photo selection. We ensure that each image matches the defined vision and your brand identity.
Do you work with external teams? expand_more
Yes. We have a network of photographers, videographers, stylists, makeup artists and set designers we collaborate with regularly. We can also work with your existing providers by bringing our art direction and creative supervision. The important thing is that every contributor follows the same vision for a cohesive and impactful result.
How much does an art direction service cost? expand_more
The price depends on the project scope. Art direction for a one-off photoshoot starts from 2,000 euros. Comprehensive brand art direction including moodboards, guidelines and campaign supervision ranges between 5,000 and 15,000 euros. We provide a precise and detailed quote after analyzing your needs and creative objectives.
