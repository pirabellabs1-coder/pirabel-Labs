The problem

IS YOUR BRAND IMAGE HOLDING YOU BACK?

If you recognise yourself in any of these situations, your visual identity needs a serious overhaul.

shuffle

No consistency between your materials

Your website uses one colour palette, your social media another, and your business cards yet a third. Nobody on your team knows which fonts to use or which logo format is the right one. The result: a fragmented brand that loses credibility with every new material.

visibility_off

You are invisible against the competition

Your competitors have polished and memorable brands. Yours goes unnoticed. Nothing distinguishes you visually in your sector. Your prospects choose the cheapest option because they perceive no difference in value between you and the others.

sentiment_dissatisfied

Your image does not reflect your quality

You offer an excellent service or product, but your visual identity tells a different story. It looks amateur, outdated or generic. Your potential clients judge your professionalism at a glance — and what they see does not convince them to trust you.

timer

Every visual is created from scratch

Without a defined graphic system, every Instagram post, every sales brochure, every PowerPoint presentation is an improvisation. You waste time and money reinventing the wheel each time, and the result always lacks cohesion.

Frequently Asked Questions

YOUR QUESTIONS ABOUT VISUAL IDENTITY

What does a complete visual identity include? expand_more
A complete visual identity includes the main logo and all its variants (horizontal, vertical, icon, monochrome, negative), the colour palette with codes for web and print (Hex, RGB, CMYK, Pantone), the typographic system with heading and text hierarchies, custom patterns and graphic elements, custom iconography and a detailed brand guide of 30 to 50 pages. It is the combination of all these elements, used consistently, that builds a recognisable and professional brand.
How much does a complete visual identity cost? expand_more
A complete visual identity starts at 2,500 euros at Pirabel Labs. The price varies according to the complexity of your project, the number of materials to produce and your specific needs. A rebranding project for an established business with numerous variations will naturally be more complex than a first identity for a startup. Each project is subject to a personalised and detailed quote after our discovery call.
What is the difference between a logo and a visual identity? expand_more
The logo is a central element of your visual identity, but it only represents a small part of the system. The visual identity encompasses the logo, the colour palette, the typographies, the patterns, the photographic style, the icons, the layout rules and the application examples. It is the combination of all these elements that creates the visual consistency of your brand. A logo alone is not enough to guarantee this consistency across all your communication materials.
How long does it take to create a visual identity? expand_more
Allow 4 to 6 weeks for a complete visual identity of professional quality. The first week is dedicated to the brief and strategic research. The second to moodboards and creative direction validation. Weeks 3 and 4 to concept creation and the complete graphic system. Weeks 5 and 6 to revisions, finalisation and brand guide delivery. We never rush the process — a solid visual identity requires time and reflection.
Can I use my visual identity on all my materials? expand_more
Yes, that is precisely the goal of our approach. Your visual identity is designed to work on all materials: website, social media, business cards, packaging, signage, vehicles, presentations, stationery, uniforms. The brand guide we deliver contains specific application rules for each format and each medium. Your team can create materials that comply with your identity without needing a designer for every publication.
Do I receive the source files? expand_more
Absolutely. You receive all editable source files (Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Figma), export formats for web (SVG, PNG, WebP in various sizes), for print (high-resolution PDF, EPS in CMYK) and the complete brand guide as an interactive PDF. You own all the created elements. No usage restrictions, no hidden licences. Your identity belongs to you 100%.
