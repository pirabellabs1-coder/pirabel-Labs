The problem

DOES THIS SOUND FAMILIAR?

If you recognize yourself in any of these situations, you're in the right place.

history

Your image is outdated

Your logo is ten years old, your colors no longer match your positioning and your visual assets look like they belong to another era. Prospects judge your seriousness at a glance — and what they see is no longer convincing them to engage.

thumb_down

Your logo looks amateurish

You had it made by a relative, on Canva or through a 50-euro template site. The result does not reflect the quality of what you actually sell. Your competitors have polished brands and you are missing contracts because your image fails to inspire trust.

shuffle

No visual consistency

Your website uses one color palette, your social media another, and your business cards yet a third. No one on your team knows which fonts to use or which is the correct logo format. The result: a fragmented and illegible brand.

help_outline

Confusion around your brand

Your clients do not understand what you do at first glance. Your positioning is blurry, your message diluted. You know your offer is solid, but your visual identity does not tell the right story and does not connect with the right emotions.

groups

No differentiation

Your sector is saturated. Your competitors all look alike, and so do you. Nothing in your visual identity sets you apart. You are interchangeable in the eyes of prospects, who pick the cheapest option because they perceive no difference in value.

broken_image

Inconsistent materials

Every new brochure, every Instagram post, every PowerPoint presentation is created from scratch. You waste time, money and credibility. Without a graphic system, every asset becomes an improvisation that weakens your brand over time.

Our approach

WE DON'T JUST DO GRAPHICS.
WE BUILD BRANDS THAT LAST.

At Pirabel Labs, design is never merely decorative. Every visual choice — color, typography, shape, spacing — is a strategic decision serving your positioning. We do not draw pretty logos: we build complete visual systems that work everywhere, from the favicon on your website to the signage on your storefront.

Our method always begins with deep understanding of your market, your customers and your competitors. Before touching a pencil, we define with you the brand territory, the values to communicate and the emotions to evoke. This strategic groundwork is the foundation of every design that stands the test of time.

The result? A coherent, distinctive and memorable visual identity that inspires trust at first contact. A graphic system that your team can use easily, without needing a designer for every new publication. And a brand that stands out in a saturated market.

Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING YOU WANT TO KNOW ABOUT DESIGN & BRANDING

How much does a complete visual identity cost? expand_more
A complete visual identity package — including logo, brand guidelines, color palette, typography system, graphic elements and brand guide — starts at 2,500 euros. The final price depends on project complexity, the number of brand applications to design and your specific business requirements. For a standalone logo, we offer packages starting from 800 euros. Every project receives a personalized quote after our initial discovery conversation to align expectations and budget realistically.
How long does logo design take? expand_more
Allow 2 to 4 weeks for a professional logo design. The first week is dedicated to the brief and strategic research. The second week focuses on concept creation. Weeks 3 and 4 are dedicated to revisions and finalization. For a complete visual identity, plan for 4 to 6 weeks. We never rush the creative process — a strong logo deserves proper time, deep reflection and rigorous craftsmanship.
What is the difference between a logo and a visual identity? expand_more
The logo is a central element of your visual identity, but it represents only one part of the broader system. The full visual identity encompasses the logo, color palette, typography, patterns, photographic style, iconography, layout rules and application examples. It is the cohesive ensemble of these elements, used consistently, that builds a recognizable and professional brand presence over time.
Why invest in branding rather than just a logo? expand_more
A logo alone is not enough to build a strong brand. Without brand guidelines or a coherent visual system, every asset you create becomes an improvisation. Complete branding guarantees that your website, social media, business cards and presentations all tell the same story with the same quality. This consistency is what builds trust and recognition for your brand over time across every customer touchpoint.
How many logo proposals will I receive? expand_more
You systematically receive 3 distinct logo concepts, each exploring a different creative direction. Every proposal comes with a strategic rationale explaining the design choices. After selecting your preferred concept, we proceed with 2 rounds of revisions to refine it until it matches exactly what you envisioned, with adjustments to typography, proportions, color treatment and final details.
What does the final brand guide contain? expand_more
The brand guide is a complete document that includes: all logo versions with their usage rules and clear-space requirements, the color palette with Hex, RGB, CMYK and Pantone codes, primary and secondary typography hierarchies, complementary graphic elements, application examples across various media and visual prohibitions. It is the reference document that allows your team — or any external agency — to create assets that respect your brand identity perfectly.
Do you work with startups or only large enterprises? expand_more
We work with all client profiles: early-stage startups in launch phase needing their first solid identity, growth-stage small businesses wanting to professionalize their image and large enterprises undergoing complete brand refresh. Every project adapts to your budget, your stage of development and your strategic ambitions. We firmly believe that every business deserves a visual identity worthy of its ambitions, regardless of company size or industry sector.
Do you also handle packaging design? expand_more
Yes, packaging design is integral to our design and branding offering. We design packaging that attracts attention on retail shelves, tells your brand story and respects technical printing constraints. From product labels to gift boxes, including shipping cartons, we create unboxing experiences that reinforce your premium positioning and build customer loyalty across direct-to-consumer and retail channels alike.
