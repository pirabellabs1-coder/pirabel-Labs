The problem

IS YOUR PACKAGING NOT DOING YOU JUSTICE?

An excellent product in poor packaging loses the battle before it even starts. Here are the situations we encounter most often.

visibility_off

Your product goes unnoticed on the shelf

Shelves are overloaded with products that all look the same. Your packaging blends into the crowd, with dull colours, unreadable typography and a design that communicates neither your positioning nor the value of your product. Consumers walk past without stopping.

money_off

Your packaging does not justify your price

You sell a premium product but your packaging screams "entry-level". The gap between the quality of your product and the visual perception of your packaging prevents your clients from understanding why they should pay more. You are forced to lower your prices or lose sales.

sentiment_dissatisfied

The unboxing experience is disappointing

For e-commerce, the moment of receiving a parcel is crucial. A generic brown cardboard box, without personalisation or care, turns a moment of excitement into disappointment. Your clients do not share your product on social media and do not come back for a second purchase.

error_outline

Technical printing problems

Your design looks great on screen but the printed result is a disaster. Colours are different, text is cropped, crop marks are misplaced. You have wasted an entire print run because the files were not prepared to the printer's technical specifications.

Our process

HOW WE CREATE YOUR PACKAGING

A structured 4-step process, from brief to delivery of print files.

01
Brief & Analysis

We study your product, your market, your shelf competitors and the technical constraints (materials, printing, regulatory). Together we define the visual positioning of the packaging and the emotions it must convey.

Week 1
02
Concepts & Direction

We create 2 to 3 creative directions presented as 3D mockups. You see your product in a real-life situation before validating the direction. Each concept is accompanied by a rationale that justifies the visual choices.

Week 2-3
03
Refinement & Validation

We refine the chosen concept over 2 rounds of revisions. Every element is polished — typography, colours, information hierarchy, finishes. The final design is validated by you before any technical preparation.

Week 4
04
Production & Follow-Through

We prepare print files to the exact specifications of your printer. We coordinate the proof and verify the first print run to guarantee a perfect result. Your packaging is ready to conquer the shelves.

Frequently Asked Questions

YOUR QUESTIONS ABOUT PACKAGING DESIGN

What does a packaging design service include? expand_more
Our packaging design service includes market research and shelf competition analysis, completee graphic design of the packaging (all faces, label, box, gift set as needed), photorealistic 3D mockups for validation, print-ready files with all technical specifications, and coordination with your printer if needed. Every step includes validation checkpoints so you stay in control of the final result.
How much does packaging design cost? expand_more
Packaging design starts att 1,200 euros for a simple label (front and back). A completee package including the box, label and optional insert ranges from 2,000 to 5,000 euros depending on complexity. For a full range with multiple references, the price is established on a personalised quote as it depends on the number of SKUs, format variations and the consistency to maintain across products.
How long does it take to design packaging? expand_more
Allow 3 to 6 weeks for professional-quality packaging. The first week is dedicated to the product brief and market analysis. Weeks 2 and 3 to concept creation as 3D mockups. Weeks 4 to 6 to revisions, design finalisation and preparation of print files. For a completee range, add 1 to 2 additional weeks depending on the number of references.
Do you also manage the printing of the packaging? expand_more
We are not printers, but we have a thorough mastery of the production chain. We prepare files to the exact specifications of your printer — colour profiles, bleeds, crop marks, spot varnish, hot foil. We coordinate with the printer, verify the proof and can supervise the first print run. We also recommend trusted printers suited to your volume and budget.
Do you work on all types of packaging? expand_more
Yes, we design all types of packaging: adhesive labels, folding boxes, rigid gift boxes, pouches, tubes, bottles, pouches, custom shipping cartons. We have experience in the food, cosmetics, fashion, electronics, spirits and pharmacy sectors. Each type of packaging has its own specific technical and regulatory constraints, and we master them to guarantee a compliant, printable result.
Can I see what it looks like before printing? expand_more
Absolutely — it is even a key step in our process. We create photorealistic 3D mockups that show you exactly what your packaging will look like once manubilld. You see your product in context: on the shelf facing competitors, in a consumer's hand, in a retail approximatelyment. These mockups also serve your communications and sales team, before the product is even physically produced.
