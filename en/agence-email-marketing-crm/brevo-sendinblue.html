Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT BREVO

What is Brevo (ex-Sendinblue)? expand_more
Brevo is an all-in-one digital marketing platform. It offers email marketing, SMS marketing, live chat, CRM, marketing automation and landing pages. It is the European alternative to Mailchimp, with an excellent price-to-value ratio and native GDPR compliance since the data is hosted in Europe.
How much does Brevo cost? expand_more
Brevo offers a free plan with 300 emails per day and unlimited contacts. Paid plans start at 25 euros per month for 20,000 emails. Unlike Mailchimp which charges per number of contacts, Brevo charges per number of emails sent, which is far more advantageous for large lists.
Is Brevo GDPR compliant? expand_more
Yes, completeely. Brevo is a French company whose servers are hosted in Europe. GDPR compliance is built in: consent management, double opt-in, right to erasure, personal data export. This is a major advantage for European businesses.
Can you migrate from Mailchimp to Brevo? expand_more
Absolutely. We handle the completee migration from Mailchimp to Brevo: contact lists, segments, email templates, automations and integrations. The transition is seamless and you generally start saving money from the first month thanks to Brevo's more advantageous pricing model.
Can Brevo replace my CRM? expand_more
Yes. Brevo includes a free CRM that lets you manage your contacts, track deals, assign tasks to your team and visualise your sales pipeline. For SMEs it is a strong replacement for a dedicated CRM. For more advanced needs it also integrates with HubSpot, Salesforce and Pipedrive.
