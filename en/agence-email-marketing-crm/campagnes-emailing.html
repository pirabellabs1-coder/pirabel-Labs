The problem

WHY YOUR CAMPAIGNS ARE UNDERPERFORMING

You send emails, but the results are disappointing? Here are the most common mistakes we see.

report

Your emails end up in spam

Your DNS configuration is incomplete or incorrect. SPF, DKIM, DMARC — these acronyms may mean nothing to you, but they determine whether your emails reach the inbox or the spam folder. Without proper authentication, even the best email in the world will never be read. Every campaign lost to spam is a wasted investment and customers you will never reach.

visibility_off

Nobody opens your emails

Your subject lines are boring, generic or too long. Your emails are sent at the wrong time, when your contacts are busy or inactive. Your sender name does not inspire trust. Result: an open rate of 10–15% when you should be at 35–40%. Every unopened email is a lost sales opportunity.

group_off

The same email for everyone

You send the same campaign to your entire list without segmentation. A new prospect receives the same message as a loyal client of 3 years. A visitor interemaind in product A receives a promotion for product B. This lack of segmentation kills your engagement and pushes your contacts to unsubscribe out of frustration.

broken_image

A design that breaks on mobile

Over 60% of emails are opened on mobile, but your template shows cropped images, unreadable text and buttons impossible to tap on a small screen. An email that displays poorly is deleted immediately. Your design must be flawless on every device — smartphone, tablet, desktop, Outlook, Gmail, Apple Mail.

Frequently Asked Questions

YOUR QUESTIONS ABOUT OUR EMAIL CAMPAIGNS

How many email campaigns should I send per month? expand_more
The ideal frequency depends on your audience and your sector. Generally, 2 to 4 campaigns per month strike a good balance: enough to stay top of mind with your contacts without overwhelming them. We adjust frequency based on engagement metrics and feedback from your list.
Why do my emails end up in spam? expand_more
Several factors cause emails to land in spam: incorrect DNS configuration (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), a list that has not been cleaned of invalid addresses, overly promotional content, an unbalanced image-to-text ratio, or a high complaint rate. Our deliverability audit pinpoints exactly what is blocking delivery and we fix each issue to reach 95%+ deliverability.
What open rate can I expect? expand_more
The average open rate across all sectors is 21%. With our method — precise segmentation, subject lines tested via A/B testing, sending at the optimal time — our clients consistently reach 35 to 50% open rates. The key is relevance: the right message, to the right person, at the right time.
Do you create the email design or just the strategy? expand_more
Both. We define the editorial strategy, create custom responsive templates, write the copy and subject lines, configure the sends and analyse the results. You have nothing to manage — we handle everything from conception to post-campaign analysis.
How do you segment sending lists? expand_more
We segment according to several criteria: behaviour (opens, clickks, purchases), demographics (location, sector), lifecycle stage (new contact, active client, inactive), and engagement (engagement score). Each segment receives a message tailored to its context and needs, which maximises relevance and conversions.
What is the average ROI of email marketing? expand_more
Email marketing delivers an average ROI of 36 euros for every 1 euro invested, making it the most profitable marketing channel. With a well-executed strategy — segmentation, personalisation, A/B testing — our clients consistently reach an ROI of 40 to 50 euros for every 1 euro invested. No other channel offers this level of return.
