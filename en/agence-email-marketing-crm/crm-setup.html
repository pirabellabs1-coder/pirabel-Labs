Frequently Asked Questions

YOUR QUESTIONS ABOUT CRM SETUP

Which CRM do you recommend? expand_more
It depends on your size, your budget and your needs. For SMEs and startups we often recommend HubSpot for its powerful freemium or Pipedrive for its excellent price-to-value ratio and simplicity. For larger businesses with complex needs, Salesforce remains the reference. We analyse your situation and recommend the most suitable tool, without bias toward any platform.
How long does a CRM setup take? expand_more
For a standard setup with data migration, allow 3 to 6 weeks. For an advanced configuration with multiple integrations and complex workflows, the timeline can extend to 8 to 12 weeks. We proceed in phases so your team can start using the CRM in the first few weeks, even before the full project is complete.
Can you migrate our data from our old system? expand_more
Yes. Data migration is an integral part of our service. We migrate your contacts, companies, deals, interaction history and documents from your old CRM, your Excel files, Google Sheets or any other system. We clean and deduplicate the data during migration so you start on a clean and usable base.
Do you train our teams on how to use the CRM? expand_more
Absolutely. Training is a key step in our service. We train each team with hands-on sessions adapted to their role: sales, managers, marketing and support. We also create personalised video documentation with your specific workflows so that new team members can train themselves independently.
Can the CRM integrate with our existing tools? expand_more
Yes. We configure integrations with your existing tools: email platform, website, forms, calendar, billing tools, Slack, Zapier, Make and many more. The goal is for your CRM to become the central hub of your entire commercial activity, connected to all your tools without double data entry or loss of information.
Do you offer support after implementation? expand_more
Yes. After launch, we provide 30 days of included support to answer your teams' questions and adjust configurations as needed. Beyond that, we offer monthly maintenance and optimisation contracts to evolve your CRM as you grow, add new workflows and train new team members.
