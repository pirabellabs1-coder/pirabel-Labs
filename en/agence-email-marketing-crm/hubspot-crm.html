Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT HUBSPOT CRM

What is HubSpot CRM? expand_more
HubSpot CRM is a customer relationship management platform that centralizes all your contacts, interactions and commercial data. The free CRM includes unlimited contact management, deal tracking, forms, live chat and basic reporting. The paid hubs add marketing automation, customer service and CMS.
Is HubSpot CRM really free? expand_more
Yes, the basic HubSpot CRM is free for life with unlimited contacts. It includes contact and company management, deal and pipeline tracking, forms, live chat, email tracking and basic reporting. The paid hubs start at 20 euros per month.
How long does it take to configure HubSpot? expand_more
Basic CRM configuration takes 1 to 2 weeks. A complete implementation with Marketing Hub, automation workflows, integration with your existing tools and team training takes 3 to 6 weeks depending on the complexity of your commercial and marketing processes.
Does HubSpot integrate with our existing tools? expand_more
Yes. HubSpot offers more than 1500 native integrations with tools like Gmail, Outlook, Slack, Zoom, Shopify, WordPress, Salesforce, Google Ads and Facebook Ads. For custom integrations, we use the HubSpot API to connect any tool to your CRM.
Can we migrate from another CRM to HubSpot? expand_more
Absolutely. We handle complete migrations from Salesforce, Pipedrive, Zoho CRM, Monday or any other tool. We transfer your contacts, interaction histories, deals, notes and files. The migration is meticulously planned to minimize disruption to your business activity.
