TOUT SAVOIR SUR HUBSPOT CRM

What is HubSpot CRM? expand_more
HubSpot CRM is a plateforme de gestion de la relation client that centralise all your contacts, interactions and données commerciales. Le CRM free inclut la gestion de contacts illimites, le suivi deals, the formulaires, le chat en direct and le reporting de base. The hubs payants ajoutent le marketing automation, le service client and le CMS.
HubSpot CRM est-il vraiment free? expand_more
Oui, le CRM de base de HubSpot is free a vie with contacts illimites. Il inclut la gestion contacts and businesss, le suivi deals and du pipeline, the formulaires, le chat en direct, le suivi emails and le reporting de base. The hubs payants commencent a 20 euros per month.
How long does it take to configurer HubSpot? expand_more
La configuration de base du CRM prend 1 a 2 semaines. A implementation complète with Marketing Hub, workflows d'automation, integration with your tools existants and formation de your team prend 3 a 6 semaines according to la complexite de your processesesus commerciaux and marketing.
HubSpot s'integre it with our outils existants? expand_more
Oui. HubSpot propose more than 1500 integrations natives with outils like Gmail, Outlook, Slack, Zoom, Shopify, WordPress, Salesforce, Google Ads and Facebook Ads. For the integrations custom, we utilisons l'API HubSpot for connecter n'importe quel outil à your CRM.
Peut-on migrer since un autre CRM towards HubSpot? expand_more
Absolument. We assurons la migration complète since Salesforce, Pipedrive, Zoho CRM, Monday or everything autre outil. We transferons your contacts, historiques d'interactions, deals, notes and fichiers. La migration is planifiee minutieusement for minimiser l'interruption de your business commerciale.
