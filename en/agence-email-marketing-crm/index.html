Frequently Asked Questions

TOUT CE QUE VOUS VOULEZ SAVOIR SUR L'EMAIL MARKETING

Why do my emails arrivent-ils en spam ? expand_more
Several facteurs causent le passage en spam : mauvaise configuration DNS (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), liste non nettoyee with adresses invalides, contenu trop promotionnel or taux de plaintes eleve. Our audit identifie exactly what bloque and we corrigeons each point for atteindre 95%+ de délivrabilite.
Quel CRM choisir for mon business ? expand_more
Le choix depend de your taille, your budget and your needs. For the startups, HubSpot free or Brevo are excellents. For the PME, ActiveCampaign or Pipedrive offrent un bon equilibre. For the businesss more grandes, Salesforce or HubSpot Pro. We you guidons towards la meilleure option after un audit de your processesesus.
Quel open rate puis-je esperer ? expand_more
Le open rate moyen all sectors is de 21%. With our method — segmentation fine, objets testes, envoi au bon moment — our clients atteignent régulièrement 35 a 50% de open rate. La clé is la pertinence : le bon message, a la bonne personne, au bon moment.
Combien de temps for voir results en email marketing ? expand_more
The premiers results are visibles the first campagne optimizede, soit in 2 a 4 weeks. L'amelioration is progressive : au bout de 3 month, you have a base segmentee, sequences automatisees and metriques stables. Le ROI complete se mesure généralement a 6 month.
What is marketing automation exactly ? expand_more
Le marketing automation consiste a envoyer automatiquement le bon email au bon moment, according to le comportement de your contacts. Par example : un email de bienvenue when quelthan a s'inscrit, un rappel when un panier is abandonne, a sequence de nurturing for transform un prospect froid en client chaud. Tout tourne without intervention manuelle.
Is it necessary to a grande base de contacts for get started ? expand_more
Non. Meme with 500 contacts, l'email marketing is rentable si your liste is qualifiede. Mieux vaut 500 contacts engages that 10 000 that n'ouvrent never your emails. We you aidons a construire and faire grandir your liste with strategys d'acquisition ethiques and high-performance.
Gérez-you la creation emails or juste la strategy ? expand_more
The deux. We définissons la strategy, creons the templates, rédigeons the textes, configurons the automations and analysons the results. You n'have rien a gérer — on s'occupe de everything, de A a Z. You validez and we exécutons.
Comment mesurez-you le ROI de l'email marketing ? expand_more
We trackons every euro génère by your campaigns : open rate, taux de clic, conversions, revenus attribues. With un CRM bien configure, we savons exactly combien each email, each sequence and each segment rapporte. You receive un reporting mensuel clair and actionnable.
