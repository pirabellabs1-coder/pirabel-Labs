Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING YOU WANT TO KNOW ABOUT EMAIL MARKETING

Why do my emails land in spam folders? expand_more
Several factors trigger spam filters: poor DNS configuration (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), unclean lists with invalid addresses, overly promotional content or high complaint rates. Our audit identifies exactly what is blocking your emails and we fix every issue to reach 95%+ deliverability across major mailbox providers.
Which CRM should I choose for my business? expand_more
The choice depends on your size, budget and needs. For startups, HubSpot free tier or Brevo are excellent. For small and medium businesses, ActiveCampaign or Pipedrive offer good balance. For larger enterprises, Salesforce or HubSpot Pro. We guide you to the best option after auditing your processes and team workflows.
What open rates can I expect? expand_more
The average open rate across all industries is 21%. With our method — fine-grained segmentation, tested subject lines, optimal send timing — our clients regularly achieve 35 to 50% open rates. The key is relevance: the right message, to the right person, at the right moment in their customer journey.
How long does it take to see email marketing results? expand_more
First results are visible from the first optimized campaign, within 2 to 4 weeks. Improvement is progressive: after 3 months you have a segmented base, automated sequences and stable metrics. The complete ROI is generally measured at the 6-month mark with substantial revenue attribution.
What is marketing automation exactly? expand_more
Marketing automation means sending the right email at the right time automatically, based on contact behavior. Examples: a welcome email when someone subscribes, a reminder when a cart is abandoned, a nurturing sequence to transform a cold prospect into a warm customer. Everything runs without manual intervention.
Do I need a large contact base to get started? expand_more
No. Even with 500 contacts, email marketing is profitable if your list is qualified. 500 engaged contacts beat 10,000 who never open your emails. We help you build and grow your list with ethical, high-performing acquisition strategies tailored to your industry context.
Do you handle email creation or just strategy? expand_more
Both. We define the strategy, create templates, write the copy, configure automations and analyze results. You have nothing to manage — we take care of everything, from A to Z. You validate the direction and we execute the work with rigor.
How do you measure email marketing ROI? expand_more
We track every dollar generated by your campaigns: open rates, click-through rates, conversions, attributed revenue. With a properly configured CRM, we know exactly how much each email, each sequence and each segment generates. You receive a clear, actionable monthly report.
