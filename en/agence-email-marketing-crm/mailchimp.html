Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT MAILCHIMP

Why choose Mailchimp for email marketing? expand_more
Mailchimp is the world's most popular email marketing platform with more than 13 million users. It offers an intuitive interface, professional templates, powerful automation and integrations with more than 300 tools. Ideal for businesses that want a reliable, easy-to-use tool.
How much does Mailchimp cost? expand_more
Mailchimp offers a free plan up to 500 contacts. Paid plans start at 13 dollars per month for the Essentials plan. The Standard plan at 20 dollars per month offers advanced automations and the Premium plan at 350 dollars per month is for large organizations.
Is Mailchimp suitable for the international market? expand_more
Yes. The Mailchimp interface is available in multiple languages. Templates are customizable in any language and sign-up forms can be configured in any language. For European businesses concerned about GDPR, we configure the appropriate compliance settings.
What automations can be set up on Mailchimp? expand_more
Mailchimp allows you to create powerful automations: welcome emails, nurturing sequences, abandoned cart follow-ups, birthday emails, product recommendations and re-engagement of inactive subscribers. The Customer Dayney Builder allows you to create complex journeys with conditions and multiple branches.
