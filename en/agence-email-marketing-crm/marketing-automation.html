Frequently Asked Questions

YOUR QUESTIONS ABOUT MARKETING AUTOMATION

What exactly is marketing automation? expand_more
Marketing automation means automating the sending of emails and marketing actions based on the behaviour of your prospects and clients. For example: a visitor downloads your guide, they automatically receive a sequence of 5 emails over 2 weeks that educates them and guides them toward a purchase. Everything happens automatically, 24/7, without human intervention. It is like having a sales rep who never sleeps.
Which marketing automation tools do you use? expand_more
We work with the main platforms on the market: HubSpot, ActiveCampaign, Mailchimp, Brevo (ex-Sendinblue), Klaviyo for e-commerce, and ConvertKit for content creators. We choose the tool best suited to your size, your budget and your objectives. If you already have a tool in place, we work with it and optimise it.
How long does it take to set up automation? expand_more
A completee setup generally takes 4 to 8 weeks. The first 2 weeks are dedicated to the audit, strategy and writing the sequences. The following 2 to 4 weeks to technical configuration, testing and launch. The first sequences start producing results in the first week after going live.
Does marketing automation work for B2B? expand_more
Absolutely. Marketing automation is actually particularly effective in B2B where sales cycles are long. Nurturing sequences educate your prospects over weeks or months, lead scoring identifies the hottest prospects for your sales team, and automated workflows re-engage dormant opportunities without any manual effort.
How many emails do you send in an automated sequence? expand_more
It depends on the objective and the buying cycle. A welcome sequence generally contains 3 to 5 emails over 7 to 14 days. A B2B nurturing sequence can contain 8 to 12 emails over 4 to 8 weeks. An abandoned cart sequence contains 3 to 4 emails over 3 days. We test and optimise the number of emails to find the sweet spot between engagement and conversion.
How do you measure the results of automation? expand_more
We track the key KPIs of each sequence: open rate, click rate, conversion rate, revenue generated per sequence, unsubscribe rate and overall ROI. Every month you receive a detailed report with the performance of each workflow and our optimisation recommendations. We A/B test continuously to improve results.
