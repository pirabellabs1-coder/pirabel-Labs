Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT AI AGENTS

What is an AI agent and how does it work? expand_more
An AI agent is an artificial intelligence system capable of acting autonomously to accomplish complex tasks. Unlike a simple chatbot that answers questions, or a workflow that executes predefined steps, an AI agent understands context, analyzes data, makes decisions and executes actions on its own. It is connected to your tools (CRM, analytics, e-commerce, marketing) and uses available information to reach the objectives you have defined for it. It learns from each interaction and continuously improves.
What is the difference between an AI agent and a chatbot? expand_more
The difference is fundamental. A chatbot is reactive: it waits for a question and answers it. An AI agent is proactive: it takes initiative, analyzes situations without being asked and executes actions autonomously. For example, a chatbot can answer the question "what is my revenue this month?". An AI agent continuously analyzes your sales, detects a 15% drop on a specific segment, ideallyntifies the probable cause and recommends — or executes — a corrective action. It is a true digital collaborator.
What types of tasks can an AI agent accomplish? expand_more
The possibilities are vast and depend on your specific needs. Here are the most common use cases: data analysis and intelligent report generation, lead qualification and nurturing, marketing content creation (articles, posts, emails, product sheets), advertising campaign management and optimization, automated competitive intelligence, project management and task assignment, document processing and classification, advanced customer service with complex problem resolution, and real-time price optimization.
Does the AI agent make decisions without human control? expand_more
You always retain control. We define the autonomy limits of your agent together according to three levels. Level 1: the agent automatically executes low-risk and repetitive decisions (sending emails, updating data, generating reports). Level 2: the agent recommends an action and waits for your validation before executing it. Level 3: the agent escalates to a human for unusual situations or high-stakes decisions. You adjust these levels at any time and receive detailed reports on every action taken.
How much does it cost to implement an AI agent? expand_more
AI agents are part of our custom Entertakingfer. The cost depends on several factors: the complexity of tasks to automate, the number of integrations required (CRM, analytics, e-commerce, marketing), the desired level of autonomy and the volume of data to process. We offer a free audit to evaluate your needs and provideally a precise, tailored quote. The return on investment is generally visible within the first 2 months thanks to time saved and faster decisions.
How long does it take to deploy an AI agent? expand_more
A simple AI agent — for example a data analysis agent that generates automatic reports — can be operational in 2 to 3 weeks. A complex agent with multiple integrations, advanced decision-making, content generation and continuous learning takes 4 to 8 weeks. We proceed in phases: each capability is developed, tested and validated individually before adding new ones. This approach guarantees a reliable and high-performing agent from day one of deployment.
