Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT AI CHATBOTS

What is an AI chatbot and how does it work? expand_more
An AI chatbot is an intelligent conversational assistant trained on your business's specific data: your products, your services, your pricing, your FAQ and your communication style. Unlike a rule-based classic chatbot, it understands natural language and adapts to the way each visitor phrases their questions. It provides relevant, customised answers 24 hours a day. It can be deployed on your website, WhatsApp, Instagram or Facebook Messenger.
How is an AI chatbot different from a classic chatbot? expand_more
A classic chatbot works with predefined rules and rigid decision trees. It only understands questions that were anticipated in advance and fails when the phrasing is slightly different. An AI chatbot understands natural language, adapts to each user's varied phrasings and learns from each interaction to improve over time. It can answer questions it has never seen before, as long as they relate to its area of expertise.
On which channels can I deploy my AI chatbot? expand_more
Your AI chatbot can be deployed simultaneously on your website as an embedded widget, on WhatsApp Business, Facebook Messenger, Instagram Direct, Telegram and even by SMS. We configure the chatbot once and manage deployment across all desired channels. Your clients contact you on their preferred channel and receive exactly the same quality of response, with the same consistency of tone and content, everywhere.
Can the chatbot qualify my leads automatically? expand_more
Absolutely, and it's one of its most powerful assets. The chatbot engages in conversation with each visitor, asks strategic questions to identify their needs, budget, urgency level and contact details. It assigns a qualification score to each lead and sends it directly to your CRM with all the collected information. Your sales team only handles genuinely qualified and warm prospects, which drastically improves the conversion rate.
Can my chatbot respond in multiple languages? expand_more
Yes. Our AI chatbots are natively multilingual. They automatically detect the language in which the visitor writes and respond in that same language, fluently and naturally. French, English, Arabic, Spanish, Portuguese, German... Your chatbot adapts to your international audience without you having to create and maintain separate versions for each language. One chatbot, all languages.
How long does it take to deploy an AI chatbot? expand_more
A functional AI chatbot is deployed in 1 to 2 weeks. The first week is dedicated to data collection, training and personalization. The second week covers integration with communication channels, thorough testing and going live. For more complex chatbots with advanced lead qualification, multiple CRM integrations and human handover scenarios, allow 2 to 3 weeks. Optimization then continues permanently.
