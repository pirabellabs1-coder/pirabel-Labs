Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT CHATGPT FOR BUSINESS

How can ChatGPT help my business? expand_more
ChatGPT can automate your customer service, generate marketing content, analyze data, write emails, translate documents, create reports and assist your teams in their daily tasks. The use cases are vast and we adapt them to your sector and your specific needs to maximize the return on investment.
Is ChatGPT secure for professional use? expand_more
Yes, with the right configuration. ChatGPT Enterprise and the OpenAI API offer data encryption, no use of data for model training, SOC 2 compliance and the ability to host data in Europe. We configure the secure approximatelyment tailored to your privacy requirements.
What is the difference between ChatGPT and ChatGPT Enterprise? expand_more
ChatGPT Enterprise is the professional version with enhanced security guarantees, no usage limits, faster performance with unlimited GPT-4, team management, shareable custom GPTs and dedicated support. It is the recommended version for serious businesses.
How long does ChatGPT integration take? expand_more
Basic integration including team training and implementation of simple workflows takes 1 to 2 weeks. Advanced integrations via the OpenAI API such as a custom chatbot, process automation or connection to your internal systems take 3 to 6 weeks depending on complexity.
