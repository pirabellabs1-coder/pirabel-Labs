Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT CLAUDE AI FOR BUSINESS

What is Claude AI? expand_more
Claude is an AI assistant developed by Anthropic, recognized for its trustworthiness, security and ability to handle long documents. Claude excels at document analysis, writing, complex reasoning and following instructions. It is one of the most advanced and safest models on the market for professional use.
Why choose Claude rather than ChatGPT? expand_more
Claude stands out with its very large context window allowing it to handle documents of 200,000 tokens, superior reliability for complex tasks, its ethical approach to AI and its better ability to follow detailed instructions. Claude is particularly suited for document analysis and tasks requiring precision.
Is Claude secure for business data? expand_more
Yes. Anthropic designed Claude with security as a priority. The Claude API does not use your data to train models. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Businesses can use Claude via the API with confidentiality guarantees that comply with enterprise security standards.
What are the use cases for Claude in business? expand_more
Claude excels at analyzing long documents such as contracts and reports, writing structured content, extracting information, translation, intelligent customer support, data analysis and code generation. Its ability to handle large volumes of text makes it the ideal tool for document-heavy businesses.
