FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS — CHATBOT

How much does chatbot creation cost? expand_more
A basic FAQ chatbot starts att 800€. An advanced AI chatbot with lead qualification and CRM integration costs betwein 2,000€ and 5,000€. We offer a free quote tailored to your exact needs.
How long until my chatbot is ready? expand_more
An FAQ chatbot is delivered in 1 to 2 weeks. An advanced AI chatbot with integrations takes 3 to 4 weeks. Training and optimization are ongoing after launch.
On which platforms does the chatbot work? expand_more
Website, WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook Messenger, Telegram and SMS. We integrate the platform that best matches where your clients are.
Can the chatbot respond in multiple languages? expand_more
Yes. Our AI chatbots are multilingual. They detect the user's language and automatically respond in French, English, Arabic, Spanish or any other configured language.
Does the chatbot replace my team? expand_more
No. The chatbot handles repetitive questions (80% of requests) and qualifies leads. Your team focuses on complex cases and high-value sales. It is an assistant, not a replacement.
How does the chatbot learn my information? expand_more
We train it on your data: website, FAQ, product documentation, conversation history. The more data it receives, the more precise it becomes.
