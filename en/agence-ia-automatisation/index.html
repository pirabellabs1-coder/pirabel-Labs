Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING YOU WANT TO KNOW ABOUT AI AND AUTOMATION

What is automation and why does my business need it? expand_more
Automation means delegating repetitive tasks to intelligent tools (Make, N8N, Zapier) that execute them without human intervention. Instead of copying data, sending emails manually or updating files one by one, your systems do it for you. Result: your team focuses on high-value tasks, errors disappear and your productivity explodes.
What is the difference between Make, N8N and Zapier ? expand_more
Zapier is the simplest for basic automations with 6,000+ integrations. Make (formerly Integromat) offers an excellent power-to-price ratio with an advanced visual interface and complex conditional logic. N8N is open-source and ideallyal if you want to host your workflows on your own servers to keep total control of your data. We recommend the tool best suited to your needs, budget and privacy requirements.
What is an AI chatbot and how does it work? expand_more
An AI chatbot is a conversational assistant trained on your specific data — your products, services, FAQ and communication tone. It answers your clients' questions 24/7, qualifies your leads by asking the right questions, and can even book appointments automatically. It integrates with your website, WhatsApp, Instagram or Facebook Messenger.
How long does it take to set up an automation? expand_more
A simple workflow (data transfer, notifications, automatic emails) is operational within 3 to 5 days. A completee system with several interconnected automations, a chatbot and advanced logic takes 2 to 4 weeks. We proceed step by step to ensure each workflow is tested and validated before moving on to the next.
Will automation replace my employees? expand_more
No. Automation eliminates repetitive, low-value tasks — the ones nobody enjoys doing. Your teams focus on what truly matters: strategy, client relationships, creativity and decision-making. Your employees become more productive and more fulfilled, not obsolete.
What is an AI agent and how is it different from a chatbot? expand_more
A chatbot answers questions in a conversation. An AI agent goes much further: it analyses data, makes decisions, executes complex tasks and improves over time. For example, an AI marketing agent can analyse your performance, adjust your campaigns and generate reports — all autonomously. It is a true digital collaborator.
Is my data secure with your automations? expand_more
Absolutely. We use encrypted connections (SSL/TLS), comply with GDPR and can host your workflows on your own servers with N8N if privacy is a priority. Your data always remains yours. We sign a confideallyntiality agreement with every client and never share your information.
What budget should I plan for automating my processes? expand_more
Our Starter plan starts att €800 for essential automations (3 workflows, 5 connected tools). The Growth plan at €2,000 covers a completee system with AI chatbot and advanced integrations. For complex needs (AI agents, dedicated hosting), we offer custom Enterprise solutions. Return on investment is generally visible from the first month.
