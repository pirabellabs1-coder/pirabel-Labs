Frequently Asked Questions

TOUT SAVOIR SUR L'AUTOMATISATION MAKE

What is Make and a quoi that sert? expand_more
Make (anciennement Integromat) is a platform d'automation visuelle that permand connecter your applications between elles and créer workflows automatises without ecrire a seule ligne de code. You pouvez automatiser transferts de données between outils, envois d'emails, la management de commandes e-commerce, la creation de rapports and much more still. Son interface drag-and-drop rend la creation de workflows intuitive, same for the scenarios the most complexes.
What is the difference between Make and Zapier? expand_more
Make offre a visual interface a lot more avancée that Zapier, with logiques conditionslles, boucles, routeurs and a management d'erreurs intégrée. Zapier is more simple for automations basiques de type "si ceci then cela". Make is généralement more powerful and more economique for the workflows complexes with several branches and conditions. We you recommandons l'outil the most suited to your needs spécifiques.
Combien integrations are available on Make? expand_more
Make propose more than 1500 integrations natives with the applications the most populaires : Google Workspace, Slack, HubSpot, Shopify, WooCommerce, Stripe, Airtable, Notion, Mailchimp and hundreds d'autres. For the applications that does not hasnt not integration native, Make permand créer connexions via HTTP, webhooks and API REST. You pouvez therefore connecter pratiquement any outil à your système d'automation.
How much does it cost the implementation d'a automation Make? expand_more
Our forfait Starter starts at 800 euros for 3 workflows automatises and la connexion de 5 outils. Le forfait Growth a 2 000 euros couvre workflows illimites with integrations avancées and un chatbot IA. For systèmes complexes, we offer solutions Enterprise custom. A this s'ajoute the cost de l'subscription Make lui-meme (starting from 9 euros per month). Le return on investment is visible the first month.
Does Make peut gérer automations complexes? expand_more
Absolument. Make excelle in the workflows complexes. Il has d'iterateurs for handle listes de donnees, d'aggregateurs for consolider informations, de routeurs for créer branches conditionslles multiples and modules de management d'erreurs for garantir la trustworthiness. Un seul scenario Make peut contenir tens d'etapes with branchements multiples and managements d'erreurs sophistiquees. It's l'outil idéal for the automations avancées.
En combien de temps mes automations Make seront-elles operationnelles? expand_more
Un workflow simple — par example un transfert automatique de données between deux outils — is operationnel en 3 a 5 days. Un système complete with several workflows interconnectes, logiques conditionslles avancées and integrations multiples prend 2 a 4 weeks. We proceed par étapes : each workflow is teste and valideally en conditions reelles before de passer au suivant. You have a visibility complète on l'avancement du projet.
