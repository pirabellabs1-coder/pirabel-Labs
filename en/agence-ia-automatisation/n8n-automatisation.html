Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT N8N AUTOMATION

What is N8N and why choose this solution? expand_more
N8N is an open-source automation platform that lets you create complex workflows while maintaining total control over your data. Unlike Make or Zapier, N8N can be hosted on your own servers, which guarantees full GDPR compliance and complete data sovereignty. It is the ideal solution for businesses with strict confidentiality requirements or that want maximum technical flexibility.
What is the difference between N8N and Make or Zapier? expand_more
N8N is open-source and can be self-hosted: your data never passes through third-party servers. It is also much more flexible for developers thanks to the ability to add custom JavaScript code at any step of a workflow. Make and Zapier are cloud solutions that are simpler to get started with but offer less control over your data and less technical flexibility. We recommend the solution best suited to your needs.
Is N8N really free? expand_more
The N8N software is open-source and free if you self-host it. This involves server costs — starting from 5 euros per month for a basic VPS — and regular technical maintenance (updates, backups, security). N8N also offers a paid cloud version for businesses that prefer not to manage the hosting. In both cases, the cost is generally lower than Make or Zapier for intensive use since there is no per-execution billing.
Is N8N suitable for businesses with strict GDPR requirements? expand_more
Absolutely. This is one of the main advantages of N8N compared to other automation solutions. By hosting N8N on your own servers or on servers located in France or Europe, your data never leaves your infrastructure. No data passes through American or third-party servers. It is the ideal solution for regulated sectors: finance, healthcare, legal, and public administration.
Do you need technical skills to use N8N? expand_more
N8N has a drag-and-drop visual interface accessible to non-developers for simple and intermediate workflows. For advanced workflows with custom JavaScript code, internal database connections, or complex API integrations, technical skills are a plus. But rest assured: we handle all installation, configuration, development, and maintenance. Your team does not need to be technical to use the workflows on a daily basis.
How long does it take to deploy N8N and create workflows? expand_more
Installing and configuring N8N on your infrastructure takes 2 to 3 days. The first workflows are operational within the first week. A complete system with complex workflows, custom modules, and multiple integrations takes 2 to 4 weeks. We provide continuous support after deployment to ensure everything works perfectly and to evolve your workflows according to your needs.
