Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT ZAPIER AUTOMATION

What is Zapier and what is it used for? expand_more
Zapier is the most popular no-code automation platform in the world. It lets you connect over 5,000 applications together to automate repetitive tasks without writing a single line of code. Each automation (called a "Zap") works on the trigger-action principle: when an event occurs in one application (a new lead, an email received, a payment made), Zapier automatically executes one or more actions in other applications.
Why choose Zapier over Make or N8N? expand_more
Zapier is the ideal choice if you are looking for simplicity and speed. Its interface is the most intuitive on the market, its catalog of over 5,000 applications is unmatched, and its setup is the fastest. It is perfect for linear automations and businesses that want to get started without technical complexity. If you need more complex visual workflows, Make is better suited. If data sovereignty is a priority, N8N is the solution. We guide you toward the right tool.
How many applications can you connect with Zapier? expand_more
Zapier offers over 5,000 native integrations — the largest catalog on the market. You will find all the popular applications: Gmail, Google Sheets, Slack, HubSpot, Salesforce, Shopify, Stripe, Trello, Notion, Mailchimp, Airtable, Calendly, Typeform, and thousands more. If an application is not available natively, connections via webhooks and APIs are possible.
How much does a Zapier automation cost? expand_more
Our Starter package starts at 800 euros for the implementation of 3 automated workflows, the audit of your processes, and team training. On top of that is the cost of the Zapier subscription: free for 100 tasks per month (perfect for getting started), then starting from 19 euros per month for more intensive usage. The return on investment is generally visible in the first week thanks to the time saved immediately.
How quickly will my Zapier automations be active? expand_more
This is one of the great advantages of Zapier: speed. A simple Zap (for example: new lead in a form → automatic addition to your CRM + Slack notification) is operational in just a few hours. A complete system with multiple interconnected Zaps takes 1 to 2 weeks. It is the fastest solution to deploy among all the automation tools we use.
Is Zapier suitable for large businesses? expand_more
Yes. Zapier offers Team and Enterprise plans with advanced features: team-based access management, shared folders, audit logs, SLA, and premium support. It is perfectly suited to large businesses for standard automations and integrations between tools. For very complex workflows with advanced logic, we recommend combining Zapier with Make or N8N to get the best of each tool.
