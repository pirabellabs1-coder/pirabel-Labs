Frequently Asked Questions

TOUT SAVOIR SUR L'AUTOMATISATION ZAPIER

What is Zapier and a quoi that sert? expand_more
Zapier is la platform d'automation no-code the most populaire au monde. Elle permand connecter more than 5000 applications between elles for automatiser tâches répétitives without ecrire a seule ligne de code. Each automation (appelee "Zap") fonctionne on le principe declencheur-action : when un événement se produit in a application (un nouveau lead, un email reçu, un paiement effectue), Zapier execute automatiquement a or several actions in d'autres applications.
Why choisir Zapier plutot that Make or N8N? expand_more
Zapier is le choix idéal si you cherchez la simplicite and la rapidite. Son interface is the most intuitive du marche, son catalogue de more than 5000 applications is inegalable and sa setup is the most rapide. Il is parfait for the automations lineaires and the businesss that veulent startsr without complexity technique. Si you have need workflows visuels more complexes, Make sera more adapte. Si la souverainete données is a priorite, N8N is la solution. We you guidons towards le bon outil.
Combien d'applications peut-on connecter with Zapier? expand_more
Zapier propose more than 5000 integrations natives — it's the most grand catalogue du marche. You y trouverez all the applications populaires : Gmail, Google Sheets, Slack, HubSpot, Salesforce, Shopify, Stripe, Trello, Notion, Mailchimp, Airtable, Calendly, Typeform and thousands d'autres. Si a application is not disponible nativement, connexions via webhooks and API are possibles.
How much does it cost a automation Zapier? expand_more
Our forfait Starter starts at 800 euros for the implementation de 3 workflows automatises, l'audit de your processesesus and la formation de your team. A cela s'ajoute the cost de l'subscription Zapier : free for 100 tâches per month (parfait for startsr), puis a partir de 19 euros per month for a usage more intensif. Le return on investment is généralement visible the first week thanks to temps gagne immédiatement.
En combien de temps mes automations Zapier seront-elles actives? expand_more
It's l'un grands avantages de Zapier : la vitesse. Un Zap simple (par example : nouveau lead in a formulaire → ajout automatique in your CRM + notification Slack) is operationnel en a few heures. Un système complete with several Zaps interconnectes prend 1 a 2 weeks. It's the solution the most rapide a deployer among all the outils d'automation that we use.
Does Zapier convient aux grandes businesss? expand_more
Oui. Zapier propose plans Team and Enterprise with features avancées : management acces par équipe, dossiers partages, journal d'audit, SLA and support premium. Il convient parfaitement aux grandes businesss for the automations standard and the integrations between outils. For workflows tres complexes with logiques avancées, we recommend de combiner Zapier with Make or N8N for tirer le meilleur de each outil.
