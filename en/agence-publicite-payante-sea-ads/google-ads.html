The problem

ARE YOUR GOOGLE ADS CAMPAIGNS COSTING MORE THAN THEY RETURN?

If you recognise yourself in any of these situations, it is time to hand your campaigns to experts.

money_off

Your Quality Score is in free fall

Your ads have a Quality Score of 4 or 5, which means you pay double the cost per click compared to a competitor with a score of 8 or 9. Your keywords don't match your ads, your landing pages aren't optimised, and Google penalises you by increasing your costs. Every Quality Score point lost is money wasted.

search_off

You appear on the wrong keywords

You spend hundreds of euros on queries that have no relation to your offering. Your broad match keywords attract unqualified traffic, your negative keywords are nonexistent, and your budget is diluted across terms that never convert. The result: clicks without clients and an exploding CPA.

conversion_path_off

Your tracking is incomplete or broken

Your conversions don't report correctly in Google Ads. The pixel is improperly installed, conversions are counted twice or not at all, and Smart Bidding algorithms optimise on faulty data. Without reliable tracking, all optimisation is impossible and you make decisions blindly.

trending_down

Your ROAS is stagnating or declining

Your campaigns have been running for months but performance is no longer improving. The same budget generates fewer and fewer results, your cost per click is rising, and you don't know how to restart the machine. Creative fatigue, lack of testing and absence of daily optimisation are killing your performance slowly.

Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING YOU WANT TO KNOW ABOUT GOOGLE ADS

What minimum budget is needed for Google Ads? expand_more
We recommend a minimum budget of 1,000 euros per month on Google Ads to obtain enough data and allow the Smart Bidding algorithms to optimise your bids. With this budget, we can already generate concrete results by targeting the most profitable keywords in your sector. For highly competitive markets (insurance, real estate, legal), a higher budget is often necessary to obtain a significant click volume.
How long does it take to see results on Google Ads? expand_more
The first clicks and conversions arrive within 24 to 48 hours after launch. Real optimisation of bids and Quality Score takes 2 to 4 weeks of data collection. From the second month, campaigns reach their full potential with steadily improving ROAS. Google Ads is the fastest channel to generate leads and sales because you target people who are actively searching for what you offer.
What is the difference between Search, Shopping and Performance Max? expand_more
Search campaigns display text ads when a user types a query into Google. Google Shopping displays your products with photo, price and reviews directly in search results — ideal for e-commerce. Performance Max combines all Google channels (Search, Shopping, Display, YouTube, Gmail, Discover, Maps) in a single campaign driven by Google's artificial intelligence. We often recommend a mix of all three to maximise your coverage and return on investment.
Are you certified Google Partner? expand_more
Yes. Pirabel Labs is a certified Google Partner agency. This means our teams have passed the official Google Ads certifications, we meet the performance standards required by Google and we manage a significant advertising budget volume. This certification gives us access to beta features before their general rollout, priority technical support from Google and exclusive insights on search trends.
How do you handle conversion tracking? expand_more
We install complete tracking via Google Tag Manager: Google Ads conversion tags, Google Analytics 4 with custom events, Enhanced Conversions for precise tracking even with cookie restrictions, and offline conversions if you have a long sales cycle. Every valuable action is tracked so that Smart Bidding algorithms have the most reliable data possible to optimise your bids.
Can I keep my Google Ads account if I end the collaboration? expand_more
Absolutely. Your Google Ads account belongs to you 100%. We work directly on your account, not on a proprietary MCC account. If you decide to end our collaboration, you keep everything: campaigns, historical data, remarketing audiences, accumulated Quality Score and all optimisation performed. No lock-in, no hidden clauses.
