Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING YOU WANT TO KNOW ABOUT PAID ADVERTISING

What is the minimum budget needed to launch advertising campaigns? expand_more
We recommend a minimum advertising budget of 1,000 euros per month to obtain significant data and optimize your campaigns effectively. Below this threshold, the algorithms do not have enough data volume to learn and optimize bids. That said, we always adapt our approach to your budget: even with 1,000 euros, we can generate concrete results by focusing on a single platform and very precise targeting.
How long does it take to see results with paid advertising? expand_more
Unlike SEO, the first results are almost immediate. The first conversions generally arrive within 48 to 72 hours after launch. Real ROAS optimization takes 2 to 4 weeks of data collection. From the second month, campaigns reach their cruising speed and performance continually improves thanks to daily optimization.
Which advertising platform should I choose for my business? expand_more
It depends on your target audience and your offer. Google Ads is ideal for capturing existing demand — people actively searching for your products or services. Meta Ads excels at creating demand and retargeting. LinkedIn Ads is essential in B2B for targeting decision-makers. TikTok Ads offers very low costs for reaching 18-35 year olds. During our initial audit, we analyze your market to recommend the optimal mix.
How do you measure the ROI of my campaigns? expand_more
We set up complete tracking from the first week: conversion pixel on all platforms, UTM on every link, Google Analytics 4 configured, and a real-time dashboard. You see exactly how much every euro spent brings you in theads, sales and revenue. No vanity metrics — we focus on the numbers that matter for your business.
Why not manage my campaigns myself? expand_more
You can, but advertising platforms evolve constantly. A certified expert optimizes your bids, your audiences and your creatives daily. Our clients save on average 45% on their CPA compared to non-specialized in-house management. The time you would spend managing your campaigns is better invested in your core business.
Do you also handle the creation of ad visuals and videos? expand_more
Yes. Our creative team produces static visuals, short videos, carousels and optimized ad copy for each platform. High-performing creatives are the key to profitable campaigns. We continuously test new variants to identify what resonates best with your audience.
What happens if my campaigns are not profitable? expand_more
If after 30 days of optimization your campaigns show no positive trajectory, we completely re-evaluate the strategy at no extra charge. Our 98% retention rate proves that this almost never happens. We commit contractually to clear and measurable performance objectives.
Can I keep control of my advertising accounts? expand_more
Absolutely. Your advertising accounts belong to you 100%. We work on your own Google Ads, Meta Business Manager accounts, etc. You have full access at all times. If you decideally to end our collaboration, you keep everything: the accounts, the data, the history, the audiences.
