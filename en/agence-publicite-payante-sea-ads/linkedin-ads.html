The problem

IS YOUR B2B LEAD GENERATION STALLING?

If you recognize yourself in any of these situations, LinkedIn Ads could transform your sales pipeline.

person_off

Your leads are not the right stakeholders

You generate leads via Google or Facebook, but when your sales team calls them, they are not the decision-makers. They are interns, curious browsers or people without purchasing power. Your sales team wastes hours qualifying contacts that lead nowhere. On LinkedIn, you target the director, the CEO or the purchasing manager directly.

Your B2B sales cycle is too long

Your prospects take months to make a decision because they do not know you when your sales rep reaches out. Without a prior presence in their professional feed, you start from zero at each outreach. LinkedIn Ads lets you nurture your prospects with valuable content before they even speak to your team, considerably shortening the sales cycle.

Your B2B CPA has become unsustainable

You tried LinkedIn Ads on your own and the cost per lead was astronomical. Your campaigns targeted too broadly, your creatives were not adapted to the professional audience, and you had no nurturing strategy. LinkedIn CPA is naturally higher than on other platforms, but with the right approach, the cost per signed client is often the lowest in the B2B market.

You cannot reach enterprise accounts

You want to sign contracts with mid-market or enterprise companies, but you cannot reach the decision-makers at those organizations. Cold emails end up in spam, phone calls never get past the front desk. LinkedIn Ads with an ABM (Account-Based Marketing) approach lets you target decision-makers at named companies and build a relationship before even the first outreach.

Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING YOU WANT TO KNOW ABOUT LINKEDIN ADS

What is the minimum budget for LinkedIn Ads? expand_more
LinkedIn is the most expensive advertising platform in terms of CPC, but also the one that generates the highest quality B2B leads. We recommend a minimum budget of 1,500 euros per month to obtain sufficient data and optimize your campaigns effectively. With this budget, we can precisely target your ideal decision-makers and generate between 15 and 30 qualified leads per month depending on your sector and audience competitiveness. For ABM strategies targeting enterprise accounts, we recommend a minimum of 2,500 euros.
Is LinkedIn Ads profitable despite the high CPCs? expand_more
Yes, if you sell B2B with an average deal value above 1,000 euros. The CPC on LinkedIn is on average 5 to 10 times more expensive than on Facebook, but the lead quality is incomparable. You target decision-makers by exact job title, company size and industry — impossible on any other platform. A LinkedIn lead has a 2 to 3 times higher client conversion rate than a Facebook lead for B2B offers. When you calculate the cost per signed client (not the cost per lead), LinkedIn is often the most profitable platform for B2B.
What ad formats do you use on LinkedIn? expand_more
We primarily use four formats depending on your objectives. Sponsored Content (sponsored posts in the feed) for brand awareness and lead generation — it is the most versatile format. Lead Gen Forms that collect contact details directly within LinkedIn without a landing page, with a conversion rate 2 to 3 times higher than standard forms. Message Ads (sponsored InMail) for direct and personalized contact with decision-makers — ideal for premium offers. And Dynamic Ads for personalized retargeting at scale.
How do you target decision-makers on LinkedIn? expand_more
This is LinkedIn Ads' unique strength and the reason this platform is essential for B2B. We target by exact job title (CEO, Marketing Director, CTO, CFO), by company size (1-50, 51-200, 201-1000, 1001+ employees), by industry, by seniority level (junior, senior, director, VP, C-level), by declared skills and even by named company. For ABM strategies, we import lists of target companies to reach only the strategic accounts you want to convert. This level of precision is impossible on Google or Meta.
What is ABM (Account-Based Marketing) on LinkedIn? expand_more
Account-Based Marketing involves targeting a limited number of strategic companies identified as your ideal clients, rather than aiming at a broad audience. On LinkedIn, we import your list of target accounts (10, 50, 200 companies) and deliver personalized content to decision-makers at those specific companies. It is the most effective approach for long sales cycles and high-value contracts. We combine this with multi-stage nurturing content to build trust before the commercial outreach.
Do you also create content for my LinkedIn campaigns? expand_more
Yes. Our team produces visuals, videos, document carousels, infographics and ad copy tailored to LinkedIn's professional audience. The tone is fundamentally different from Facebook or TikTok: here, content must provide value, demonstrate your expertise and build trust. We create data-driven case studies, industry infographics, client video testimonials and thought leadership content that position your brand as a reference in your field. Each creative is tested and iterated to maximize engagement and conversion.
