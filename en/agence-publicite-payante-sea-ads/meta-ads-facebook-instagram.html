The problem

YOUR META ADS CAMPAIGNS NOT TAKING OFF?

If you recognize yourself in any of these situations, your Meta Ads strategy needs a serious boost.

block

Your creatives are no longer performing

The same visuals have been running for weeks, your audience has seen them dozens of times and your CPM keeps increasing. Creative fatigue is the silent killer of Meta Ads campaigns. Without constant renewal of your creatives, performance inevitably drops and your cost per acquisition skyrockets.

groups_off

Your targeting has become blurry

Since the iOS 14.5 changes, your interest-based targeting no longer works like before. Your audiences are too broad or too narrow, your lookalikes no longer resemble your best customers, and you are not leveraging the new Advantage+ features that let the algorithm find the right people for you.

visibility_off

Your pixel is losing conversions

With third-party cookie blocking and tracking restrictions, your Meta pixel only captures a fraction of actual conversions. The algorithm optimizes on incomplete data, which degrades your performance day after day. Without the Conversion API (CAPI), you are flying blind and your ROAS is underestimated.

shopping_cart_off

You are not retargeting your visitors

97% of your website visitors do not convert on the first visit. If you are not retargeting them with messages adapted to their level of engagement, you are letting potential clients walk away who were on the verge of buying. Retargeting is the most profitable lever in Meta Ads, and you are not leveraging it.

Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING YOU WANT TO KNOW ABOUT META ADS

What is the minimum budget to launch Meta Ads? expand_more
We recommend a minimum of 1,000 euros per month to allow the Meta algorithm to exit the learning phase and optimize your campaigns effectively. With this budget, we can test 2 to 3 audiences and several creatives to quickly identify the winning combinations. For e-commerce businesses with a product catalogue, we recommend a minimum of 1,500 euros to feed Advantage+ Shopping campaigns.
Is Facebook Ads still profitable in 2026? expand_more
Absolutely. Despite iOS changes and algorithm evolutions, Meta remains the most powerful advertising platform for demand creation and retargeting. The key is to adapt your strategy to new realities: short video creatives, Advantage+ campaigns that leverage Meta AI, and the Conversion API for reliable tracking. Our clients achieve an average ROAS of 3.5 on Meta Ads. The platform is far from dead — it has simply changed the rules.
What is the difference between Facebook Ads and Instagram Ads? expand_more
Facebook and Instagram are part of the same Meta ecosystem and are managed from the same Business Manager. The difference resides in the formats and audiences. Instagram is more visual, with Stories, Reels and carousel formats that perform very well among 18-40 year olds. Facebook offers a broader and more diverse audience, with formats like Lead Ads and groups that are very effective. We generally recommend broadcasting on both platforms and letting the Advantage+ algorithm optimize distribution automatically.
How do you create the visuals and videos for my campaigns? expand_more
Our creative team produces static visuals, short videos of 15 to 30 seconds, product carousels and UGC-style content (user generated content). Each creative is designed to stop the scroll and trigger action, with a visual hook in the first 3 seconds. We continuously test new variants — angles, formats, messages — to identify what resonates best with your audience and combat creative fatigue.
How does retargeting work on Meta? expand_more
Meta retargeting allows you to re-target people who have already interacted with your brand: website visitors, people who watched more than 50% of your videos, engaged with your posts, or abandoned their cart. We create multi-step retargeting sequences with messages adapted to each level of engagement. For example: client testimonial for site visitors, special offer for cart abandoners, reminder for clients who purchased 90 days ago. The conversion rate from retargeting is 3 to 5 times higher than that of cold acquisition.
What is Meta Conversion API and why is it important? expand_more
The Conversion API (CAPI) sends conversion data directly from your server to Meta servers, complementing the browser pixel. With iOS restrictions, ad blockers and third-party cookie blocking, the pixel alone only captures 60 to 70% of actual conversions. The CAPI fills this gap and ensures that the Meta algorithm has complete and reliable data to optimize your campaigns. We install it systematically for all our clients, as it is an essential prerequisite for high-performance campaigns in 2026.
