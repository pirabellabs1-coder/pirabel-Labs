Frequently Asked Questions

TOUT CE QUE VOUS VOULEZ SAVOIR SUR META ADS

Quel budget minimum for lancer Meta Ads? expand_more
We recommandons un minimum de 1 000 euros per month for allowstre a l'algorithme Meta de sortir de la phase d'apprentissage and d'optimize your campaigns effectively. With ce budget, we pouvons tester 2 a 3 audiences and several creatives for identifier quickly the combinaisons gagnantes. For the e-commerces with a catalogue de produits, we recommend 1 500 euros minimum for alimenter the campagnes Advantage+ Shopping.
Facebook Ads est-il still rentable in 2026? expand_more
Absolument. Malgre the changements iOS and the evolutions de l'algorithme, Meta reste la platforme publicitaire the most puissante for the creation de demande and le retargeting. La clé is d'adapter sa strategy aux nouvelles realites : creatives vidéo shorts, campagnes Advantage+ that exploitent l'IA de Meta, and API conversion rates for a tracking fiable. Our clients obtiennent on average un ROAS de 3.5 on Meta Ads. La platforme is loin d'be morte, elle a simplement change de règles.
What is the difference between Facebook Ads and Instagram Ads? expand_more
Facebook and Instagram font partie du same ecosysteme Meta and se gerent since le same Business Manager. La différence reside in the formats and the audiences. Instagram is more visuel, with formats Stories, Reels and carrousels tres performants aupres 18-40 ans. Facebook offre a audience more large and diversifiee, with formats like the Lead Ads and the groupes tres efficaces. We recommandons généralement de diffuser on the deux platformes and laisser l'algorithme Advantage+ optimize la repartition automatiquement.
Comment créez-you the visuels and vidéos for mes campagnes? expand_more
Our team creative produit visuels statiques, vidéos shorts de 15 a 30 secondes, carrousels produits and contenus UGC-style (user generated content). Each creative is designed for arreter le scroll and trigger l'action, with a hook visuel in the 3 premières secondes. We testons continuellement de nouvelles variantes — angles, formats, messages — for identifier what resonne le mieux with your audience and combattre la fatigue creative.
How does ... work le retargeting on Meta? expand_more
Le retargeting Meta permand recibler the personnes that have already interagi with your brand : visiteurs de your website web, personnes ayant regarde your vidéos a more than 50%, engage with your publications, or abandonne their panier. We create sequences de retargeting en several étapes with messages adaptés a each niveau d'engagement. Par example : temoignage client for the visiteur du site, offre speciale for l'abandon de panier, rappel for the client that a achete il y a 90 days. Le conversion rate du retargeting is 3 a 5 fois superieur a celui de l'acquisition froide.
What is API conversion rates Meta and pourquoi est-ce important? expand_more
L'API conversion rates (CAPI) envoie the données conversion rate directly de your serveur aux serveurs de Meta, en complement du pixel navigateur. With the restrictions iOS, the bloqueurs de publicites and le blocage cookies tiers, le pixel seul ne capte more that 60 a 70% conversions reelles. La CAPI comble ce manque and garantit that l'algorithme Meta dispose de données completees and fiables for optimize your campaigns. We l'installons systematiquement for all our clients, car c'est un prerequis indispensable for campagnes high-performance in 2026.
