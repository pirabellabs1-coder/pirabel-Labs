Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING YOU WANT TO KNOW ABOUT TIKTOK ADS

Is TikTok Ads suitable for my business? expand_more
TikTok is no longer reserved for teenagers. In 2026, 67% of users are between 18 and 44 years old, with real purchasing power. If your target audience includes the 18-45 age group, TikTok Ads is a channel worth testing seriously. The sectors that perform best are e-commerce (fashion, beauty, food), online training, mobile apps, consumer services and even B2B with educational content. We analyze your market during the free audit to tell you if TikTok Ads makes sense for your business.
What is the minimum budget for TikTok Ads? expand_more
TikTok Ads is one of the most accessible platforms in terms of budget. We recommend a minimum of 800 euros per month to test effectively and allow the algorithm to optimize. CPMs on TikTok are 2 to 3 times cheaper than on Meta, which means your budget goes a lot further in terms of reach and impressions. With 800 euros, you can already reach hundreds of thousands of people and generate significant conversions.
Is it necessary to create content specifically for TikTok? expand_more
Yes, absolutely. It's the number one rule. The ads that perform on TikTok don't look like traditional ads. They look like organic content: authentic, spontaneous, filmed on a smartphone, with a hook in the first 2 seconds that captures attention. We create UGC-style content (User Generated Content) specifically designed for the TikTok feed. Polished corporate formats from Facebook or Google don't work here and will be ignored by the algorithm.
What are Spark Ads and why are they effective? expand_more
Spark Ads allow you to boost existing organic content — whether it's yours or a content creator's — as paid advertising. The advantage is twofold: the content retains its native authenticity in the feed, and all interactions (likes, comments, shares) accumulate on the original post, reinforcing its social credibility. Spark Ads have a 142% higher engagement rate than standard In-Feed Ads and a 30% lower cost per view. It's one of the most powerful formats in TikTok Ads.
How do you measure conversions on TikTok? expand_more
We install the TikTok Pixel on your website, configured via Google Tag Manager, to track each conversion event: page view, add to cart, checkout initiation, purchase, registration. We also configure the Events API (the server-side equivalent of the pixel) for server-side tracking that compensates for cookie limitations and ad blockers. Every valuable action is tracked and reported in the TikTok Ads Manager dashboard, with detailed weekly reporting.
Can you manage TikTok Shop for my e-commerce? expand_more
Yes. TikTok Shop allows selling directly within the app without the user ever leaving TikTok, reducing friction and increasing conversion rates. We configure your TikTok Shop, synchronize your product catalog, and create Video Shopping Ads campaigns that showcase your products in a native and engaging format. For e-commerce brands, TikTok Shop is an extremely high-performing sales channel with conversion rates often exceeding the standard website journey.
