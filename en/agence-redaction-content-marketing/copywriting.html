The problem

WHY YOUR ADS ARE NOT PERFORMING

You dépensez du budget publicitaire but the results ne suivent pas. These erreurs de copywriting plombent your performance.

visibility_off

Your accroches are generiques and n'arretent personne

In un fil d'actualite sature, you have moins de 3 secondes for capter l'attention. Si your accroche ressemble a celles de all your competitors, your audience scrolle without same la remarquer. A accroche faible, it's du budget publicitaire jete by the fenetre.

ads_click

Your annonces manquent de clarte and direction

Your message is confus, your proposition de valeur floue, your appel a l'action faible. Le lecteur does not understand what you proposez nor why il devrait cliquer. A annonce efficace dit exactly quoi, for that and why en a few mots.

mail

Your emails are not ouverts and still moins cliques

Your email subject lines are ennuyeux, your textes trop longs, your CTA invisibles. Your liste email is un actif precieux that you under-exploitez. Each email non ouvert is a opportunité de vente perdue, and each email non clique is un prospect that refroidit.

content_copy

You utilisez le same texte partout

Your annonce Google ressemble à your post LinkedIn that ressemble à your email. Each canal a ses propres codes, ses propres contraintes and ses propres audiences. Un texte that fonctionne on Google Ads sera un echec total on TikTok. L'adaptation is la cle.

Frequently Asked Questions

YOUR QUESTIONS ABOUT COPYWRITING PUBLICITAIRE

What is the difference between le copywriting publicitaire and la writing classique? expand_more
Le copywriting publicitaire a un seul objectif : declenexpensive a action immédiate chez le lecteur. Cliquer on a annonce, ouvrir un email, s'inscrire a a offre, buy un produit. Each mot is choisi for sa capacité a persuader in a format court and contraint. La writing classique informe and eduque on un sujet ; le copywriting vend and pousse a l'action. The deux are complémentaires in a strategy marketing completee.
Combien de variantes d'annonces livrez-you? expand_more
We deliver systematiquement 3 a 5 variantes par annonce for allowstre l'A/B testing en conditions reelles. Each variante explore un angle de communication, a accroche or un appel a l'action different. The données de performance guident then l'optimisation : we renforcons what fonctionne and remplasons what does not performe pas. Ce process iteratif ameliore your results month after month.
Ecrivez-you also sequences email? expand_more
Oui, the sequences email conversion rate are l'a de our specialites. We create sequences de bienvenue for your nouveaux abonnes, sequences de nurturing for éduquer your prospects, sequences de relance panier abandonne for recuperer ventes, sequences de launch produit and sequences de reengagement for your contacts inactifs. Each email is designed for guider le destinataire towards l'etape suivante du parcours d'achat.
What is the delai de livraison for du copywriting publicitaire? expand_more
Le copywriting publicitaire is our service the most rapide. The annonces Google Ads and Social Ads are delivereds under 48 a 72 heures. The sequences email completees under 5 a 7 days ouvrables. The scripts vidéo under 3 a 5 days. Each livraison inclut deux rounds of revisions for affiner le ton and the messages according to your feedback. We offer also options express for the launchs urgents.
Comment adaptez-you le ton a our marque? expand_more
We start par a analyse approfondie de your voix de marque existante : your communications actuelles, your website web, your social media. We etudions also your competitors and your target audience. A partir de this analyse, we define ensemble un guide de ton that sert de référence for toute la production copywriting. Each texte is then written and valide par rapport a ce guide for garantir la coherence.
Travaillez-you on all the platforms publicitaires? expand_more
Oui, we maîtrisons the formats and contraintes spécifiques de each platform : Google Ads (Search, Display, YouTube, Performance Max), Meta Ads (Facebook and Instagram), LinkedIn Ads, TikTok Ads and Twitter/X Ads. Each platform a ses propres codes, ses propres limites de caracteres and ses propres bonnes pratiques.
