The problem

WHY YOUR ADS ARE NOT PERFORMING

You are spending ad budget but the results are not following. These copywriting mistakes are dragging down your performance.

visibility_off

Your hooks are generic and stop nobody

In a saturated news feed, you have less than 3 seconds to capture attention. If your hook looks like everyone else's, your audience scrolls past without even noticing it. A weak hook means ad budget thrown out the window.

ads_click

Your ads lack clarity and direction

Your message is confusing, your value proposition vague, your call to action weak. The reader does not understand what you are offering or why they should click. An effective ad says exactly what, for whom, and why in just a few words.

mail

Your emails are not opened, let alone clicked

Your email subject lines are boring, your copy is too long, your CTAs invisible. Your email list is a precious asset you are under-exploiting. Every unopened email is a lost sales opportunity, and every unclicked email is a prospect growing cold.

content_copy

You use the same copy everywhere

Your Google ad looks like your LinkedIn post which looks like your email. Each channel has its own codes, its own constraints, and its own audiences. Copy that works on Google Ads will be a total failure on TikTok. Adaptation is key.

Frequently Asked Questions

YOUR QUESTIONS ABOUT ADVERTISING COPYWRITING

What is the difference between advertising copywriting and traditional writing? expand_more
Advertising copywriting has one single objective: to trigger an immediate action from the reader. Click on an ad, open an email, sign up for an offer, buy a product. Every word is chosen for its ability to persuade within a short and constrained format. Traditional writing informs and educates on a subject; copywriting sells and pushes to action. The two are complementary within a complete marketing strategy.
How many ad variants do you deliver? expand_more
We systematically deliver 3 to 5 variants per ad to enable A/B testing under real conditions. Each variant explores a different communication angle, hook, or call to action. Performance data then guides the optimization: we reinforce what works and replace what does not perform. This iterative process improves your results month after month.
Do you also write email sequences? expand_more
Yes, high-conversion email sequences are one of our specialties. We create welcome sequences for your new subscribers, nurturing sequences to educate your prospects, abandoned cart sequences to recover sales, product launch sequences, and re-engagement sequences for your inactive contacts. Each email is designed to guide the recipient toward the next step in the purchase journey.
What is the delivery time for advertising copywriting? expand_more
Advertising copywriting is our fastest service. Google Ads and Social Ads are delivered within 48 to 72 hours. Complete email sequences within 5 to 7 business days. Video scripts within 3 to 5 days. Each delivery includes two rounds of revisions to refine the tone and messages based on your feedback. We also offer express options for urgent launches.
How do you adapt the tone to our brand? expand_more
We start with a thorough analysis of your existing brand voice: your current communications, your website, your social media. We also study your competitors and your target audience. Based on this analysis, we define together a tone guide that serves as a reference for all copywriting production. Each text is then written and validated against this guide to ensure consistency.
Do you work on all advertising platforms? expand_more
Yes, we master the formats and specific constraints of each platform: Google Ads (Search, Display, YouTube, Performance Max), Meta Ads (Facebook and Instagram), LinkedIn Ads, TikTok Ads, and Twitter/X Ads. Each platform has its own codes, its own character limits, and its own best practices. We know these specifics and exploit them to maximize your performance on each channel.
