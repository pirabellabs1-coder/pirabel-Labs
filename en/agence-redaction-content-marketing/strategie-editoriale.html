Frequently Asked Questions

YOUR QUESTIONS ABOUT EDITORIAL STRATEGY

What is a strategy éditoriale exactly? expand_more
A strategy éditoriale is le plan directeur de everything your content. Elle definit precisement a that you parlez (personas détaillés), what you dites (piliers de contenu and cocons sémantiques), comment you le dites (voix and ton de marque), when you le publiez (calendrier éditorial) and comment you mesurez the results (KPI). C'est la différence fondamentale between publier du contenu au hasard and construire methodiquement a machine a traffic and a leads.
How long does it take to construire a strategy éditoriale? expand_more
Comptez 2 a 4 weeks for a strategy éditoriale completee. La première week is consacree a l'audit de your content existant and a la recherche concurrentielle. La deuxieme a la definition personas and la voix de marque. The weeks suivantes au calendrier éditorial and a la finalisation livrables. You are implique a each étape de validation for garantir that la strategy correspond à your vision.
Que contient le livrable de strategy éditoriale? expand_more
You receive un document operationnel complete comprenant : l'audit détaillé de your content existant with recommandations, the fiches personas (3 a 5 personas with demographics, douleurs, objectifs), la charte de voix de marque with examples, the piliers de contenu and cocons sémantiques, le calendrier éditorial on 3 a 12 month with each sujet, mot-cle cible, format and objectif, and le tableau de bord KPI a suivre.
Is it necessary to avoir du contenu existant for get started? expand_more
Non, not du everything. We build strategys editorials also bien for businesss that partent de zero that for celles that have already hundreds d'articles a optimize. Si you have du contenu existant, we commencons by a audit complete that identifie what fonctionne and what doit be ameliore. Si you partez de zero, we build la strategy a partir de l'analyse de your market, de your competitors and your objectives business.
A quelle fréquence le calendrier éditorial est-il mis up to date? expand_more
Le calendrier initial couvre 3 a 12 month according to your formule. Il is revu and ajuste every month based on performance contenus publies, tendances emergentes and nouvelles opportunites SEO identifiees. The feedback de your audience, the données analytics and l'evolution de your market alimentent these mises up to date régulieres. Le calendrier is un document vivant that s'adapte permanently.
Does you executez also la strategy or juste la conception? expand_more
The deux options are possibles. We pouvons concevoir la strategy and you former for l'executer en interne with your teams, or prendre en charge l'execution complète : writing articles, creation contenus, publication in your CMS and suivi performance. La plupart de our clients choisissent l'support complete car il garantit la coherence and la quality over time. You gardez le controle total on la validation.
