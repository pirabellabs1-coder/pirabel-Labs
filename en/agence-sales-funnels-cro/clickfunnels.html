Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT CLICKFUNNELS

What is ClickFunnels? expand_more
ClickFunnels is the world-leading platform for creating online sales funnels. Founded by Russell Brunson, it allows you to build sales pages, capture pages, follow-up sequences and payment systems without technical skills. ClickFunnels 2.0 also integrates CRM, email marketing and online course management.
What is the difference between ClickFunnels and Systeme.io? expand_more
ClickFunnels is more powerful in terms of advanced funnel features: native A/B testing, smart funnels, advanced third-party integrations. Systeme.io is more affordable and simpler. If you have an established business that generates revenue and you want to optimize each stage of your funnel, ClickFunnels is the best choice.
How much does ClickFunnels cost? expand_more
ClickFunnels 2.0 offers plans starting from 147 dollars per month. The Funnel Hacker plan at 297 dollars per month offers unlimited funnels, integrated CRM and advanced features. The investment is higher than other tools but the ROI is proportional for serious businesses that generate revenue.
Can you do A/B testing with ClickFunnels? expand_more
Yes, A/B testing is one of ClickFunnels' strongest features. You can test different versions of your pages — headlines, images, buttons, colors, prices, offers — and let the data decide which version converts best. We set up systematic tests to optimize each stage of your funnel and maximize your ROI.
