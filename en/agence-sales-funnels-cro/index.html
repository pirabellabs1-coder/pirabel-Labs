The problem

DOES THIS SOUND FAMILIAR?

If you recognize yourself in any of these situations, you are losing money every day.

traffic

Traffic, but zero conversion

You have visitors. You may even have a lot. But they arrive on your website and leave without doing anything. No purchase, no filled form, no contact. Your traffic costs money but generates nothing. It is like having a busy store where nobody ever pays at the register.

exit_to_app

Bounce rate is exploding

Your visitors arrive and leave within seconds. Your homepage or service pages fail to retain anyone. The message is unclear, the value proposition gets lost, and the call-to-action is invisible. Every bounce is a potential customer you will never see again.

remove_shopping_cart

Massive cart abandonment

Your customers add products to the cart, start the checkout process... and disappear. Your checkout is too long, too complex, or fails to inspire trust. Every abandoned cart represents revenue lost while the customer was actually ready to pay.

edit_off

Forms nobody fills out

Your contact or quote form stays desperately empty. Too many fields, untrustworthy design, or simply poorly placed on the page. Your prospects want to contact you, but your form discourages them before they even start filling it out.

hourglass_top

Pages that load too slowly

Each additional second of loading time scares away 7% of your visitors. Your landing page takes 4 seconds to display? You have already lost a quarter of your audience before they even see your offer. Speed is not a technical detail — it is a direct conversion lever.

route

No structured customer journey

Your visitors arrive on your website without knowing where to go. No sales funnel, no logical sequence, no follow-up. You hope they find the path to purchase on their own. Without a customer journey, you let chance decide your conversions.

Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING YOU WANT TO KNOW ABOUT CRO

What is CRO and why is it important? expand_more
CRO (Conversion Rate Optimization) means improving the percentage of visitors who complete a desired action on your website: purchase, signup, quote request. It is important because doubling your conversion rate doubles your revenue without spending an extra euro on advertising. You extract more value from the traffic you already have.
How long does it take to see CRO results? expand_more
First A/B testing results are visible within 2 to 4 weeks, depending on your traffic volume. A complete CRO strategy shows significant gains within 2 to 3 months. The advantage of CRO over SEO is that improvements are immediate: once a test wins, you benefit from the gain on 100% of your traffic.
What is the difference between a landing page and a regular page? expand_more
A landing page is designed with one single conversion goal: no distracting navigation menu, no outbound links, a clear message and a unique call-to-action. A regular page has multiple objectives and offers many navigation options. This extreme focus is what makes landing pages dramatically more effective — on average 2 to 5x higher conversion rates than a standard page.
How does A/B testing work? expand_more
A/B testing means creating two versions of a page or element (title, button, image, form) and sending half the traffic to each. We measure which converts best with statistically reliable data. When we have a clear winner, we deploy the better version and launch a new test. It is a cycle of continuous improvement based on evidence, not opinions.
What is a sales funnel? expand_more
A sales funnel is a structured journey that guides your visitors step-by-step toward the purchase. Instead of letting them navigate randomly on your website, you accompany them with content, offers and reminders adapted to each stage of their decision: discovery, consideration, decision. Result: higher conversion rates, higher average order value and more satisfied customers.
What budget is needed to get started with CRO? expand_more
Our Starter package at 800 euros per month is ideal to begin. It includes the conversion audit, two optimized landing pages and a first A/B testing cycle. The return on investment is often visible from the first month thanks to immediate conversion gains. Unlike SEO which takes time, CRO generates results quickly.
Does CRO work for small businesses? expand_more
Absolutely. CRO is even more impactful for small businesses because every visitor matters more. If you receive 500 visitors per month and move from 1% to 3% conversion, you triple your customers without increasing your marketing budget. It is one of the most profitable levers for businesses with limited budgets.
Which tools do you use for CRO? expand_more
We use professional tools like Google Analytics 4 for data analysis, Hotjar for heatmaps and session recordings, Google Optimize and VWO for A/B testing, plus advanced tracking solutions for multi-channel attribution. All tools are configured to respect GDPR and protect your visitors' data.
