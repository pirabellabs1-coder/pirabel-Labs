Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT KAJABI

What is Kajabi? expand_more
Kajabi is a premium all-in-one platform designed for content creators, trainers, coaches and entrepreneurs who want to sell digital products online. It integrates course creation, website hosting, sales funnels, email marketing, CRM and payments in a single elegant and powerful interface.
Is Kajabi suitable for French-speaking trainers? expand_more
Yes. Although the admin interface is in English, all content visible to your clients can be entirely in French. Sales pages, emails, membership area, courses — everything can be customized in the language of your choice. We configure everything in French for you and your audience.
How much does Kajabi cost? expand_more
Kajabi offers plans starting from 149 dollars per month for the Basic plan. The Growth plan at 199 dollars per month and the Pro plan at 399 dollars per month offer more products, pipelines and contacts. It is a premium investment but it replaces 5 to 7 different tools.
Can you migrate to Kajabi from another platform? expand_more
Absolutely. We handle the complete migration from Teachable, Thinkific, WordPress, Systeme.io or any other platform. We transfer your courses, your contacts, your email sequences and your sales pages. The transition is seamless, without any data loss and without service interruption.
