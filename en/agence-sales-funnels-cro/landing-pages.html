The Problem

WHY YOUR PAGES DON'T CONVERT

Do any of these situations sound familiar? Every day without an optimized landing page, you're losing customers.

visibility_off

Your message gets lost

Your homepage tries to say everything to everyone. Too many menus, too many links, too many options. Your visitor arrives with a specific intent, but ends up overwhelmed by information. In 3 seconds, they're confused. In 5 seconds, they're gone. Your value proposition never comes through clearly, and your call to action is invisible amid the noise.

smartphone

Your mobile experience is catastrophic

Over 60% of your traffic comes from mobile, but your page isn't optimised for small screens. Buttons are too small, text is unreadable, forms are impossible to fill out with a thumb. You're losing the majority of your visitors before they can even read your offer. Every extra second of friction on mobile drives your prospects away.

speed

Your page loads too slowly

Every additional loading second drives away 7% of your visitors. Your landing page takes more than 3 seconds to display? You've already lost a quarter of your audience before they even see your offer. Unoptimized images, heavy code and unnecessary scripts turn your advertising investment into pure waste.

edit_off

No social proof or trust signals

Your page contains no testimonials, no client logos, no concrete figures. Your visitor doesn't know you and has no reason to trust you. Without social proof, your page looks like an empty promise. Visitors hesitate, compare, and end up going to a competitor who displays concrete results and verified reviews.

Frequently Asked Questions

YOUR QUESTIONS ABOUT OUR LANDING PAGES

How much does a landing page cost? expand_more
The price varies based on complexity and features. Our Starter plan includes 2 optimized landing pages from €800 per month. Each page is custom-designed with persuasive copywriting, conversion-focused design and complete tracking. Return on investment is generally visible from the first month thanks to immediate conversion gains.
What is the difference between a landing page and a regular website page? expand_more
A landing page has a single conversion objective. No navigation menu, no outbound links, a clear message and a single call to action. A regular page offers multiple paths and distracts the visitor. This focus makes landing pages 2 to 5 times more effective at conversion.
How long does it take to create a landing page? expand_more
Allow 5 to 10 business days for a complete landing page: research, copywriting, design, development and go-live. Urgent pages can be delivered in 3 days with a surcharge. Every page goes through a validation cycle before publication.
Do you use templates or is everything custom-built? expand_more
Every landing page is custom-designed for your offer, your audience and your objectives. We don't use generic templates. Our approach is based on analysis of your market, your competitors, and your visitor behavior to create a unique, high-performing page.
How do you measure landing page performance? expand_more
We track conversion rate, cost per lead, bounce rate, time on page and scroll depth. Every landing page is equipped with complete tracking via Google Analytics 4 and heatmap tools. You receive a clear report with key metrics and our optimization recommendations.
Can you optimize an existing landing page? expand_more
Absolutely. We carry out a complete audit of your existing page: heatmap analysis, user journey, copywriting and design. We identify friction points and propose concrete optimizations. Sometimes a few targeted changes are enough to double your conversions.
