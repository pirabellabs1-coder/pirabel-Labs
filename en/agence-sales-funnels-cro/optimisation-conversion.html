The problem

WHAT IS SILENTLY KILLING YOUR CONVERSIONS

Most conversion problems are invisible to the naked eye. Here are the most common ones we discover with our clients.

touch_app

Clicks that lead nowhere

Your visitors click on elements that are not clickable. They tap on images thinking they are buttons. They try to interact with dead zones on your page. These micro-frustrations accumulate and push your visitors to abandon. Heatmaps reveal these confusion zones that you did not even suspect.

remove_shopping_cart

A checkout that drives people away

Your clients are ready to pay — they have added products to the cart, filled in their details — but your payment process discourages them. Too many steps, mandatory account creation, hidden fees that appear at the last moment, limited payment options. Each friction in the checkout is money slipping away.

unfold_more

Content that nobody reads

You have invested in quality content, but scroll analysis shows that 70% of your visitors never go beyond the first screen. Your most important information may be at the bottom of the page, where nobody goes. Your content structure does not match the reading behavior of your visitors.

phone_iphone

The mobile experience is neglected

Your website looks great on a 27-inch screen, but on a smartphone it is a different story. Buttons are too close together, text overflows, images hide important content. More than 60% of your traffic comes from mobile — if the experience is mediocre, you lose the majority of your potential clients.

Our method

SURGICAL CORRECTIONS,
NOT A COMPLETE REDESIGN.

We use real behavioral data to identify and fix the problems that most impact your conversions.

local_fire_department

Advanced heatmap analysis

Heatmaps reveal where your visitors click, scroll and spend time. We analyze the heat maps of every strategic page to understand the real behavior of your users. Hot zones reveal what attracts attention, cold zones show what is ignored.

videocam

Session recordings

We watch hundreds of real session recordings to observe how your visitors navigate, where they hesitate, where they click by mistake and where they abandon. This qualitative analysis reveals problems that numbers alone do not show.

shopping_cart_checkout

Checkout and form optimization

We analyze each step of your payment process and your forms. Field reduction, step simplification, adding trust badges, payment flow optimization — each change reduces friction and increases the completion rate.

speed

Speed optimization

Each additional second of load time reduces your conversions by 7%. We optimize images, code, cache and hosting for load times under 2 seconds. A fast site converts better — this is proven by every study.

accessibility

Complete UX audit

Our UX experts examine your website against a grid of 100+ criteria: navigation, readability, visual hierarchy, accessibility, consistency. Each problem is documented with a concrete recommendation and prioritized by potential impact on your conversions.

person_pin

Personalization and segmentation

Not all your visitors are identical. We implement personalization that adapts content, offers and messages according to the visitor segment: new vs. returning, traffic source, browsing behavior. A personalized message converts up to 3 times better than a generic message.

Frequently Asked Questions

YOUR QUESTIONS ABOUT CONVERSION OPTIMIZATION

What is conversion rate optimization (CRO)? expand_more
Conversion rate optimization consists of improving the percentage of visitors who complete the desired action on your website: purchase, registration, quote request. We analyze the real behavior of your visitors, identify friction points and fix them with measured modifications. Each improvement is tested to guarantee a positive impact.
How do you identify conversion problems? expand_more
We use a combination of tools: Google Analytics 4 for quantitative data, Hotjar for heatmaps and session recordings, manual UX audits and funnel analysis. This multi-layered approach reveals exactly where your visitors hesitate, click without effect, scroll without reading or abandon.
Is it necessary to rebuild my site to improve conversions? expand_more
No. In most cases, targeted modifications are sufficient to significantly improve your conversions. Changing a headline, simplifying a form, repositioning a button or adding social proof can have an enormous impact. We do not rebuild your website — we improve the elements that matter most for your conversions.
How long before seeing results? expand_more
The first improvements are visible within 2 to 4 weeks. The initial audit identifies quick wins — fast fixes with high impact. Deeper optimizations take 2 to 3 months to show their full effect. The advantage of CRO is that each gain is permanent: once an improvement is validated, it benefits 100% of your traffic.
Does CRO work with low traffic? expand_more
Yes. With low traffic volume, A/B tests take more time to reach statistical significance, but qualitative analysis (heatmaps, recordings, UX audit) is equally effective. Moreover, CRO is even more impactful for small businesses because each visitor counts more.
What is the difference between CRO and a site redesign? expand_more
A redesign changes the appearance of your website without any guarantee of improving conversions — sometimes it even degrades them. CRO is a data-driven approach that identifies real problems and provides measured corrections. Each modification is tested before being deployed. You keep what works and improve what does not.
