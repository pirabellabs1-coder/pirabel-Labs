Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT SYSTEME.IO

What is Systeme.io and why use it? expand_more
Systeme.io is a French all-in-one platform that allows you to create sales funnels, capture pages, automated email sequences, online courses and manage payments. It is an affordable and complete alternative to tools like ClickFunnels, ideal for French-speaking entrepreneurs and SMBs who want a simple and effective solution.
How much does Systeme.io cost? expand_more
Systeme.io offers a free plan with essential features: up to 2000 contacts, 3 sales funnels and 1 membership area. Paid plans start at 27 euros per month and offer more contacts, unlimited funnels and advanced features like A/B testing, webinars and removal of the Systeme.io branding.
Can you migrate from another tool to Systeme.io? expand_more
Yes, we handle the complete migration from ClickFunnels, Kajabi, WordPress, Teachable or any other tool. We transfer your pages, your email sequences, your contact lists and your courses. The migration is transparent, without any data loss and without interruption to your business.
Is Systeme.io suitable for selling online courses? expand_more
Absolutely. Systeme.io natively integrates a membership area for hosting your online courses. You can structure your modules, deliver content progressively (drip content), manage access and payments (one-time or recurring) directly from the platform. It is one of the best solutions for French-speaking trainers and coaches.
How quickly can a sales funnel be operational? expand_more
A complete sales funnel on Systeme.io is operational in 1 to 2 weeks. This includes strategy, page creation, copywriting, email sequence implementation and complete testing. Complex funnels with multiple upsells, courses and affiliate programs take 2 to 3 weeks.
