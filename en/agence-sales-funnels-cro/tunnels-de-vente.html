The problem

WHY YOUR PROSPECTS ARE NOT BUYING

Without a sales funnel, you let chance decide your conversions. Here is what is probably happening for you.

route

No defined customer journey

Your visitors arrive on your website without clear direction. No logical path from discovery to purchase. They navigate randomly between your pages, lose the thread of your value proposition and end up leaving your site. Without a structured journey, you rely on luck to generate sales — and luck is not a scalable strategy.

money_off

You sell too early or too late

You push your offer on the first visit when your prospect does not know you yet. Or conversely, you wait so long that the prospect has forgotten your existence. Timing is crucial: a sales funnel delivers the right message at the right time, when the prospect is ready to hear your offer and make their decision.

leak_remove

Leaks at every step

At each stage of your purchase journey, you lose prospects. The landing page does not convince, the product page does not inspire trust, the checkout is too complex. Without identifying and plugging these leaks, you waste your marketing budget. Each drop-off is a potential client who will probably never return.

shopping_cart

Your average order value stagnates

Your clients buy the minimum and leave. No upsell, no order bump, no complementary offer at the right time. You miss additional revenue on each transaction because your funnel offers nothing after the initial purchase. A well-designed funnel increases your average order value by 20 to 40% without additional effort.

Frequently Asked Questions

YOUR QUESTIONS ABOUT SALES FUNNELS

What is a sales funnel exactly? expand_more
A sales funnel is a structured journey that accompanies your prospect from their first interaction with your brand all the way to purchase. Instead of letting your visitors navigate randomly, you guide them with adapted content, progressive offers and automated follow-ups at each stage of their decision process: discovery, consideration, decision.
How long does it take to build a sales funnel? expand_more
A complete sales funnel takes between 3 and 6 weeks to design and deploy. This includes strategy, page creation, content writing, email sequence implementation and tracking setup. A simple funnel (lead magnet + email sequence) can be operational in 2 weeks.
What is the difference between TOFU, MOFU and BOFU? expand_more
TOFU (Top of Funnel) is the discovery phase where you attract attention with educational content. MOFU (Middle of Funnel) is the consideration phase where you nurture interest with case studies and proof. BOFU (Bottom of Funnel) is the decision phase where you trigger purchase with irresistible offers and guarantees.
Is a large ad budget necessary to make a funnel work? expand_more
No. A well-designed sales funnel works with any volume of traffic. It is about optimizing the journey to convert a maximum of visitors, regardless of their number. Even with 200 visitors per month, a structured funnel will convert much better than a standard website without a defined path.
How do you integrate upsells and order bumps? expand_more
Upsells are complementary offers presented just after the main purchase, when the client is in a buying mindset. Order bumps are additions offered on the payment page itself. We integrate them naturally and non-intrusively to increase your average order value by 20 to 40% on average.
Does a sales funnel work for B2B services? expand_more
Absolutely. In B2B, the funnel is often longer because the decision cycle is more complex. We create B2B funnels with high-value lead magnets (white papers, webinars, free audits), educational nurturing sequences and qualification stages that prepare the prospect for the sales conversation.
