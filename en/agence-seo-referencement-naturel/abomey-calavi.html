Is Pirabel Labs really headquartered in Abomey-Calavi? expand_more

Do you only work with clients in Calavi and Cotonou? expand_more
No. Our HQ is in Abomey-Calavi, but we serve businesses across Benin, in West Africa (Côte d'Ivoire, Senegal, Togo), in the Maghreb (Morocco, Tunisia) and the French-speaking diaspora in Europe and North America.
How much does an SEO engagement cost in Abomey-Calavi? expand_more
Pricing depends on your industry, competition and goals. A local SEO engagement for an SME typically starts around 250,000 XOF / month (~€380). We offer a free audit before any quote.
