Frequently Asked Questions

YOUR QUESTIONS ABOUT AUDIT SEO

What is an SEO audit and why do I need one? expand_more
An SEO audit is a complete diagnostic of your website that identifies everything slowing down your visibility on Google. It covers four dimensions: technical (is your site accessible and fast for Google?), content (is it relevant and optimized?), links (do you have enough authority?) and competition (what are those outranking you doing?). You need it to know exactly where to act, instead of guessing what is not working.
What is the difference between your free audit and the full audit? expand_more
The free audit is a quick diagnostic that gives you an overview of your situation: your current visibility on Google, the most glaring technical errors, and the main opportunities. It takes about 30 minutes and you receive a summary by email. The full audit is an in-depth study of 5 to 10 days that analyzes every dimension across more than 250 control points, with a detailed report, a prioritized action plan and a video presentation.
How long does a full SEO audit take? expand_more
A full audit takes between 5 and 10 business days depending on the size and complexity of your website. For a brochure site with fewer than 100 pages, allow about 5 business days. For an e-commerce site with thousands of pages and product catalogues, allow up to 10 days. You then receive a videoconference presentation of the report to go through the results together and answer all your questions.
Do you need technical skills to understand the report? expand_more
Absolutely not. That is actually our strong point. Our reports are written in clear, accessible language, without unnecessary technical jargon. Each identified issue is explained simply: what it is, why it is a problem, what impact it has on your visibility, and what the solution is. We illustrate with screenshots and concrete examples. The video presentation also gives you the chance to ask all your questions.
How often should an SEO audit be redone? expand_more
We recommend a full audit at least once a year to ensure your website stays healthy. It is also essential after any major change: site redesign, CMS or domain migration, mass content or feature additions. Between full audits, monthly technical monitoring detects and corrects emerging issues before they become critical.
What happens after the audit? expand_more
After the audit is delivered, you have two options. First option: your internal technical team implements the recommendations and we support you as an advisor to validate the fixes. Second option: we take full charge of implementing all the recommendations with monthly support. In both cases, you leave with a clear action plan, defined priorities and a realistic timeline.
