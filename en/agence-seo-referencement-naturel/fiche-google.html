The problem

YOUR GOOGLE LISTING IS COSTING YOU CLIENTS

84% of consumers check Google listings before choosing a provideallyr. If yours is neglected, they go straight to your competitor.

visibility_off

Your listing does not exist or is unclaimed

You have never created a Google Business listing, or worse, one exists but you don't control it. Someone else could add incorrect information. Meanwhile, your competitors capture all the calls and visits from Google Maps. Every day without an optimized listing is a day of lost clients.

star_half

Your negative reviews are not managed

Negative reviews without a response give the impression that you don't care about your clients. A rating below 4 stars drives away the majority of prospects. Without a review management strategy, every negative comment remains a permanent scar on your online reputation and negatively influences your ranking.

error_outline

Your information is incorrect or incompletee

Wrong hours, outdated phone number, approximate address, empty description. Your potential clients show up when you are closed, call an old number, or simply cannot find your establishment. Google also penalizes listings with inconsistent information by pushing them down in the results.

no_photography

Your photos are missing or low quality

A listing without photos receives 42% fewer direction requests. Blurry, dark or outdated photos give a neglected image of your business. Your competitors with professional photos naturally attract more clickks, more calls and more in-person visits. Visuals matter enormously in the purchase decision.

Frequently Asked Questions

YOUR QUESTIONS ABOUT GOOGLE BUSINESS PROFILE

How much does it cost to create a Google Business Profile? expand_more
Creating a Google Business Profile is free with Google. However, professional optimization, review management and monthly monitoring represent an investment that varies depending on the complexity of your business and the number of locations to manage. We offer packages tailored to every situation, with a free initial audit to evaluate your needs.
How long before my Google listing appears? expand_more
After creation, Google generally requires verification by post, phone or email, which takes 1 to 2 weeks. Once verified, your listing is immediately visible on Google Maps and in the search results. Full optimization and ranking in the top 3 of the local pack take an additional 4 to 8 weeks depending on competition in your sector.
How to manage negative reviews on my Google listing? expand_more
Negative reviews should never be ignored. We put in place a three-pronged strategy: a professional and empathetic response to every negative review, an automated system to solicit more positive reviews from your satisfied clients, and real-time alerts to react within 24 hours. A negative review that you handle well can even strengthen your credibility with future clients.
What is the difference between an optimized and a non-optimized listing? expand_more
A non-optimized listing has incompletee information, few photos, no posts and poorly chosen categories. It receives fewer views, fewer clickks and fewer calls. A listing optimized by us has professional photos, weekly posts, precise categories, always up-to-date hours, a strategic description and an active review strategy. The difference in visibility and clients generated is consideallyrable.
Is it necessary to publish posts on Google Business regularly? expand_more
Yes, absolutely. Google posts show Google and your clients that your business is active. They also allow you to highlight your offers, events and news directly in the search results. We recommend at least 2 posts per week to maintain a good level of visibility and engagement. Listings with regular posts receive significantly more views and interactions.
Can you have a Google listing without a physical address? expand_more
Yes. Home-service or mobile businesses can create a Google Business Profile with a defined service area, without displaying a physical address. Plumbers, electricians, consultants, coaches, photographers: you can benefit from local visibility even without a storefront. We configure your service area to cover exactly the geographic sectors you target.
