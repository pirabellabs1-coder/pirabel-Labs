Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING YOU WANT TO KNOW ABOUT SEO

How long does it take to see results SEO ? expand_more
The first technical gains are visible within 30 days: error fixes, speed improvements, better indexing. For a solid ranking on the first page of Google, allow 3 to 6 months depending on your industry competition. SEO is a medium-term investment, but the results are lasting — unlike advertising which stops the moment you cut the budget.
What is the difference between SEO and Google Ads? expand_more
Google Ads (paid advertising) brings you traffic immediately, but only as long as you pay. SEO builds a lasting asset: once well ranked, your website attracts free traffic for months or even years. The ideal is often to combine both: Google Ads for quick results while SEO gains momentum.
Is SEO really worth the investment ? expand_more
SEO offers the best return on investment of all digital marketing channels. One euro invested in SEO returns on average 4 to 5 euros in revenue over 12 months. Unlike advertising, SEO results accumulate over time: the longer you invest, the greater the return.
How do I know if my site needs SEO ? expand_more
If your website does not appear on the first page of Google when you search for your services, you are losing clients every day. If you depend on paid advertising for traffic, if your traffic is stagnant or declining, you need SEO. Our free audit shows you exactly where you stand and what you can gain.
What does your free SEO audit include? expand_more
Our free audit includes: an analysis of your current visibility on Google, a technical diagnostic of your website (speed, errors, indexing), a study of your main competitors, and concrete recommendations with a prioritized action plan. You receive a clear document, without jargon, with the most impactful actions to take first.
Do you work with businesses of all sizes? expand_more
Yes. Startups, SMBs, mid-size and large enterprises. We adapt our strategy to your budget, your market and your specific goals. We work with clients in Paris, Marseille, Lyon, Brussels, Montreal, Cotonou, Casablanca, Dakar, Abidjan and Tunis.
What guarantees do you offer on results? expand_more
We commit contractually to measurable objectives: keyword positions, organic traffic volume, and conversion rate indicators. If we do not reach the agreed targets, we continue working at no extra charge until we do. This is our way of proving that we believe in our method.
How does day-to-day collaboration work? expand_more
You have a dedicated project manager, reachable by email and phone. You receive a clear monthly report with results, completed actions and the plan for the following month. We hold a monthly video call to discuss strategy. You have nothing to manage — we take care of everything.
