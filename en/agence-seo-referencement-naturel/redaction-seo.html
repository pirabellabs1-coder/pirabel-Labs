Frequently Asked Questions

YOUR QUESTIONS ABOUT SEO WRITING

What is SEO writing and how is it different from regular copywriting? expand_more
SEO writing combines quality writing with optimisation for search engines. A regular copywriter produces text that is pleasant to read. An SEO writer does the same, but knows how to position that text on precise keywords in Google. This involves mastering keyword research, content structure for SEO, HTML markup, internal linking and Google's E-E-A-T criteria.
Do you use artificial intelligence to write? expand_more
AI is an assistance tool in our workflow, but every text is written, verified and enriched by a human writer who is an expert in your sector. We use AI to accelerate certain research and structuring phases, but the real added value — subject-matter expertise, concrete examples, editorial touch, fact-checking — is 100% human. Google increasingly values content that demonstrates genuine expertise and lived experience.
How many articles should be published per month to see results? expand_more
The ideal frequency depends on your sector, your competition and your objectives. In general, 4 to 8 articles per month is a good pace to build solid topical authority and start seeing significant results within 3 to 6 months. But quality always outweighs quantity: one in-depth 2,500-word article that is well-researched and perfectly optimised is worth more than 10 rushed 500-word articles.
How long before an SEO article attracts traffic? expand_more
A well-optimised article published on a site with decent authority starts to rank in Google within 2 to 4 weeks of publication. It generally reaches its full traffic potential between 3 and 6 months. The great advantage of SEO content is its lifespan: evergreen articles continue to attract traffic for years without additional effort, unlike social media posts.
What is a semantic cluster and why is it important? expand_more
A semantic cluster is a content structure where a pillar page (the main topic) is surrounded by satellite pages (the sub-topics) that are all interconnected through internal links. Google interprets this organisation as a very strong signal of expertise on the subject, which boosts the ranking of the entire content cluster. It is the most effective strategy for dominating an entire topic in search results.
Do you deliver content ready to publish? expand_more
Yes, absolutely. Every piece of content we deliver is ready to publish as-is. You receive the written and optimised text, title tags and meta description, the complete heading structure, suggested internal links, recommended illustration images with their alt attributes, and any structured data to integrate. You simply copy and paste into your CMS, or we can handle the publication for you.
