Frequently Asked Questions

VOS QUESTIONS SUR LE SEO LOCAL

What is local SEO and who is it for? expand_more
Le SEO local is l'ensemble techniques that allow à your business d'apparaitre in the results de recherche geolocalises : le pack local Google, Google Maps and the results organiques a intention locale. Il s'adresse a toute business that recoit clients physiquement or that opere in a zone geographique precisee : commerces, restaurants, cabinets medicaux, artisans, cliniques, agences immobilieres, garages, salons de coiffure, and bien d'autres.
How does ranking on Google Maps work? expand_more
Google Maps classe the businesss according to trois critères principaux. La pertinence : your listing correspond-elle a ce that l'utilisateur cherche? La distance : etes-you geographiquement proche de l'utilisateur? La brand awareness : avez-you avis positifs, citations cohérentes and un site web faisant autorite? We optimize these trois leviers simultanement for maximize your chances d'apparaitre in le top 3 du pack local.
How long to appear in the Google local pack? expand_more
The premiers results apparaissent généralement under 4 a 8 semaines after l'optimisation de your listing Google Business Profile. For the sectors tres concurrentiels like la restauration or le juridique in the grandes villes, comptez 2 a 4 mois for obtenir and maintenir a position stable in le top 3 du pack local. The actions on the avis and the citations produisent effets cumulatifs over time.
Do you don'ted a physical location for local SEO? expand_more
Non, not necessarily. Businesses in services that deplacent chez their clients — plombiers, electriciens, coaches, consultants — peuvent also bénéficier du SEO local thanks tox zones de service configurables on Google Business Profile. You n'avez not besoin d'a vitrine ouverte au public for apparaitre in the results locaux and recevoir appels since Google Maps.
How to manage negative reviews on Google? expand_more
The avis negatifs are not a fatalite, and it does not faut surtout not the ignorer. We mettons en place a strategy en trois volets : a reponse professionnelle and empathique a each avis negatif, un système automatise for solliciter davantage d'avis positifs aupres de your satisfied clients, and alertes en temps reel for reagir rapidement. Un avis negatif auquel you repondez bien peut same renforcer your credibilite aupres futurs clients.
Does local SEO work for multiple locations? expand_more
Absolument. We manage le SEO local multi-etablissements with a fiche Google Business Profile dedicatede and optimizede par emplacement, pages locales spécifiques on your website web, and a strategy de citations cohérente for each adresse. Each point de vente bénéficie d'a visibility locale optimizede everything en conservant la coherence de your brand a l'echelle nationale.
