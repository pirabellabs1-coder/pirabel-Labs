Frequently Asked Questions

YOUR QUESTIONS ABOUT LOCAL SEO

What is local SEO and who is it for? expand_more
Local SEO is the set of techniques that allow your business to appear in geolocalized search results: the Google local pack, Google Maps and organic results with local intent. It is for any business that receives clients physically or operates in a specific geographic area: shops, restaurants, medical practices, tradespeople, clinics, estate agencies, garages, hair salons, and many others.
How does ranking on Google Maps work? expand_more
Google Maps ranks businesses according to three main criteria. Relevance: does your listing match what the user is searching for? Distance: are you geographically close to the user? Prominence: do you have positive reviews, consistent citations and an authoritative website? We optimize all three levers simultaneously to maximize your chances of appearing in the top 3 of the local pack.
How long to appear in the Google local pack? expand_more
First results generally appear within 4 to 8 weeks after optimizing your Google Business Profile listing. For highly competitive sectors like restaurants or legal services in therge cities, allow 2 to 4 months to reach and maintain a stable position in the top 3 of the local pack. Actions on reviews and citations produce cumulative effects over time.
Do you need a physical location for local SEO? expand_more
No, not necessarily. Service-based businesses that travel to their clients — plumbers, electricians, coaches, consultants — can also benefit from local SEO through configurable service areas on Google Business Profile. You do not need a storefront open to the public to appear in local results and receive calls from Google Maps.
How to manage negative reviews on Google? expand_more
Negative reviews are not inevitable, and you should never ignore them. We put in place a three-pronged strategy: a professional and empathetic response to every negative review, an automated system to solicit more positive reviews from your satisfied clients, and real-time alerts to react quickly. A negative review that you handle well can even strengthen your credibility with future clients.
Does local SEO work for multiple locations? expand_more
Absolutely. We manage multi-location local SEO with a dedicated and optimized Google Business Profile listing per location, specific local pages on your website, and a consistent citation strategy for each address. Each location benefits from optimized local visibility while maintaining your brand's consistency at a national level.
