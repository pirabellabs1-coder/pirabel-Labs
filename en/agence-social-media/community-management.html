Frequently Asked Questions

YOUR QUESTIONS ABOUT COMMUNITY MANAGEMENT

What exactly is community management? expand_more
Community management consists of animating, moderating and developing your community on social media. This includes publishing content, responding to comments and private messages, moderating discussions, e-reputation monitoring and crisis management. The goal is to build an authentic relationship between your brand and your audience to turn your followers into loyal customers.
How much time per day do you dedicate to managing our community? expand_more
Our community managers are active on your accounts between 3 and 8 hours per day depending on your plan. Private messages receive a response within 2 hours on average during active hours. We cover your audience's peak hours to maximise engagement and leave no interaction unanswered.
How do you handle a crisis on social media? expand_more
We have a 4-step crisis management protocol: immediate detection through our continuous monitoring, severity assessment, drafting a validated response with you, and post-crisis follow-up. In an emergency, we contact you within 30 minutes and propose an action plan. Our goal is to turn every crisis into an opportunity to show your brand's transparency.
Do you also respond to private messages? expand_more
Yes. Managing private messages is an integral part of our community management service. We respond to Instagram DMs, Facebook Messenger messages, LinkedIn InMails and any other platform messaging. The average response time is 2 hours during active hours.
How do you maintain our brand voice on social media? expand_more
We create a comprehensive brand voice guide at the start of our collaboration. This document defines your brand personality, expressions to use or avoid, the level of formality and types of responses for each situation. Our community managers are trained to your brand identity and follow this guide daily to ensure perfect consistency.
What reporting do we receive on community activity? expand_more
Every month you receive a detailed report including: interactions handled, average response time, engagement rate, community growth, audience sentiment (positive, neutral, negative), the most discussed topics and our strategic recommendations for the following month.
