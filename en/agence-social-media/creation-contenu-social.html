Frequently Asked Questions

YOUR QUESTIONS ABOUT SOCIAL CONTENT CREATION

What types of content do you produce for social media? expand_more
We produce all formats adapted to each platform: retouched and styled photos, short videos (Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts), educational and engaging carousels, animated stories, infographics, visual quotes, behind-the-scenes and UGC content. Every visual is custom-created for your brand identity and optimised for each platform's algorithm.
How many pieces of content do you produce per month? expand_more
It depends on your plan and objectives. Our packages range from 12 to 30 pieces of content per month, including a mix of static and video formats. We adapt the volume to your ideal publishing frequency and priority platforms. Each piece of content is scheduled in your editorial calendar for maximum consistency.
Do we need to provide the base photos and videos? expand_more
Not necessarily. We can work from your existing visuals, organise dedicated photo and video shoots, or create all the content from scratch using graphic elements, premium image banks and motion design. We adapt to your resources and budget.
How do you ensure the content matches our brand image? expand_more
At the start of our collaboration, we create a social media visual guidelines document defining your colours, typography, photo styles, filters, templates and layout rules. Every piece of content is validated by you before publication. We use this guide as a reference to ensure every post is immediately recognisable as yours.
What is the content approval process? expand_more
Each month, we send you a complete batch of content for approval at least a week before publication. You can request unlimited revisions. Once approved, content is scheduled at optimal times. For breaking news or opportunities, we also create reactive content validated on an express basis.
Do you also write the post captions? expand_more
Yes. Our service includes complete copywriting for every post: hooks, body text, calls to action and strategic hashtags. Every text is adapted to the platform and your brand voice. We also write your story captions, reel descriptions and carousel text.
