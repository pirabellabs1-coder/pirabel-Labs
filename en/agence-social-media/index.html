Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING YOU WANT TO KNOW ABOUT SOCIAL MEDIA

Why hire a social media agency rather than managing in-house? expand_more
A social media agency brings you strategic expertise, professional tools and a publishing consistency that most in-house teams cannot maintain. You benefit from a complete team — strategist, community manager, content creator, graphic designer — for the price of a single salary. You gain time, coherence and above all measurable results.
Which social networks should you be on? expand_more
It depends entirely on your target audience and your objectives. Instagram and TikTok are ideal for visual B2C and lifestyle brands. LinkedIn is essential for B2B and social selling. Facebook remains powerful for local communities and groups. We analyse your market and audience to recommend the most profitable platforms — better to excel on 2 networks than to be mediocre on 5.
How long does it take to see results on social media? expand_more
The first results in terms of engagement and community growth are visible from the first month with a consistent content strategy. For a significant impact on your leads and sales, allow 3 to 4 months of regular strategy. Social networks reward consistency: the most constant you are, the most the algorithms favour you.
What budget should I plan for an effective social media strategy? expand_more
Our packages start at 600 euros per month for essential support across 2 platforms. The ideal budget depends on the number of platforms, publication frequency, video content creation and your growth objectives. We always adapt the strategy to your budget to maximise return on investment.
Do you also create visual content? expand_more
Yes. Our creative team produces all visual content: retouched photos, short videos, reels, animated stories, carousels and infographics. You have nothing to produce on your sideally if you prefer not to. We can also work from your existing visuals and adapt them to each platform's formats.
How do you measure the social media return on investment? expand_more
We track concrete indicators tied to your business: engagement rate, community growth, traffic generated to your website, qualified leads, conversions and attributable revenue. Every month, you receive a clear report with the figures that count — not vanity metrics, but actionable data.
What is the difference between community management and social media strategy? expand_more
Social media strategy is the master plan: it defines the objectives, key messages, editorial calendar, tone of voice and target platforms. Community management is the daily execution: publishing, moderation, responding to comments and messages, animating the community. Both are complementary and essential for lasting results.
Do you work with influences? expand_more
Yes. We have a network of micro-influences and content creators across several sectors (fashion, food, tech, lifestyle, B2B). We identify the most relevant profiles for your brand, negotiate partnerships, coordinate campaigns and measure results in terms of reach, engagement and conversions.
