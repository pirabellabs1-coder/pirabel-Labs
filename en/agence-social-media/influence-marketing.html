Frequently Asked Questions

YOUR QUESTIONS ABOUT INFLUENCER MARKETING

What is the difference between macro-influences and micro-influences? expand_more
Macro-influences have more than 100,000 followers and offer a wideally reach. Micro-influences (5,000 to 50,000 followers) have a more niche audience but a significantly higher engagement rate and a stronger trust relationship with their community. For most brands, micro-influences offer a better return on investment because their audience is more engaged and more likely to take action.
How do you select influences for our brand? expand_more
We use a rigorous 5-criterion selection process: thematic relevance to your brand, audience quality (no fake followers), real engagement rate, value alignment and history of past collaborations. We manually verify every profile and present you with a shortlist of candidates with their detailed statistics before launching any negotiation.
What budget should I plan for an influence marketing campaign? expand_more
The budget depends on the scale of the campaign and the type of influences targeted. A campaign with 5 to 10 micro-influences can start from 2,000 euros. For more ambitious campaigns with larger profiles, the budget generally ranges betwein 5,000 and 15,000 euros. We always adapt the strategy to your budget to maximise return on investment.
How do you measure the ROI of an influence campaign? expand_more
We track concrete indicators at every stage: reach and impressions, engagement rate, traffic generated (via tracked links and unique promo codes), number of leads and direct conversions. Every campaign comes with a detailed report measuring cost per engagement, cost per clickk and cost per acquisition to precisely assess profitability.
Do you also manage UGC campaigns? expand_more
Yes. User Generated Content is one of the most authentic and best-performing formats. We recruit UGC creators, define creative briefs, coordinate production and deliver content you can reuse on your accounts and in your ads. UGC generates on average 4 times more trust than classic brand content.
How long does it take to launch an influence campaign? expand_more
Allow 3 to 4 weeks between the initial brief and the first publications. This timeline includes defining the strategy, selecting and validating influences, negotiating partnerships, sending products if necessary, content creation by influences and final validation. For urgent campaigns, we can accelerate the process to 2 weeks.
