The problem

DO YOUR VIDEOS LOOK AMATEUR?

If you recognize yourself in any of these situations, professional video editing is the solution you need.

video_file

Unusable raw footage

You have gigabytes of rushes on your hard drive. Interviews, events, content filmed daily. But these raw files cannot be published as they are. They are too long, poorly framed in places, with inconsistent audio. Without editing, all this content remains unused and brings you nothing.

handyman

No editing skills

You have tried editing your videos yourself with free software. The result is acceptable, but far from professional. The transitions are basic, the pacing is flat, the audio is uneven and the final output does not match the image you want to project for your brand. Video editing is a craft, not a hobby.

low_priority

Amateur quality that undermines your image

Your published videos have inconsistent colours from one shot to the next, audio that crackles at times, no subtitles and abrupt cuts. This amateur quality undermines your credibility and drives away your prospects. In a world where video is king, the quality of your editing directly reflects the quality of your business.

schedule

No time to edit regularly

Even if you know how to edit, video editing takes considerable time. A 10-minute video can require 4 to 8 hours of work. Multiply that by the number of videos you need to publish each week and editing becomes a time sink that prevents you from focusing on your core business.

Frequently Asked Questions

YOUR QUESTIONS ABOUT VIDEO EDITING

What types of videos do you edit? expand_more
We edit all types of videos: YouTube videos, video podcasts, interviews, client testimonials, corporate videos, training content, webinars, vlogs and social media content. Whether you have raw footage filmed on a smartphone or files from professional cameras, we transform your raw material into impactful, publish-ready videos.
How do you transfer the video files? expand_more
We use secure transfer platforms like Google Drive, Dropbox or WeTransfer Pro for large files. We provide you with a dedicated upload link to send your rushes easily. For recurring projects, we set up a permanent shared folder with a clear file organisation for a smooth workflow.
What is the turnaround time for editing a video? expand_more
The standard turnaround is 3 to 5 business days for a video of 5 to 15 minutes. Short videos for social media are delivered in 1 to 2 days. For urgent projects, we offer an express service with delivery within 24 to 48 hours. Each project systematically includes two rounds of revisions so the result perfectly matches your expectations.
Is color grading included in the editing? expand_more
Yes. Each edit includes professional color grading to harmonise the shots, correct exposure differences and give a cohesive cinematic look to your video. For projects that require advanced colorimetric treatment, we offer premium color grading with a specific artistic direction suited to your visual universe.
Can you add subtitles to our videos? expand_more
Absolutely. Subtitling is included in our editing services. We offer burned-in subtitles with a design consistent with your brand guidelines, or separate SRT files for platforms that support dynamic subtitles like YouTube and LinkedIn. We handle French and English, and can coordinate other languages on request.
Do you offer monthly subscriptions? expand_more
Yes. For content creators and businesses that publish regularly, we offer monthly packages with a defined number of videos per month. The subscription includes preferential rates, a dedicated editor who knows your style, and guaranteed delivery times. It is the ideal solution for maintaining regular and consistent video production.
