Frequently Asked Questions

YOUR QUESTIONS ABOUT MOTION DESIGN

What is motion design exactly? expand_more
Motion design is the art of bringing movement to graphic elements. It combines graphic design, animation and visual storytelling to create dynamic content: explainer videos, logo animations, animated infographics, transitions and video overlays. It is the ideal tool for making a complex message simple and memorable, whether for your social media, your website or your sales presentations.
How long does it take to produce a motion design video? expand_more
Turnaround depends on project complexity. A simple 10–15 second animation can be delivered in 5 to 7 business days. A full 60–90 second explainer video typically requires 2 to 4 weeks including script, storyboard, animation and revisions. We set a precise schedule from day one so you know exactly when to expect your animation.
Which software do you use for motion design? expand_more
We primarily work with After Effects for complex 2D animations, Cinema 4D for 3D elements, Illustrator and Photoshop for graphic asset creation, and Premiere Pro for final editing. For lightweight web animations, we use Lottie which delivers smooth, high-performance animations directly on your website.
Is motion design suitable for every industry? expand_more
Yes. Motion design is a universal format that adapts to every industry. Whether you're in tech, healthcare, finance, education or e-commerce, animations help simplify complex concepts, present products in an engaging way and tell your story. We adapt the visual style, pace and tone to your industry and target audience.
Can you create animations for our social media? expand_more
Absolutely. It's one of our most frequent use cases. We create animations optimised for each platform: square and vertical formats for Instagram and TikTok, horizontal formats for YouTube and LinkedIn. We deliver files in every required format with the right dimensions and durations to maximise engagement on each social network.
How much does a motion design video cost? expand_more
Pricing varies based on duration, complexity and desired animation style. A short social media animation starts at a few hundred euros, while a complete explainer video with script and voice-over typically ranges between 2,000 and 8,000 euros. We provide a precise, detailed quote after analysing your brief so you know exactly what you're getting.
