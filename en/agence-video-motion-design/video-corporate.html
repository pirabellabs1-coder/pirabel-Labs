Frequently Asked Questions

YOUR QUESTIONS ABOUT VIDEO CORPORATE

What exactly is a corporate video? expand_more
A corporate video is a professional film that presents your business, your values, your teams or your products. It can take several forms: an institutional film, an executive interview, a client testimonial, an internal report or event coverage. The goal is to tell your story in an authentic and engaging way to strengthen your brand image with your prospects, clients and future collaborators.
How much does a corporate video cost? expand_more
The budget depends on the type of video, its duration and the complexity of the production. A simple client testimonial starts from 1,500 euros, a full company film with multiple interviews ranges from 3,000 to 10,000 euros, and a premium production with several days of filming can go beyond that. We provide a precise and transparent quote after analysing your brief.
How long does it take to produce a corporate video? expand_more
The full process typically takes 3 to 6 weeks. One week of pre-production for the brief, script and location scouting. One to two days of filming. And two to three weeks of post-production for editing, colour grading and sound mixing. Urgent projects can be delivered in 2 weeks with an accelerated schedule.
Do you provide the filming equipment? expand_more
Yes. We arrive with all the necessary equipment: professional 4K cameras, studio or portable lighting, lapel microphones and boom mics, monitoring screens and all filming accessories. You do not need to provide anything. We also handle filming permits when required.
Can you film on our premises? expand_more
Absolutely. The majority of our corporate shoots take place at our clients' premises. We carry out a prior site visit to identify the best spaces, optimise natural lighting and minimise noise. If your premises are not suitable, we can also organise the shoot at a professional studio or an appropriate external location.
How many versions do we receive? expand_more
You systematically receive a long version of 2 to 5 minutes for your website and YouTube, plus short cut-down versions for your social media in 30, 60 and 90 seconds. Two rounds of revisions are included in every project. We deliver the files in all necessary formats with integrated subtitles and optimised thumbnails.
