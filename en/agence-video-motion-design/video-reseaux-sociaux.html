ARE YOUR VIDEOS UNDERPERFORMING?

If you recognize yourself in any of these situations, your social videos need a professional approach.

Formats not adapted to platforms

You publish the same video everywhere, in the same format. A horizontal video on Instagram, content that is too long for TikTok, no subtitles on LinkedIn. The algorithms penalize non-native content and your reach collapses on each platform.

No video engagement

Your videos accumulate a few dozen views, zero comments and no shares. The problem is not the algorithm — it is that your videos do not capture attention in the first 3 seconds. Your audience scrolls without even stopping on your content.

content_copy

Monotonous and repetitive content

Your videos all look the same. Same framing, same pace, same structure. Your audience gets bored and the algorithms detect the lack of variety. You need creative formats, visual hooks and a diversity of content that surprises and retains your audience.

Frequently Asked Questions

YOUR QUESTIONS ABOUT SOCIAL MEDIA VIDEOS

What video formats do you produce for social media? expand_more
We produce all video formats adapted to social platforms: Instagram Reels in 9:16 up to 90 seconds, TikTok in 9:16 up to 3 minutes, YouTube Shorts in 9:16 up to 60 seconds, Stories in 9:16 of 15 seconds, LinkedIn videos in 16:9 or 1:1, and square videos for feeds. Each video is optimized for the technical specifications of the target platform.
How many videos can you produce per month? expand_more
It depends on the video complexity. In regular production mode, we deliver between 8 and 20 short videos per month. For brands that need a high volume, we set up a batch shooting workflow: one or two shooting days per month that generate enough content for 4 weeks of regular publication.
Do we need to appear in the videos? expand_more
Not necessarily. If you or your team are comfortable in front of the camera, it is a plus for authenticity. But we also offer faceless formats: product videos, animations, voiceover on B-roll, dynamic text edits and user-generated content style. We adapt the format to what works best for your brand and your audience.
Do you also handle video publishing? expand_more
Yes, if you wish. We can manage video publishing on your social accounts at optimal times, with the hashtags, descriptions and calls to action adapted to each platform. This service is optional and can be combined with our community management offering for complete management of your social presence.
How do you ensure that videos perform on the algorithms? expand_more
We apply the best practices of each platform: visual hook in the first 3 seconds to capture attention, subtitles for silent viewing, native formats, trending audio when relevant, and narrative structures that retain attention until the end. We also analyze the performance of each video to continuously adjust the strategy.
What is the delivery time for social videos? expand_more
For one-off projects, allow 5 to 7 business days between the brief and delivery. In monthly subscription mode, we deliver videos in weekly batches according to the editorial calendar defined together. For reactive content tied to trends, we have an accelerated process of 24 to 48 hours so you do not miss the momentum.
