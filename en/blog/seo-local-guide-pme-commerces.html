Optimiser each champ of your listing

  • Nom of the business : use your nom commercial exact, without add of keywords superflus. Google pénalise the keyword stuffing in the noms of fiches.

The citations NAP and the consistency data

The citations NAP (Name, Address, Phone) are the mentions of your nom d'business, address and numéro of téléphone on the web. Elles constitute a signal of confiance fundamental for Google in the évaluation of the légitimité and of the localisation of your business.

Où build your citations

  • Annuaires généralistes : Pages Jaunes, Yelp, Foursquare, Apple Plans, Bing Places, TripAdvisor.
  • Annuaires sectoriels : according to your activity (Doctolib for the santé, The Fourchette for the restauration, etc.).
  • Chambres of commerce and associations professionallows : sources d'authority for your industry and your zone géographique.
  • Presse locale and blogs local : mentions in médias local renforcent considerably your authority locale.

The importance cruciale of the consistency

The règle d'or citations NAP is the consistency absolue. Your nom, your address and your numéro of téléphone must être rigorously identiques everywhere. The moindre variation, like « Rue » vs « R. » or a numéro of téléphone different, can semer the confusion chez Google and diluer your authority locale. Réaread an audit of your citations existantes and corrigez all inconsistency.
