WordPress vs Shopify: Complete CMS Comparison for 2026

Pirabel Labs Team February 28, 2026 9 min read

Introduction : the choice of the CMS, a decision strategic

The choix of your CMS (Content Management System) is one of the decisions the most structurantes of your projet web. Il determines non only the features available today, but also your capacité à évoluer, à scaler and à you adapter to the needs of demain. En 2026, WordPress and Shopify dominent the market with philosophies radically different.

WordPress propulse 43 % of all the websites in the world, while Shopify s'est imposé like the platform e-commerce of reference with more of 4,6 millions of boutiques online actives. Chacun has forces indéniables and limites that he convient of connaître before of faire your choix.

Ce comparatif détaillé, préparé par the experts of Pirabel Labs, analyse these deux platforms under all the angles for you aider à prendre the best decision based on of your situation specific.

The choix between WordPress and Shopify dépend fundamentally of the nature and objectives of your projet.

Facilité d'utilisation and prise en main

Shopify : the simplicité before tout

Shopify was designed dès the départ for enable à quiconque of create a boutique online without no compétence technical. The interface is intuitive, the process of configuration is guidé step par step, and you can lancer your boutique en a few heures only. The management products, commandes and paiements is intégrée nativement and fonctionne of way fluide.

WordPress : the flexibilité to the price of the complexité

WordPress offers a courbe d'apprentissage more prononcée. The installation requiert a hosting web, the configuration of a database and the installation of the CMS. For the transform en boutique online, you devrez installowr and configure WooCommerce, a plugin e-commerce puissant but that ajoute a couche of complexité additional. However, a fois maîtrisé, WordPress offers a contrôle total on each aspect of your site.

Si you n'have no compétence technical and want to sell online quickly, Shopify is the choice évident. Si you recherchez a contrôle total and the possibilité of create a site that dépasse largement the cadre of the e-commerce, WordPress is imbattable.

Fonctionnalités and extensions

The écosystem WordPress

WordPress dispose of the most vaste écosystem of plugins and of thèmes to the world. With more of 60 000 plugins available, you can ajouter pratiquement any feature imaginable :

  • WooCommerce : transform WordPress en a platform e-commerce completeee, with management products, stocks, paiements and expéditions.
  • Yoast SEO / Rank Math : tools d'optimisation Advanced SEO directly intégrés à your tableau of bord.
  • Elementor / Divi : constructeurs of pages visuels for designer your site without écrire of code.
  • WPForms / Gravity Forms : creation of formulaires complexes with logique conditionslle.
  • WPML / Polylang : management multilingue native of your content.

The App Store of Shopify

Shopify propose more of 8 000 applications in son App Store, spécialement conçues for the e-commerce. The applications of Shopify are generally more fiables and better intégrées that the plugins WordPress, car they are soumises à a process of validation strict. Parmi the applications essential : Klaviyo for the email marketing, Oberlo for the dropshipping, Judge.me for the reviews clients and PageFly for the customisation advanced pages.

WordPress offers a flexibilité of development quasi illimitée thanks to son code open-source.

Compareason costs

The costs WordPress

WordPress lui-même is free and open-source, but the costs réels include several postes :

  1. Hébergement web : of 5 à 50 euros par month for a hosting mutualisé of quality, jusqu'à 200 euros and more for a serveur dédié or a hosting managé premium.
  2. Nom name : approximately 10 à 15 euros par an.
  3. Thème premium : betwein 50 and 200 euros en purchase unique.
  4. Plugins premium : of 0 à 500 euros par an according to your needs (WooCommerce extensions, Yoast Premium, etc.).
  5. Maintenance and updates : free si you the faites vous-même, or 50 à 200 euros par month via a provider.

The costs Shopify

Shopify fonctionne on a model d'subscription mensuel tout-en-un :

  • Basic Shopify : 36 euros par month, ideal for the créateurs and petites businesses.
  • Shopify : 105 euros par month, for the businesses en growth with needs of reporting avancé.
  • Advanced Shopify : 399 euros par month, for the businesses à strong volume nécessitant features advanced.

À these subscriptions s'ajoutent the commissions on the transactions (de 0,5 à 2 % si you n'use not Shopify Payments) and the cost applications tierces that s'accumule quickly.

SEO : quel CMS for the organic search ?

The SEO is a criterion of choix fondamental for all business that desired generate of the organic traffic. On this point, WordPress has a advantage historique significatif.

With WordPress, you have a contrôle total on the structure of your URL, your tags meta, your fichier robots.txt, your sitemap XML, your markup Schema.org and each aspect technical of the SEO. The plugins Yoast SEO and Rank Math offer features d'optimisation advanced that do not have d'équivalent on Shopify.

Shopify a considerably amélioré ses capacités SEO to the fil years, but some limitations persistent : the structure URL impose a préfixe /collections/ and /products/ that you ne can not modifier, the customisation of the fichier robots.txt is limitée, and the management of the content dupliqué between the collections requires a attention particulière.

The SEO technical is a field where WordPress offers a contrôle nettement higher à Shopify.

E-commerce : that gagne the bataille ?

For the vente online pure, Shopify domine. The platform was construite exclusively for the e-commerce : management stocks, paiements multi-quotees, calcul automatique taxes, intégrations transporteurs and checkout optimisé are natifs and fonctionnent perfectly dès the first jour.

WordPress with WooCommerce offers a alternative puissante and more flexible, but that demande dadvantage of configuration and of maintenance. WooCommerce excelle for the catalogues complexes, the products configurables and the models of vente non conventionnels (subscriptions, réservations, marketplace). Son advantage décisif : no commission on the ventes, contrairement à Shopify.

Notre verdict : how choisir

After avoir accompagné tens of clients in this choix, voici our recommendation at Pirabel Labs :

  • Choose Shopify si : your activity is mainly e-commerce, you want to sell quickly without compétences technicals, you have a catalogue of products standard and you préférez the simplicité à the customisation poussée.
  • Choose WordPress si : your site combine content and vente, the SEO is a priorité strategic, you have need of a customisation advanced, you disposez of resources technicals internes or of a provider of confiance, and you want to garder a contrôle total on your data and your hosting.
Il n'existe not of CMS universallowment meilleur. The best CMS is celui that correspond à your needs specific, à your compétences and à your ambitions. Ne choose never a tool par mode : choose-le par adéquation.
