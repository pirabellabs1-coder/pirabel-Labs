The problem

DOES THIS SOUND FAMILIAR?

If you recognize yourself in any of these situations, you're in the right place.

history

Your image is outdated

Your logo is ten years old, your colors no longer match your positioning, and your visual assets look like they're from another era. Your prospects judge your seriousness in a split second — and what they see doesn't convince them to stay.

thumb_down

Your logo looks amateur

You had it made by a cousin, on Canva, or via a 50-euro site. The result doesn't reflect the quality of what you sell. Your competitors have polished brands, and you're missing out on contracts because your image doesn't inspire trust.

shuffle

No visual consistency

Your site uses one color palette, your social networks another, and your business cards a third. No one in your team knows which fonts to use or what the correct logo format is. The result: a fragmented and unreadable brand.

help_outline

Confusion around your brand

Your customers don't understand what you do at a glance. Your positioning is blurry, your message diluted. You know your offer is solid, but your visual identity doesn't tell the right story and doesn't touch the right emotions.

groups

No differentiation

Your sector is saturated. Your competitors all look alike, and so do you. Nothing in your visual identity sets you apart. You are interchangeable in the eyes of your prospects, and they choose the cheapest option for lack of perceived value.

broken_image

Inconsistent assets

Every new brochure, every Instagram post, every PowerPoint presentation is created from scratch. You lose time, money, and credibility. Without a graphic system, every asset is an improvisation that weakens your brand.

Our approach

WE DON'T DO GRAPHIC DESIGN.
WE BUILD BRANDS THAT LAST.

At Pirabel Labs, design is never decorative. Every visual choice — color, typography, shape, space — is a strategic decision at the service of your positioning. We don't draw pretty logos: we build complete visual systems that work everywhere, from your site's favicon to your shop sign.

Our method always starts with a deep understanding of your market, your customers, and your competitors. Before touching a pencil, we define with you the brand territory, the values to convey, and the emotions to evoke. This strategic work is the foundation of any design that stands the test of time.

The result? A consistent, distinctive, and memorable visual identity that inspires trust from the first contact. A graphic system that your team can easily use, without needing a designer for every publication. And a brand that stands out in a saturated market.

Frequently asked questions

EVERYTHING YOU WANT TO KNOW ABOUT DESIGN & BRANDING

How much does a complete visual identity cost? expand_more
A complete visual identity — logo, brand guidelines, color palette, typographies, graphic elements, and brand guide — starts at 2,500 euros. The final price depends on project complexity, number of assets, and specific needs. For a logo alone, we have plans starting at 800 euros. Every project gets a custom quote after our discovery call.
How long does it take to create a logo? expand_more
Allow 2 to 4 weeks for a professional logo. The first week is for brief and research. The second for concept creation. Weeks 3 and 4 are for revisions and finalization. For a full visual identity, allow 4 to 6 weeks. We never rush the creative process — a good logo needs time and reflection.
What is the difference between a logo and a visual identity? expand_more
The logo is a central element of your visual identity, but it's only part of the system. Visual identity encompasses the logo, color palette, typographies, patterns, photographic style, icons, layout rules, and application examples. It's the set of these elements used consistently that builds recognition.
Why invest in branding rather than just a logo? expand_more
A logo alone isn't enough to build a strong brand. Without brand guidelines or a visual system, every asset you create is an improvisation. Complete branding guarantees that your site, social networks, cards, and presentations all tell the same story with the same quality. This consistency builds long-term trust.
How many logo proposals will I receive? expand_more
You systematically receive 3 distinct logo concepts, each exploring a different creative direction. Each proposal is presented with a rational explaining the design choices. Once you select your favorite, we proceed with 2 rounds of revisions to refine it until it's perfect.
What does the final brand guide contain? expand_more
The brand guide is a complete document including: all logo versions with usage rules and clear zones, color palette with Hex, RGB, CMYK, and Pantone codes, primary and secondary typographies with hierarchies, complementary graphic elements, application examples, and design 'don'ts'. It's the reference for your team or external agencies to stay on-brand.
Do you work with startups or only large companies? expand_more
We work with all profiles: startups needing a solid initial identity, SMEs wanting to professionalize their image, and large companies undergoing rebrands. Every project is tailored to your budget, stage, and ambitions. We believe every company deserves a visual identity that match its goals.
Do you also do packaging design? expand_more
Yes, packaging is integral to our design and branding offer. We design packaging that catches the eye, tells your story, and respects print constraints. From labels to boxes to shipping cartons, we create unboxing experiences that reinforce your positioning and build loyalty.
