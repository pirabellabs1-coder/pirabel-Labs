Our hiring process

FOUR STEPS TO JOINING THE TEAM

Our hiring process respects your time and provides a realistic preview of working at Pirabel Labs. We avoid trick interview questions, abstract whiteboarding and unpaid project work. Instead we focus on collaborative conversations and a paid practical exercise that demonstrates how you actually approach problems.

01

Application review

Submit your application through our careers form including your CV, portfolio links if relevant, a brief cover letter explaining your motivation and your earliest available start date. Every application is personally reviewed by a senior team member within five business days. We respond to every applicant whether or not we move forward.

02

Discovery conversation

A 45-minute video call with the hiring manager to discuss your background, your career aspirations, your perspective on the discipline and your interest in Pirabel Labs. This is genuinely a two-way conversation. We want to understand your professional story and you should evaluate whether our team and projects align with your ambitions.

03

Practical exercise

A scoped paid exercise representative of real client work, typically requiring four to six hours of focused effort completed at your own pace within ten business days. We compensate you fairly for this time. The exercise lets you demonstrate your craft realistically rather than relying on theoretical interview puzzles disconnected from actual professional work.

04

Team conversations and offer

Two to three additional conversations with potential teammates and senior leadership covering technical depth, collaboration style, cultural alignment and compensation expectations. References checked with your permission. Final offer typically extended within ten business days of your final conversation with clear written terms covering role, compensation, benefits and start date.
