Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING ABOUT DIGITAL AUDIT

What does a complete digital audit cover? expand_more
Our audit covers 6 areas: technical (speed, security, mobile), SEO (keywords, content, backlinks), UX (navigation, conversion), advertising (ROI, targeting), social media (engagement, audience) and content (quality, strategy). Each area is scored and comes with recommendations.
How long does the audit take? expand_more
The complete audit takes 3 to 5 business days. We use professional tools (Semrush, Ahrefs, Screaming Frog, Google Analytics, etc.) combined with expert manual analysis. The final report is between 20 and 40 pages depending on complexity.
Is the audit really free? expand_more
The initial free audit is a quick diagnosis covering essential points. The complete and detailed audit with comprehensive report is a paid service. Contact us to learn about pricing adapted to your situation.
What happens after the audit? expand_more
You receive a detailed report with recommendations prioritized by impact and ease of implementation. You can implement the recommendations yourself or entrust us with the implementation. We also offer monthly support.
How often should I do an audit? expand_more
We recommend a complete audit every 6 to 12 months. Digital evolves fast: Google algorithms, social media trends, user behaviors. A regular audit helps you stay competitive and identify new opportunities.
