Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING ABOUT E-COMMERCE CONSULTING

Which e-commerce platform should I choose? expand_more
It depends on your budget, catalog and ambitions. Shopify is ideal for getting started quickly. WooCommerce offers more flexibility if you already have a WordPress site. PrestaShop is suited to the French market. Magento is good for large catalogs. We guide you toward the best choice.
How much does an online store cost? expand_more
A basic Shopify store starts at 2,000 euros. A custom WooCommerce store costs between 5,000 and 15,000 euros. A complete e-commerce site with custom design, integrations and training ranges from 10,000 to 30,000 euros depending on complexity.
Should I also sell on marketplaces? expand_more
Marketplaces (Amazon, Cdiscount, Etsy) offer immediate visibility but take 15 to 30% commission. We recommend a hybrid strategy: your own store for margin and data, marketplaces for volume and visibility.
How to manage logistics? expand_more
Several options depending on your volume: in-house management with tools like ShipStation, outsourcing with a 3PL (fulfillment center), or dropshipping for certain products. We help you choose and implement the right solution.
How long to become profitable? expand_more
Profitability depends on your margins, customer acquisition and conversion rate. On average, a well-launched store reaches profitability between 6 and 12 months. SEO and content marketing gradually reduce acquisition costs.
