Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING ABOUT STARTUP CONSULTING

At what stage of the startup do you intervene? expand_more
We intervene at all stages: pre-seed (idea validation, digital MVP), seed (first users, product-market fit), Series A (scaling, massive acquisition). Our approach adapts to your maturity and budget.
What is the minimum budget for a startup? expand_more
We offer startup-friendly packages starting at 500 euros per month. What matters isn't the amount but the approach: test fast, measure, iterate. With a small well-allocated budget, results can be spectacular.
What is growth hacking? expand_more
Growth hacking is a marketing approach focused on rapid growth. It combines creativity, data analysis and rapid experimentation. Instead of long and costly campaigns, we launch 10 experiments per week and double what works.
Can you help with fundraising? expand_more
We're not fundraisers, but we prepare the marketing and digital aspect of your pitch. Growth metrics, acquisition cost, LTV, conversion rate... Investors want solid numbers, and we help you build them.
Do you work with tech startups? expand_more
Yes, it's actually our specialty. SaaS, fintech, healthtech, edtech, marketplace... We know the specifics of each vertical and the acquisition strategies that work for each one.
