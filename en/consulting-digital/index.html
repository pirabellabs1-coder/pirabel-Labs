Our approach

NOT SLIDES.
A STRATEGY THAT GETS EXECUTED.

At Pirabel Labs, consulting is not an academic exercise. We don't produce 200-page reports that end up in a drawer. We build actionable strategies with clear priorities, identified owners and precise deadlines.

Our approach is based on a rigorous diagnosis of your current situation, an analysis of your market and competitors, and the definition of a strategic roadmap with measurable milestones. Each recommendation is quantified in terms of effort, cost and expected return.

Above all, we don't disappear after delivering the report. We support you through implementation, train your teams and adjust the strategy based on results. Consulting without execution serves no purpose.

Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING YOU WANT TO KNOW ABOUT CONSULTING

Who is digital consulting for? expand_more
Digital consulting is for executives, marketing directors and digital managers who want to structure or accelerate their online presence. SMEs, mid-market and large companies that need a clear strategic vision before investing in digital actions. It's also ideal for businesses undergoing digital transformation that need an experienced guide.
What is the difference between consulting and service delivery? expand_more
Consulting gives you the vision and strategy. Service delivery executes the actions. We can do both, but consulting alone is ideal if you have an internal team capable of executing or if you work with other providers. We define the what, the why and the priorities - your team or providers handle the how.
How long does a completee digital audit take? expand_more
A completee digital audit typically takes 2 to 3 weeks. It covers your website, SEO, social media, advertising, marketing tools, CRM and internal processes. You receive a detailed report with a prioritized action plan, resource estimates and a realistic implementation timeline.
Which KPIs do you track? expand_more
We define KPIs suited to your specific business objectives: qualified traffic, conversion rate per channel, customer acquisition cost, ROI per channel, social engagement, SEO rankings, quality leads, retention rate. Each indicator is chosen to measure real business impact, not vanity metrics that lead nowhere.
Can you support a completee digital transformation? expand_more
Yes, it's one of our specialties. Our transformation support covers global strategy, tool selection and integration, team training, process implementation and long-term result tracking. We act as your part-time external digital director to manage your transformation end-to-end, with regular checkpoints.
Do you work with businesses in Africa and the Maghreb? expand_more
Absolutely. We have offices in Paris, Cotonou and Casablanca. We support businesses across the French-speaking world: France, Belgium, Canada, Benin, Morocco, Senegal, Ivory Coast, Tunisia. Our expertise is suited to each market's specifics, and we know the digital particularities of each region.
