Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING ABOUT DIGITAL STRATEGY

What is a digital strategy? expand_more
It's an action plan that defines how to use digital channels to achieve your business objectives. It includes analysis of your market, channel selection, action planning, budget and performance indicators.
How much does a digital strategy cost? expand_more
An audit and complete digital strategy starts at 2,500 euros. The price varies depending on the size of your business, the number of channels to cover and the depth of analysis desired. Monthly support is billed separately.
How long to see results? expand_more
Initial results are visible in the first month of execution. Significant results come between 3 and 6 months. SEO takes more time (6-12 months) while advertising campaigns generate immediate results.
Do I need to be present on all channels? expand_more
No. It's actually counter-productive. We identify the 2 to 3 most relevant channels for your audience and budget, and we focus your efforts there. It's better to excel on 2 channels than to be mediocre on 10.
What is the difference between strategy and execution? expand_more
Strategy defines the what, the why and the how. Execution is the concrete implementation: content creation, campaign launches, SEO optimization, etc. We offer both: strategy alone or strategy + execution.
