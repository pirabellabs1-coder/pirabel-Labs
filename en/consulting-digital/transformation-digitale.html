Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING ABOUT DIGITAL TRANSFORMATION

What is digital transformation? expand_more
It's the process of modernizing your business through the adoption of digital tools and processes. This includes document digitalization, repetitive task automation, system integration and the adoption of new collaborative work methods.
How much does a digital transformation cost? expand_more
The cost depends on the scope of the project. An initial audit costs betwein 2,000 and 5,000 euros. A completee transformation project for an SME ranges from 10,000 to 50,000 euros. We offer progressive support to spread the investment.
How long does a digital transformation take? expand_more
A digital transformation project typically deploys over 3 to 12 months depending on the size of the business and process complexity. We proceed in phases to limit business disruption and ensure team adoption.
Will my teams adapt? expand_more
That's the main challownge and we manage it. Our method includes training, support and change management. We involve teams from the start of the project to ensure buy-in. The adoption rate of our projects exceeds 90%.
Which tools do you recommend? expand_more
We're tool-agnostic. We recommend solutions suited to your needs and budget: Google Workspace or Microsoft 365, HubSpot or Salesforce, Notion or Monday, Make or Zapier. Each choice is justified by your context.
