Frequently asked questions

EVERYTHING YOU WANT TO KNOW ABOUT CONTENT MARKETING

How long does it take to see results from content marketing? expand_more
The first articles start generating organic traffic within 4 to 8 weeks after publication. A complete editorial strategy produces significant results between 3 and 6 months, with a powerful cumulative effect: each new piece of content strengthens overall visibility. Unlike advertising, content marketing is a lasting asset that continues to generate traffic and leads for months or even years.
What is the difference between SEO writing and copywriting? expand_more
SEO writing aims to attract organic traffic from Google by positioning articles on strategic keywords. Copywriting aims to convince and convert the reader into a customer through persuasion techniques. Both are complementary: SEO brings visitors, copywriting turns them into customers. At Pirabel Labs, we master both to maximize your return on investment.
How many articles should I publish per month? expand_more
Quality always primes over quantity. Generally, 4 to 8 strategic articles per month are enough to build a solid organic presence. The important thing is that each article responds to a precise search intent, targets a strategic keyword, and fits into your global strategy. One well-optimized article that ranks on the first page is worth more than 20 generic articles that no one reads.
How do you measure content ROI? expand_more
We track concrete and actionable indicators: organic traffic generated by each piece of content, time on page, bounce rate, conversion rate, number of leads generated, and attributable revenue. You receive a clear monthly report with each content piece's performance and optimization recommendations. No vanity metrics — just numbers that matter for your business.
Do you write for all business sectors? expand_more
Yes. Our writers are trained to adapt to any sector: tech, finance, health, e-commerce, real estate, legal, industrial, education, and many others. We immerse ourselves in your field, study your market, and collaborate with your subject matter experts to guarantee the accuracy and relevance of each content piece.
Do you use AI to write your content? expand_more
AI is a tool in our arsenal, not a substitute for human expertise. We use it to accelerate research, ideation, and structuring. But each content piece is written, edited, and validated by expert human writers. The pen, the style, the nuance, and the strategy remain 100% human. Google values expert and original content — that's exactly what we produce.
What happens if the content doesn't suit me? expand_more
Every content piece goes through a rigorous validation process before publication. You receive the content for proofreading with two revision rounds included in each deliverable. We adjust the tone, style, message, and structure until you are entirely satisfied. Our goal is that every content piece perfectly represents your brand and expertise.
Do you provide an editorial calendar? expand_more
Absolutely — it's even the first thing we build together. Your editorial calendar covers 3 to 12 months depending on your plan and details each planned content piece: subject, target keyword, format, targeted persona, publication date, and conversion goal. No more blank page syndrome or random publications.
