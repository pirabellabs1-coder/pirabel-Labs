Frequently asked questions

EVERYTHING YOU WANT TO KNOW ABOUT EMAIL MARKETING

Why are my emails ending up in spam? expand_more
Several factors cause emails to go to spam: poor DNS configuration (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), uncleaned lists with invalid addresses, overly promotional content, or high complaint rates. Our audit identifies exactly what's blocking and we fix each point to reach 95%+ deliverability.
Which CRM should I choose for my company? expand_more
The choice depends on your size, budget, and needs. For startups, free HubSpot or Brevo are excellent. For SMEs, ActiveCampaign or Pipedrive offer a good balance. For larger companies, Salesforce or HubSpot Pro. We guide you to the best option after an audit of your processes.
What open rate can I expect? expand_more
The average open rate across all sectors is 21%. With our method — fine segmentation, tested subject lines, sending at the right time — our clients regularly reach 35 to 50% open rates. The key is relevance: the right message, to the right person, at the right time.
How long to see results in email marketing? expand_more
The first results are visible from the first optimized campaign, usually in 2 to 4 weeks. Improvement is progressive: after 3 months, you have a segmented base, automated sequences, and stable metrics. Full ROI is generally measured at 6 months.
What exactly is marketing automation? expand_more
Marketing automation consists of automatically sending the right email at the right time, based on your contacts' behavior. For example: a welcome email when someone signs up, a reminder when a cart is abandoned, a nurturing sequence to transform a cold prospect into a hot lead. Everything runs without manual intervention.
Do I need a large contact base to start? expand_more
No. Even with 500 contacts, email marketing is profitable if your list is qualified. Better 500 engaged contacts than 10,000 who never open your emails. We help you build and grow your list with ethical and high-performing acquisition strategies.
Do you manage email creation or just strategy? expand_more
Both. We define the strategy, create templates, write copy, configure automations, and analyze results. You have nothing to manage — we take care of everything, from A to Z. You validate, and we execute.
How do you measure email marketing ROI? expand_more
We track every dollar generated by your campaigns: open rate, click rate, conversions, attributed revenue. With a well-configured CRM, we know exactly how much every email, sequence, and segment brings in. You receive a clear, actionable monthly report.
