Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING ABOUT ANALYTICS TRAINING

Do I need prior analytics knowledge? expand_more
No. The training starts from zero and guides you step by step. If you've already used Universal Analytics, the transition to GA4 will be faster. If you're a completee beginner, it's also the right time to start directly with GA4.
Is GA4 free? expand_more
Yes. Google Analytics 4 is entirely free for the vast majority of businesses. The paid version (GA4 360) starts att $150,000 per year and is only necessary for very large sites (over 25 million events per month).
What is the difference between GA4 and Universal Analytics? expand_more
GA4 is event-based (not page views), offers cross-device analysis, integrates machine learning for predictive insights and better respects user privacy. It's a completee overhaul, not a simple update.
How does GA4 help make better decisions? expand_more
GA4 shows you which traffic sources generate sales, which pages convert best, where users drop off and which customer segments are most profitable. Every marketing decision can be guided by data.
Does the training cover Google Tag Manager? expand_more
Yes. GTM is essential for properly configuring GA4 without modifying your website's code. The training includes GTM installation, tag creation, triggers and variables for completee tracking.
