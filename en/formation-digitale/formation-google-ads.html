Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING ABOUT GOOGLE ADS TRAINING

Do I need an advertising budget for the training? expand_more
We recommend a small test budget (50 to 100 euros) to practice in real conditions. However, the training also uses demo accounts and case studies to learn without spending. The key is to understand the method.
Does the training prepare for Google certification? expand_more
Yes. Our program covers all Google Ads certification modules. At the end of the training, you'll be ready to take and pass the Google Ads certification exams (Search, Display, Shopping, Video).
How much budget do I need to start with Google Ads? expand_more
A budget of 300 to 500 euros per month is enough to start. The key is to target precisely and optimize continuously. With the right training, a small budget can generate excellent ROI in low-competition niches.
Does Google Ads work for all industries? expand_more
Google Ads works for virtually all industries: B2B, B2C, e-commerce, services, SaaS, local... The key is finding the right keywords with sufficient volume and a profitable cost per click. The training teaches you to evaluate your market's potential.
What is the difference between Google Ads and Meta Ads? expand_more
Google Ads captures existing demand (people actively search). Meta Ads creates demand (you interrupt the scroll). Both are complementary. Google Ads is often more effective for high purchase-intent searches.
