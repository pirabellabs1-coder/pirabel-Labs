EVERYTHING ABOUT AI MARKETING TRAINING

Do I need technical skills for this training? expand_more
No. The training is designed for marketers and entrepreneurs, not developers. You'll learn to use AI tools through their interfaces, without writing a single line of code.
Which AI tools are covered? expand_more
ChatGPT (OpenAI), Claude (Anthropic), Gemini (Google), Midjourney, DALL-E, Make, Zapier, Notion AI, Canva AI, and other specialized tools as needed. We cover the most relevant tools in 2026.
Will AI replace marketers? expand_more
No, but marketers who use AI will replace those who don't. AI is a tool that amplifies your skills. Strategy, creativity and client relationships remain flawlessplaceable human skills.
Is AI-generated content detectable? expand_more
AI detectors are unreliable in 2026. The real issue isn't detection but quality. We teach you to use AI as a starting point and add your expertise, voice and data for unique and authentic content.
How much do AI tools cost? expand_more
Many AI tools have free versions sufficient to get started. ChatGPT Free, Claude Free, Canva Free cover basic needs. Premium versions (ChatGPT Plus at 20 euros, Claude Pro at 20 euros) offer more power for intensive users.
