Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING ABOUT SOCIAL MEDIA TRAINING

Do I need to be on every social network? expand_more
No. We recommend mastering 2 to 3 platforms maximum. LinkedIn for B2B, Instagram for visual B2C, TikTok to reach a young audience. The training covers all platforms but helps you choose the most relevant ones.
Does the training include content creation? expand_more
Yes. You'll learn to use Canva, CapCut and AI tools to create professional content without being a graphic designer or videographer. We provide editable templates to get started immediately.
How long to see results? expand_more
Initial results (engagement, followers) are visible within the first 2 weeks of implementation. Lead generation through social media takes 1 to 3 months depending on your industry and posting consistency.
Does the training cover social advertising? expand_more
The training focuses on organic content and community management. For paid advertising (Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads), we offer a dedicated course. However, the basics of social advertising are covered.
Do I need specific equipment? expand_more
No. A recent smartphone is enough to create quality content. We show you how to film, edit and publish with your phone. Recommended tools are free or low-cost.
