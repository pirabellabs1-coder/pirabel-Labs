Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING ABOUT WORDPRESS TRAINING

Do I need to know how to code to use WordPress? expand_more
No. WordPress is designed for non-technical users. With a page builder like Elementor, you create pages by drag-and-drop without writing a line of code. Our training covers everything in visual mode, with no code.
Is WordPress free? expand_more
WordPress software is free. You'll need hosting (5 to 20 euros per month) and a domain name (10 to 15 euros per year). Some premium themes and plugins are paid but free alternatives are often sufficient.
WordPress or Wix/Squarespace? expand_more
WordPress offers much more flexibility, features and control. You own your website and your data. Wix and Squarespace are simpler to get started but more limiting in the long run. For professional use, WordPress is superior.
Can I create an online store with WordPress? expand_more
Yes, with the WooCommerce plugin (free). Our training includes a dedicated e-commerce module: store creation, product management, payment and shipping configuration. WooCommerce powers 28% of global online stores.
How do I secure my WordPress site? expand_more
The training covers WordPress security in detail: regular updates, security plugins (Wordfence, Sucuri), automatic backups, SSL certificate, two-factor authentication and best practices to prevent hacking.
