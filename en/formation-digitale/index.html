Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING YOU WANT TO KNOW ABOUT OUR TRAINING

Are your training courses adapted to beginners? expand_more
Yes, absolutely. Each training course is built with progressive levels. Whether you're a complete beginner or already have a solid foundation, we adapt the content and pace to your level. A placement test is conducted before each training to ensure the program matches your needs exactly.
Are the courses online or in-person? expand_more
Both formats are available. We offer training via video conference, in person at our offices in Paris, Cotonou and Casablanca, or directly at your premises. The format is adapted to your geographical constraints, team size and learning preferences.
Do you deliver a certification after the training? expand_more
Yes. Each participant receives a Pirabel Labs completion certificate attesting to acquired skills. This certificate details the modules completed, training hours and validated skills. It is delivered after validation of a practical exercise on your own project.
How long does a training course last? expand_more
Duration varies depending on the subject and level. Short courses last 1 to 2 days for targeted topics. Complete programs span 3 to 5 days. Custom programs can last several weeks with spaced sessions to encourage practice between modules.
Is there follow-up after the training? expand_more
Yes, it's an essential part of our approach. Each training includes 30-day post-training follow-up during which you can ask your questions by email or during a video conference follow-up session. We want you to be truly self-sufficient in the long run, not just trained for a day.
Can you train my entire team? expand_more
Absolutely. We offer team packs that can accommodate up to 8 participants per session. The program is adapted to your collective objectives and industry. Training can be done at your premises or via video conference. Each participant receives their individual certification.
