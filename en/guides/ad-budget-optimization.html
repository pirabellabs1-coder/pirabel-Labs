Optimizing Your Ad Budget.

payments ROI Strategist timer 9 min read

In a world of rising customer acquisition costs (CAC), simply spending more is not a strategy. True success comes from strategic allocation and continuous optimization based on real-world performance data.

1. The 70/20/10 Rule

Allocate 70% of your budget to proven channels, 20% to scaling emerging winners, and 10% to testing radical new ideas. This balance ensures stability while allowing for future growth breakthroughs.

2. Attribution and ROI Tracking

You cannot optimize what you cannot measure. Implement robust cross-channel attribution to understand the true impact of every dollar spent, moving beyond simple 'last-click' models.

3. Seasonal & Dynamic Scaling

Your budget shouldn't be static. Use AI-driven automation to ramp up spend during peak conversion hours and pull back when your target audience is idle, ensuring every cent is used at its maximum potential.
