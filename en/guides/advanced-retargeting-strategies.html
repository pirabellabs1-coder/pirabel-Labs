Advanced Retargeting: Closing the Sale

Performance Guide - April 2026.

Most prospects don't buy on their first visit. Advanced retargeting allows you to stay top-of-mind and provide the specific information or incentive they need to return and convert.

1. Dynamic Product Retargeting

Show your visitors the exact products they viewed on your site. This level of relevance significantly increases click-through rates and conversion compared to generic brand ads.

2. Sequential Storytelling

Don't show the same ad over and over. Use a sequence – start with a brand reminder, then move to customer testimonials, then conclude with a time-limited offer to create a sense of urgency.
