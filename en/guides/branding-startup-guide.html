Branding for Startups: Building for Scale

Startup Strategy - April 2026.

Your brand is not just a logo. It's the sum of every interaction someone has with your company. For a startup, a strong brand is the difference between being ignored and being remembered.

1. Define Your Positioning

Before designing anything, you must know who you are and who you are for. What unique problem do you solve? Why should customers care about you more than your competitors?

2. Visual Identity vs. Brand Voice

Consistency is key. Your visual identity (colors, fonts) must match your brand voice (the way you speak to your audience). If your visual is professional but your voice is casual, you create cognitive dissonance.
