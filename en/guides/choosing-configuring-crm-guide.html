Choosing and Configuring Your CRM

Operations Guide - April 2026.

A CRM (Customer Relationship Management) is the beating heart of your sales machine. But many businesses fail because they choose the wrong tool or fail to configure it to match their sales process.

1. Map Your Sales Process First

Before looking at software, document your pipeline. How do leads enter? What are the specific stages they pass through? A CRM should adapt to your business, not the other way around.

2. Data Integrity and Automation

A CRM is only as good as the data inside it. Automate as much data entry as possible to ensure your sales team spends their time selling, not filling out forms.
