A/B Testing: The Scientific Way to Grow

Optimization Guide - April 2026.

A/B testing is the process of comparing two versions of a webpage or app against each other to determine which one performs better. In 2026, it's the only way to avoid 'opinion-based' design and focus on what actually works.

1. One Variable at a Time

The secret of a successful A/B test is isolation. If you change the headline, the button color, and the image all at once, you won't know which change caused the impact on performance.

2. Statistical Significance

Don't stop your test too early. You need a sufficient sample size and a high confidence level (usually 95%) to ensure that the results you're seeing are not just due to random chance.
