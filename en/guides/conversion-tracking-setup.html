Conversion Tracking.

insights ROI Expert timer 10 min read

If you don't know exactly where your sales are coming from, you are not marketing; you are gambling. In 2026, multi-touch conversion tracking is the only way to scale with confidence.

1. Defining Micro & Macro Conversions

Your customer journey is complex. We help you track not only final purchases but every small step along the way—from email signups to add-to-carts—allowing the algorithms to optimize for the entire funnel, not just the finish line.

2. UTM Tagging & Naming Conventions

Consistency is the key to clear data. We implement a standardized UTM tagging system across all your channels, ensuring that every click from LinkedIn, Google, or Email is correctly attributed in your analytics dashboard.

3. Attribution Models in 2026

Stop relying on last-click attribution. We help you understand data-driven attribution models that give credit to every touchpoint, from the first discovery ad to the final conversion email, providing a true picture of your marketing ROI.
