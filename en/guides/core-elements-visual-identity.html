Essential Elements of a Brand Style Guide

Branding Guide - April 2026.

A style guide is the 'bible' of your brand. It ensures that every piece of communication, whether internal or external, remains consistent and aligned with your brand's unique identity.

1. Logo Usage and Spacing

Your logo is your signature. Define clear rules for minimum sizes, exclusion zones, and prohibited versions to maintain its integrity across all media and platforms.

2. Core and Secondary Palettes

Don't just provide colors; provide emotions. Define your primary brand colors for high-impact use and a secondary palette for supporting elements and digital backgrounds.
