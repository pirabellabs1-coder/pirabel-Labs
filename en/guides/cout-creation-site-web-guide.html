1. The factors that influence the price

The cost d'un website varie enormously, of a few hundred a several tens of thousand euros. This amplitude s'explained par the diversity projects ands providers. Avant of demander quote, you need to understand the factors that influence the price final.

The complexity fonctionnelle is the first factor. A showcase site of five pages costs infinitely less that a platform e-commerce with management of stocks, paiement online and client portal. The level of customisation design plays also : a template adaptsd costs less expensive that a design created from zero. Finally, the type of provider (freelancer, local agency, premium agency) impacts directly the daily rate and therefore the budget total.

What drives up the bill

  • Design custom : A design uniquely created by an art director artistique costs more that a template.
  • Features complexes : E-commerce, member area, online booking, integrations API.
  • Volume of content : The more you have of pages, more the work integration is significant.
  • Multilingual : A site additional languages multipliess the volume of work.
  • Advanced SEO : The optimisation technical and editorial advanced represente a investment additional.

2. Fourchettes by price by type of site

Here is the ranges by price observed in 2026 for differents types of websites, en using a professional (freelancer or agency).

Showcase site (5 a 10 pages)

A showcase site professional costs between 1 500 and 8 000 euros. The range low correspond a a site on template Webflow or WordPress with customisations lightweight. The range high includes a design custom, animations, a optimisation SEO and a writing of content professionalle. It is the type of site the more courant for the TPE, PME and professions liberales.

Site e-commerce

An functional e-commerce starts at 3 000 euros for a simple store on Shopify or WooCommerce, and can exceed 30 000 euros for a platform customised with advanced management inventory, loyalty program and integrations logistics. The budget moyen for a e-commerce PME is betwein 5 000 and 15 000 euros.

Custom site or application web

The projects custom (SaaS, platform of matchmaking, application web) start rarely below of 15 000 euros and can reach 100 000 euros or more according to the complexity. These projects require a team of development and a specification sheet detailed.

3. The items of expenses detaileds

The price d'un website itself decompose additional postes that he is important of connaitre for understand your quote and negocier en connaissance of cause.

Conception andsign

The phase of conception includes the etude of your market, the definition of the arborescence, the creation maquettes (wireframes) and the design graphique. Comptez 20 a 30 % of the budget total. A good design is a investment, not a expense : he conditionne the first impression and the conversion rate of your website.

Development and integration

The integration (transformation maquettes en pages fonctionnelles) represente 30 a 50 % of the budget. Ce poste includes the codage, the configuration of the CMS, the implementation features and the tests. The cost depend directly of the complexity technical of the projet.

Contenu and SEO

The writing of content professional and the optimisation SEO are often sous-estimees in the budgets. Expect 15 a 25 % of the budget for this poste. A site with a design magnifique but a content mediocre ne generatea no result. Invest in a copywriter professional and a strategy of keywords solide.

4. The costs caches a anticiper

The price of creation n'est that the part emergee of the iceberg. De numerous couts recurring are a prevoir for maintain your website en good etat of fonctionnement.

Hosting and domain name

The hosting costs betwein 50 and 500 euros per year according to the level of performance desired. The nom name costs between 10 and 30 euros per year. Si you are on Webflow, the hosting is included in the subscription.

Maintenance and updates a jour

A site WordPress requires regular regular maintenance : updates of the CMS, plugins, backups, patches of security. Expect 500 a 2 000 euros per year for a maintenance contract. Without maintenance, your website will become vulnerable to the security flaws and will lose performance.

5. How optimize your budget

Optimising your budget ne means not choisir the provider the least cher. Cela means invest intelligently based on of your priorities and your expected return on investment.

Prioritise the pages a high-impact : Focus your budget on the pages that generate the most conversions (homepage, service pages, contact page). Secondary pages can be simplified.

Start simple, iterate then : Launch a site fonctionnel with the features essential, then gradually enrich it based on user feedback and your results. It is more effective and less risky than wanting to integrate everything at the start.

Invest in the content : Content is what that attracts and converts your visitors. A site to the design modest but to the excellent content will outperform always a beautiful site with a empty content.

The cost d'un website is not an expense, it is a investment. Evaluate it based on of the return on investment that it can generate, not only based on of the price listed.
