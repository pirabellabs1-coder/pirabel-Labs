1. The factors that influencent the price

The cout d'un website varie enormement, of a few centaines a several dizaines of milliers d'euros. This amplitude s'explique par the diversite projets ands prestataires. Avant of demander devis, you need to understand the factors that influencent the price final.

The complexite fonctionnelle is the first factor. A site vitrine of five pages coute infiniment less that a plateform e-commerce with gestion of stocks, paiement online and espace client. The level of personnalisation design plays egalement : a template adaptsd coute less cher that a design cree of zero. Enfin, the type of prestataire (frealance, agency locale, premium agency) impacte directly the tarif journalier and therefore the budget total.

Ce that does grimper the facture

  • Design custom : A design unique cree par a directeur artistique coute more that a template.
  • Fonctionnalites complexes : E-commerce, espace membre, reservation online, integrations API.
  • Volume of content : Plus you avez of pages, more the travail d'integration is consequent.
  • Multilangue : A site additionallyieurs langues multiplie the volume of travail.
  • SEO advanced : The optimisation technical and editoriale poussee represente a investissement supplementaire.

2. Fourchettes of price par type of site

Here is the fourchettes of price constatees en 2026 for differents types of websites, en faisant appel a a professional (frealance or agence).

Site vitrine (5 a 10 pages)

A site vitrine professional coute between 1 500 and 8 000 euros. The fourchette low correspond a a site on template Webflow or WordPress with personnalisations legeres. The fourchette high includes a design custom, animations, a optimisation SEO and a writing of content professionalle. It is the type of site the more courant for the TPE, PME and professions liberales.

Site e-commerce

A e-commerce fonctionnel demarre a 3 000 euros for a boutique simple on Shopify or WooCommerce, and can depasser 30 000 euros for a plateform personalized with gestion advanced stocks, programme of fidelite and integrations logistiques. The budget moyen for a e-commerce PME itself situe between 5 000 and 15 000 euros.

Site custom where application web

The projets custom (SaaS, plateform of mise en relation, application web) demarrent rarement en dessous of 15 000 euros and can achieve 100 000 euros or more according to the complexite. These projets requiresnt a team of development and a cahier charges detaille.

3. The postes of expenses detaileds

The price d'un website itself decompose additionallyieurs postes that he is important of connaitre for understand your devis and negocier en connaissance of cause.

Conception andsign

The phase of conception includes the etude of your market, the definition of the arborescence, the creation maquettes (wireframes) and the design graphique. Comptez 20 a 30 % of the budget total. A good design is a investissement, not a expense : he conditionne the first impression and the conversion rate of your website.

Development and integration

The integration (transformation maquettes en pages fonctionnelles) represente 30 a 50 % of the budget. Ce poste includes the codage, the configuration of the CMS, the implementation fonctionnalites and the tests. The cout depend directly of the complexite technical of the projet.

Contenu and SEO

The writing of content professional and the optimisation SEO are often sous-estimees in the budgets. Plan 15 a 25 % of the budget for this poste. A site with a design magnifique but a content mediocre ne generatea no result. Investissez in a copywriter professional and a strategy of keywords solide.

4. The couts caches a anticiper

The price of creation n'est that the part emergee of the iceberg. De numberux couts recurring are a prevoir for maintain your website en good etat of fonctionnement.

Hebergement and nom of field

The hebergement coute between 50 and 500 euros per year according to the level of performance souhaite. The nom of field coute between 10 and 30 euros per year. Si you are on Webflow, the hebergement is inclus in the abonnement.

Maintenance and mises a jour

A site WordPress requires a maintenance regulare : mises a jour of the CMS, plugins, sauvegardes, correctifs of securite. Plan 500 a 2 000 euros per year for a contrat of maintenance. Without maintenance, your website deviendra vulnerable to the failles of security and perdra en performance.

5. How optimize your budget

Optimize son budget ne signifie not choisir the prestataire the least cher. Cela signifie invest intelligemment en fonction of your prioritys and your return on investment attendu.

Priorisez the pages a strong impact : Concentrez your budget on the pages that generate the more conversion rates (homepage, pages services, page contact). The pages secondaires can be simplifiees.

Start simple, iterez ensuite : Lancez a site fonctionnel with the fonctionnalites essentialles, then enrichissez-le gradually en fonction retours users and your results. It is more effective and less risque that of vouloir all integrate the depart.

Investissez in the content : The content is this that attire and convertit your visitors. A site to the design modeste but to the content excellent surpassera always a site magnifique with a content creux.

The cout d'un website n'est not a expense, it is a investissement. Evaluez-le en fonction of the return on investment that he can generate, not uniquement en fonction of the price affiche.
