Surgical Targeting.

person_search FB Ads Expert timer 11 min read

Targeting on Facebook (Meta) has changed. In 2026, the algorithm wants you to trust its AI. We help you move from micro-management to macro-influence through strategic audience signals.

1. The Power of Broad Targeting

By removing interest and demographic filters, you allow the algorithm to search its entire user base for your buyers. With the right creative, Broad targeting often leads to the lowest CPA and highest scalability.

2. Lookalike Audiences in 2026

Lookalikes are still powerful if fed with high-quality seed data. We show you how to use your best customers (those with high LTV) to find 'twins' across the Meta ecosystem who are most likely to convert.

3. Retargeting Arc & Frequency

Don't annoy your prospects. We help you build retargeting arcs that show different messages based on where they are in the funnel, ensuring high relevance and preventing ad fatigue.
