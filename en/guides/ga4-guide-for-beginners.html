Mastering GA4: The Beginners Guide

Data Guide - April 2026.

Google Analytics 4 (GA4) represents a paradigm shift in how we understand user behavior. Moving from a session-based model to an event-based model, it provides much deeper insights into the customer journey.

1. Everything is an Event

In GA4, every interaction – a page view, a click, a scroll, or a purchase – is recorded as an event. This allows for unified tracking across websites and mobile applications.

2. Predictive Insights

Leverage Google's machine learning models to predict future user behavior, such as churn probability or purchase likelihood, helping you optimize your marketing spend in real-time.
