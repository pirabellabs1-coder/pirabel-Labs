Mastering GA4 Insight.

analytics Analytics Expert timer 12 min read

GA4 is not just an update of Universal Analytics; it's a completely different logic based on events. Understanding it is the difference between guessing and knowing your ROI.

1. Event-Based Paradigm

Everything is an event. We help you move from tracking "pageviews" to tracking "user interactions". Every scroll, click, and video play is a data point that helps refine your marketing strategy.

2. Predictive Audiences

GA4 uses machine learning to predict user behavior. We show you how to leverage these predictive audiences to target users who are most likely to convert in the next 7 days.

3. Exploration & Custom Reports

Forget the standard reports. The real power of GA4 lies in the Exploration module. We build custom funnels and path explorations to understand exactly where you're losing customers and why.
