Google Ads Guide for Beginners: Master SEA

Updated: April 2026

Google Ads is the must-have tool for any business looking for immediate results. Unlike SEO, which takes time, SEA (Search Engine Advertising) allows you to be visible at the top of results in just a few clicks.

1. Setting Up Your Campaigns

A good campaign starts with clear goals: Lead generation, online sales, or brand awareness? Define your budget and your target carefully.

2. Keyword Selection

Don't just target the most popular terms. Focus on high-intent keywords that are more likely to convert into actual sales.
