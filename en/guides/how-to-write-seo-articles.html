How to Write SEO Articles that Rank

Content Guide - April 2026.

Writing for SEO is a balance between satisfying search algorithms and providing value to human readers. If you only write for robots, you'll have a high bounce rate. If you only write for humans, you might never be found.

1. Semantic Keyword Research

Go beyond a single keyword. Research related terms and questions that your audience is asking. Use these naturally throughout your H2 and H3 headings.

2. The Power of Structure

Use clear, descriptive headings. Keep your paragraphs short (3-4 sentences max). Use bullet points and bold text to make your content skimmable.
