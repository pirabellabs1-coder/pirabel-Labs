Boosting Your Email Open Rates in 2026

Updated: April 2026.

The subject line is the most important part of your email. If it's not opened, the content inside – no matter how good it is – doesn't exist for your subscriber.

1. Curiositiy and Personalization

Use curiosity-driven subject lines that make it impossible not to click. Personalization should go beyond just the first name; reference the subscriber's interests or recent behavior for maximum effect.

2. Sending Time and Frequency

There's no single 'best' time to send. Test different days and hours based on your specific audience's lifestyle. Frequency should be consistent enough to stay top-of-mind without becoming a nuisance.
