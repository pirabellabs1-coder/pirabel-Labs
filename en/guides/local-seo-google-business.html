How to Dominate Google Maps

Local strategy guide: April 2026.

If you have a physical location or serve a specific area, Local SEO is your most powerful tool to attract ready-to-buy customers.

1. Optimize Your Google Business Profile

Your profile is your digital storefront. Ensure your Name, Address, and Phone number (NAP) are consistent across the web. Add high-quality photos and respond to every review.

2. Local Citations

Being mentioned in local directories and news sites builds authority for your business in your specific city or region.
