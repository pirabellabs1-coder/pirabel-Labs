High-Conversion Automation Sequences

Growth Guide - April 2026.

Automation is not a 'set and forget' strategy. The best sequences are those that deliver the right message to the right person at the right moment based on their actual behavior.

1. The Indoctrination Sequence

When someone joins your list, you have their maximum attention. Use a 3-5 email sequence to introduce your brand values, solve a small problem, and set expectations for the future.

2. Abandoned Cart Recovery

Don't just end with a reminder. Use a sequence that addresses common objections (price, trust, shipping time) and offers a small, time-limited incentive to complete the purchase.
