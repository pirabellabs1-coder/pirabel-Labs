Advanced Retargeting.

refresh Re-engagement Expert timer 8 min read

98% of people don't buy on their first visit. Retargeting is your second chance to make a first impression. In 2026, it's about sequence, not just repetition.

1. The Power of Sequencing

Don't show the same ad for 30 days. Start with a brand reminder, then social proof, then an FAQ, and finally a limited-time offer. We help you build these conversion arcs.

2. Cross-Channel Synchronization

Your customer is on YouTube, LinkedIn, and Instagram. Your retargeting must follow them intelligently across these platforms to stay top-of-mind without being intrusive.

3. Dynamic Product Ads (DPA)

Show them exactly what they were looking at, but with a better angle. Use AI to generate personalized backgrounds or copies that address the specific hesitation they had on the page.
