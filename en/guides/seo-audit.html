The SEO Diagnostics.

fact_check Tech Auditor timer 13 min read

Acquiring traffic is impossible if your website's foundation is broken. In 2026, an SEO audit is a deep diagnostic that identifies the technical, semantic, and structural barriers between your brand and the top of search results.

1. Technical Crawl & Indexability

Can search engines actually see you? We help you audit your robots.txt, sitemaps, and crawl errors, ensuring that every high-value page on your site is indexed and optimized for search discovery.

2. Core Web Vitals & Mobile UX

Performance is a primary ranking factor. We audit your site's speed and responsiveness across all devices, identifying the specific technical optimizations necessary to achieve a perfect Core Web Vitals score.

3. Semantic Gap & Authority Analysis

Are you talking about the right things? We analyze your content against your competitors and user intent, identifying the 'Semantic Gaps' where you can create high-authority content to dominate your niche.
