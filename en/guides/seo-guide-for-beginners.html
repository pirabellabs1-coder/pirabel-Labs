The Complete SEO Guide for Beginners

Updated: April 2026 • 12 min read

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is often seen as a dark art, but it's actually a logical process of making your website more understandable and valuable for search engines like Google.

1. What is SEO?

SEO is the practice of increasing the quantity and quality of traffic to your website through organic search engine results. Unlike paid ads, organic traffic is free and sustainable over the long term.

2. How Google Works

Google has three main steps to generate results from web pages: crawling, indexing, and serving results. Our goal is to ensure Google finds your pages easily and understands their relevance to specific user queries.
