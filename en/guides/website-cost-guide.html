The Real Cost of a Website in 2026

Guide - April 2026.

One of the most common questions we get is: 'How much for a website?' The answer depends on your goals, the complexity, and the level of customization required.

1. Showcase Website vs. E-commerce

A simple showcase site (5-10 pages) built to represent your brand will obviously cost less than a fully integrated e-commerce platform with dozens of custom features.

2. What You're Really Paying For

You're not just paying for code. You're paying for strategy, UI/UX design, SEO optimization, and a conversion-focused engine that brings in revenue.
