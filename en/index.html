FREQUENTLY
ASKED QUESTIONS

How long before we see results from your digital marketing services? expand_more
The first gains are often visible within 30 days for technical optimizations and paid advertising campaigns. For a solid first-page ranking on Google through organic search, expect 3 to 6 months depending on the competition in your industry, the maturity of your domain authority and your existing content footprint. Email marketing and social media strategies typically show measurable engagement within 60 to 90 days. Conversion rate optimization can deliver double-digit lifts within the first quarter of testing.
Do you work with all types of businesses regardless of size or industry? expand_more
Yes. Whether you are a venture-backed startup, an established small or medium enterprise, a fast-growing scale-up or a multinational corporation, we adapt our approach to match your stage, budget and ambitions. We currently serve clients in Paris, Cotonou, Casablanca, Dakar, Abidjan, Brussels, Montreal and many other cities across Europe, Africa and North America. Our verticals span e-commerce, SaaS, real estate, healthcare, hospitality, education, finance, legal services and B2B industrial businesses.
What is the difference between SEO and paid advertising for long-term growth? expand_more
Paid advertising brings you immediate qualified traffic as long as you maintain your budget — the moment you stop spending, the leads stop. Search engine optimization builds a lasting digital asset: once your website ranks well organically, it continues attracting qualified visitors for free for months or even years. The smartest growth strategy combines both channels: paid ads to capture demand today while SEO compounds your authority for tomorrow. We typically recommend a balanced budget allocation between organic search investments and paid acquisition campaigns, optimized continuously based on your customer acquisition cost and lifetime value metrics.
What does your free strategic audit actually include? expand_more
Our complimentary audit includes a complete analysis of your current digital visibility, a technical SEO diagnostic covering Core Web Vitals, mobile usability, structured data and crawlability, a competitor benchmarking study identifying market gaps, a content quality assessment, a backlink profile review and concrete prioritized recommendations. You receive a clear written document with action items ranked by expected impact and implementation difficulty, plus a 60-minute strategy call with one of our senior consultants to discuss findings and build your custom roadmap.
Why should we choose Pirabel Labs over other digital marketing agencies? expand_more
Three reasons set us apart. First, our results are contractual: we commit to measurable objectives and we track them transparently with monthly reporting in plain language. Second, our team combines senior strategic expertise with hands-on execution capability across SEO, web development, AI automation, paid advertising, content marketing, branding and CRO — no fragmented vendor management. Third, we work with a select portfolio of clients to guarantee dedicated attention. Our clients consistently report exceeding their digital growth targets within the first 12 months of partnership.
Can you take over an existing website and improve it without rebuilding from scratch? expand_more
Absolutely. Most of our engagements start with an existing website that needs to perform better. We conduct a full technical audit, identify the highest-impact improvements, preserve your existing SEO equity and authority signals, then progressively optimize page speed, content quality, conversion paths, internal linking architecture and technical infrastructure. Complete redesigns are only recommended when fundamental architecture limitations or branding pivots make incremental improvements economically inefficient. We always protect your search rankings during any migration.
How transparent is your reporting and what metrics do you track? expand_more
Total transparency is non-negotiable. You receive a custom Looker Studio dashboard accessible 24/7 with live data on every relevant metric: organic traffic growth, keyword rankings, conversion rates, customer acquisition cost, return on ad spend, lead volume and quality scoring, content performance and channel attribution. Monthly reporting calls walk you through wins, challenges and the next sprint priorities. Annual strategic reviews recalibrate goals based on business evolution. No vanity metrics, no buried data — only what drives revenue.
Do you offer ongoing maintenance after the initial project is complete? expand_more
Yes. Most clients enter long-term partnerships with monthly retainers covering continuous SEO optimization, content production, paid campaign management, conversion rate testing, technical maintenance, security monitoring and strategic consulting. We also offer dedicated project work for specific initiatives like website launches, rebrandings, marketing automation implementations or AI agent deployments. All retainer agreements include flexible scope adjustments quarterly to match evolving business priorities and market conditions.
What budgets typically work best with your agency model? expand_more
Our retainer engagements start at 800 euros per month for the Foundation plan suitable for small businesses building their digital presence. Most growth-stage companies invest between 1500 and 5000 euros monthly across our Growth plan and custom packages combining multiple services. Enterprise clients with complex multi-market needs typically operate in the 5000 to 25000 euros monthly range. We also handle one-time projects like website builds starting at 4000 euros and brand identity packages from 3500 euros. Every proposal is tailored to expected return on investment.
How do you stay ahead of search engine algorithm updates and AI changes? expand_more
Our team dedicates structured weekly time to monitoring algorithm changes from Google, Bing and emerging generative search engines. We follow EEAT guidelines, helpful content updates, core algorithm rollouts and AI overview developments closely. Our agency invests heavily in research and development around generative engine optimization, retrieval augmented generation systems and conversational AI integration. Clients benefit from this forward-looking approach: while many agencies still chase yesterday's tactics, our strategies anticipate tomorrow's search behavior and emerging digital marketing channels.
