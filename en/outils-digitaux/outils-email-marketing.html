THE RIGHT TOOL MAKES ALL THE DIFFERENCE

Deliverability, automation, pricing... The wrong tool choice can ruin your email marketing.

mark_email_unread

Emails in spam

Deliverability varies enormously from one tool to another. Some platforms have a 95% deliverability rate while others drop to 70%. If your emails don't reach the inbox, everything else is useless.

price_change

Prices that explode

Mailchimp is free up to 500 contacts, then prices climb fast. At 10,000 contacts, the bill can range from 25 euros (Brevo) to 150 euros (Mailchimp). Choosing the right tool can save you thousands of euros per year.

settings_suggest

Limited automation

Basic automation (welcome email) is available everywhere. But advanced workflows (lead scoring, conditional sequences, A/B testing) aren't available on all tools. Choosing the tool adapted to your automation level is crucial.

Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING ABOUT EMAIL MARKETING TOOLS

What is the best email marketing tool in 2026? expand_more
For French SMEs: Brevo (best value for money, servers in France, native GDPR). For advanced automation: ActiveCampaign. For creators: ConvertKit. For e-commerce: Klaviyo. For a complete ecosystem: HubSpot.
Brevo or Mailchimp? expand_more
Brevo is cheaper, based in France (GDPR), billing per email sent (not per contact). Mailchimp is more well-known, with more templates and integrations. For the French-speaking market, Brevo is often the best choice.
Do I need to pay for email marketing? expand_more
Free versions (Brevo: 300 emails/day, Mailchimp: 500 contacts) are enough to get started. From 1,000 contacts or if you need automation, a paid plan becomes necessary. Prices start at 7 euros per month.
Which has the best deliverability? expand_more
ActiveCampaign and Brevo regularly top deliverability rankings. Mailchimp has seen declines in recent years due to the volume of spam accounts on its platform. Deliverability also depends on your sending practices.
Can I easily migrate from one tool to another? expand_more
Yes, most tools allow importing contacts via CSV. Automations will need to be recreated. We recommend choosing wisely from the start to avoid time-consuming migrations. If you need to migrate, we can support you.
