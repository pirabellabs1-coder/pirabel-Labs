Frequently Asked Questions

EVERYTHING ABOUT SEO TOOLS

What is the best SEO tool in 2026? expand_more
There is no universal answer. For complete usage: Semrush. For backlinks: Ahrefs. For technical audits: Screaming Frog. For a tight budget: SE Ranking or Ubersuggest. For beginners: Google Search Console (free) + Ubersuggest.
Do I need a paid SEO tool? expand_more
Not necessarily. Google Search Console + Screaming Frog (free version up to 500 URLs) cover basic needs. A paid tool becomes necessary when you want to do advanced keyword research, position tracking or competitive analysis.
Semrush or Ahrefs? expand_more
Semrush is more complete (SEO + Ads + Social + Content). Ahrefs excels at backlink analysis and keyword research. If you only do SEO, Ahrefs. If you also manage Ads and content, Semrush. Both are excellent.
Is Google Search Console enough? expand_more
For the basics, yes. GSC gives you your real positions on Google, your impressions, your clickks and technical errors. But it doesn't allow competitive analysis, advanced keyword research or backlink auditing. It's a complement, not a replacement.
How many SEO tools do I need? expand_more
Ideally 2 to 3: Google Search Console (free and mandatory), a keyword research and analysis tool (Semrush or Ahrefs), and Screaming Frog for technical audits. Beginners can start with GSC alone and add tools gradually.
