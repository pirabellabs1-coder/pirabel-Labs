Frequently asked questions

EVERYTHING YOU WANT TO KNOW ABOUT PAID ADVERTISING

What minimum budget is needed to launch advertising campaigns? expand_more
We recommend a minimum advertising budget of 1,000 euros per month to obtain significant data and optimize your campaigns effectively. Below this threshold, algorithms don't have enough data volume to learn and optimize bids. That said, we always adapt our approach to your budget: even with 1,000 euros, we can generate concrete results by focusing on a single platform and very precise targeting.
How long does it take to see results with paid advertising? expand_more
Unlike SEO, the first results are almost immediate. The first conversions generally arrive within 48 to 72 hours after launch. The real optimization of ROAS takes 2 to 4 weeks of data collection. From the second month, campaigns reach their cruising speed and performance improves continuously through daily optimization.
Which advertising platform should I choose for my business? expand_more
It depends on your target and your offer. Google Ads is ideal for capturing existing demand — people actively looking for your products or services. Meta Ads excels in demand creation and retargeting. LinkedIn Ads is essential in B2B to target decision-makers. TikTok Ads offers very low costs to reach 18-35 year olds. During our initial audit, we analyze your market to recommend the optimal mix.
How do you measure the ROI of my campaigns? expand_more
We set up complete tracking from the first week: conversion pixel on all platforms, UTMs on every link, Google Analytics 4 configured, and a real-time dashboard. You see exactly how much each euro spent brings you in leads, sales, and revenue. No vanity metrics — we focus on numbers that matter for your business.
Why not manage my campaigns myself? expand_more
You can, but advertising platforms are constantly evolving. A certified expert optimizes your bids, audiences, and creatives daily. Our clients save an average of 45% on their CPA compared to non-specialized internal management. The time you would spend managing your campaigns is better invested in your core business.
Do you also manage the creation of ad visuals and videos? expand_more
Yes. Our creative team produces static visuals, short videos, carousels, and ad copies optimized for each platform. High-performing creatives are the key to profitable campaigns. We continuously test new variants to identify what resonates best with your audience.
What happens if my campaigns are not profitable? expand_more
If after 30 days of optimization your campaigns don't show a positive trajectory, we completely re-evaluate the strategy at no additional cost. Our 98% retention rate proves this almost never happens. We contractually commit to clear and measurable performance goals.
Can I keep control over my advertising accounts? expand_more
Absolutely. Your advertising accounts belong to you 100%. We work on your own Google Ads, Meta Business Manager accounts, etc. You have full access at all times. If you decide to stop our collaboration, you keep everything: accounts, data, history, audiences.
