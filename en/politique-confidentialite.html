PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Pirabel Labs SAS is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This policy explains how we collect, use and protect your personal data when you visit our website or use our services.

2. Data Collected

We collect the following data:

  • Contact data: name, email, phone, company (via our forms)
  • Browsing data: pages visited, visit duration, browser (via analytics cookies)
  • Project data: information provided as part of our services

3. Data Usage

Your data is used to:

  • Respond to your contact and quote requests
  • Provide and improve our services
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Improve the user experience of our website

4. Data Sharing

Your data is never sold or transferred to third parties for commercial purposes. We may share it with technical subcontractors (hosting, analytics) strictly within the scope of our services, and only with partners that comply with the GDPR.

5. Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your data: SSL encryption, restricted access, regular backups and continuous monitoring.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: obtain a copy of your data
  • Right to rectification: correct your inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure: request the deletion of your data
  • Right to object: refuse the processing of your data
  • Right to portability: receive your data in a structured format

To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@pirabellabs.com. We respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies

We use cookies strictly necessary for the operation of the website. Analytics cookies are only activated with your explicit consent. You can manage your preferences at any time.

8. Contact

For any questions regarding this policy, contact our Data Protection Officer:
Email: contact@pirabellabs.com
Address: Pirabel Labs SAS, Paris, France
