Frequently asked questions

EVERYTHING YOU WANT TO KNOW ABOUT SEO

How long does it take to see SEO results? expand_more
Initial technical gains are visible within 30 days: error correction, speed improvement, better indexing. For a solid first-page ranking on Google, expect 3 to 6 months depending on your industry's competition. SEO is a medium-term investment, but the results are sustainable — unlike advertising which stops as soon as you cut the budget.
What is the difference between SEO and Google Ads? expand_more
Google Ads (paid advertising) brings you traffic immediately, but only as long as you pay. SEO builds a sustainable asset: once well-positioned, your site attracts traffic for free for months or even years. The ideal is often to combine the two: Google Ads for quick results while SEO gains momentum.
Is SEO really worth the investment? expand_more
SEO offers the best return on investment of all digital marketing channels. One euro invested in SEO yields an average of 4 to 5 euros in revenue over 12 months. Unlike advertising, SEO results accumulate over time: the longer you invest, the greater the return.
How do I know if my site needs SEO? expand_more
If your site doesn't appear on the first page of Google when searching for your services, you're losing customers every day. If you depend on paid advertising for traffic, if your traffic is stagnating or dropping, you need SEO. Our free audit shows you exactly where you stand and what you can gain.
What does your free SEO audit include? expand_more
Our free audit includes: an analysis of your current visibility on Google, a technical diagnosis of your site (speed, errors, indexing), a study of your main competitors, and concrete recommendations with a prioritized action plan. You receive a clear document, without jargon, with the most impactful actions to take as a priority.
Do you work with businesses of all sizes? expand_more
Yes. Startups, SMEs, and large companies. We adapt our strategy to your budget, market, and specific goals. We work with clients in Paris, Marseille, Lyon, Brussels, Montreal, Cotonou, Casablanca, Dakar, Abidjan, and Tunis.
What guarantees do you offer on results? expand_more
We commit contractually to measurable goals: keyword positions, organic traffic volume, and conversion indicators. If we don't reach the set goals, we continue to work for free until we reach them. It's our way of proving we believe in our method.
How does daily collaboration work? expand_more
You have a dedicated project manager, reachable by email and phone. You receive a clear monthly report with the results, the actions performed, and the plan for the following month. We organize a monthly video meeting to discuss strategy. You have nothing to manage — we take care of everything.
