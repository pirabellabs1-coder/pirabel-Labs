Frequently asked questions

EVERYTHING YOU WANT TO KNOW ABOUT VIDEO

How much does a professional video cost? expand_more
Our rates start at 500 euros per video for a short motion design (30 to 60 seconds). Monthly packs at 1,500 euros per month suit most businesses requiring regular content. For more ambitious projects — full corporate video, shoot, advanced post-production — we establish a custom quote after the creative brief. Every euro invested in video earns you an average of 3 to 5 times more in engagement and conversions.
What is the production lead time for a video? expand_more
Our average lead time is 48 hours for a short motion design. For a full corporate video with shooting, allow 1 to 2 weeks depending on project complexity. We also offer express options for emergencies. Each project follows a precise calendar that we share with you from the brief — you know exactly when you'll receive your video.
What video formats do you produce? expand_more
We produce all the formats you need: square (1:1) for Instagram and Facebook feeds, vertical (9:16) for TikTok, Reels, and Stories, horizontal (16:9) for YouTube and websites, and all intermediate formats. Every delivery systematically includes multi-format versions so you can publish everywhere without extra effort.
Do I need to provide images or content for the video? expand_more
Not necessarily. We can work from your existing assets (logo, photos, brand guidelines) or create the entire visual content from A to Z. For motion design, we create all illustrations and animations. For corporate video, we handle the full shoot. The creative brief follows us to define what you need.
What is the difference between motion design and classic video? expand_more
Motion design uses graphic animation to bring concepts, data, or messages to life in a visual and dynamic way. It's ideal for explaining complex things simply. Classic video films real scenes with people and places. Both approaches are complementary and we often combine them for maximum impact — for example, a filmed interview enriched with motion design to illustrate points.
Are the videos optimized for social media? expand_more
Absolutely. Every video is designed from the start to perform on target platforms. We integrate subtitles (85% of videos are seen without sound), hooks in the first 3 seconds, native platform formats, and optimized thumbnails. We know the algorithms and codes of each network to maximize your views and engagement.
Can a video be modified after delivery? expand_more
Yes. Each project includes 2 rounds of revisions in the base rate (unlimited for Premium plans). We keep the source files for 6 months, allowing you to request adjustments, variations, or subsequent updates at any time. We are flexible — the goal is for you to be 100% satisfied with the final result.
Do you work with international companies? expand_more
Yes. Our production process is 100% digital, allowing us to collaborate effectively with clients worldwide. We have teams in Paris, Cotonou, and Casablanca, and we regularly work with companies in Brussels, Montreal, Dakar, Abidjan, and Tunis. Exchanges are done via video calls, validations by email, and files are shared via secure platforms.
